# Holas: Will SEO Get Back on Its Feet, or Is It the End?
> 

The SEO world is undergoing fundamental changes that began at Google with the [helpful content update](https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2022/08/helpful-content-update) (HCU) in December 2022. The HCU promised to support original, useful content, but reality has proven more complicated. While influencers compete in "guaranteed" advice, the real change is brought by the growing dominance of paid search.

Vitkor Holas thinks that [SEO isn't changing, it's just adapting](https://www.nextvision.cz/blog/seo-na-rozcesti-kam-se-ubira-obor-a-proc-byste-meli-zbystrit/) to the new rules of the digital market, where Google remains the key power player. Although the future of organic traffic looks uncertain, SEO continues to transform and survive thanks to innovation and adaptability.
