# How to Recognize and Solve Design Debt
> 

> "Design debt reduces the creativity of the team, which is forced to constantly patch old errors."\
> — **[AB](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/design-debt-understanding-and-managing-the-long-term-impact-of-design-decisions-88ed5054ea99)**

Design debt arises when creators sacrifice design quality in the interest of quickly completing a project. This debt, although seemingly harmless at first, gradually undermines usability, scalability, and the product's competitive ability.

It is crucial not only to [know how to recognize design debt](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/design-debt-understanding-and-managing-the-long-term-impact-of-design-decisions-88ed5054ea99) (e.g., by inconsistent patterns or user feedback), but also to actively manage it. Regular UX audits, transparency in decision-making, and time for fixes minimize the negative impact. Design should be flexible and prepared for future growth.
