# 10 Steps for a Successful Kickoff Meeting
> 

The project's initial meeting is crucial for unifying the team and clearly setting goals and expectations. The goal of the kick-off meeting is to present the project scope, define roles and responsibilities, and foster team spirit.

There are various types of these meetings, including internal and external gatherings or agile kickoffs that account for project specifics. The agenda typically includes team introductions, a project overview, and time for Q&A. The Figma web offers [a ten-step checklist for the initial project meeting](https://www.figma.com/resource-library/project-kickoff-meeting/).

Important steps include preparing materials, nominating a note-taker, and allowing ample time for questions. A supportive team atmosphere and a clear definition of next steps also help ensure a successful start. Atlassian also offers [a clear agenda for the kickoff](https://www.atlassian.com/work-management/project-management/project-kickoff).
