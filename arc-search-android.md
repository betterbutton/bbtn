# Arc is not just a browser, but now also a search engine
> 

The Browser Company has introduced its [Arc Search for Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=company.thebrowser.arc&hl=cs_CZ) browser in open beta. Until recently, the Arc browser was only available in the Apple ecosystem and on Windows; it is now entering Android with the aim of becoming a multi-platform alternative to the dominant Chrome.

The search engine's main feature is "Browse for Me," which uses AI to summarize relevant information from multiple websites, [reports TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/04/the-browser-company-launches-arc-search-on-android/). Critics point out the potential negative impact on small websites due to the use of their content. The app also offers an ad blocker and bookmark archiving, although some features from the iOS version are still missing.
