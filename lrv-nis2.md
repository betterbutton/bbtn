# Draft of the Cyber Security Act received green light from the government's legislative council

> 

The government's legislative council recommended [the draft of the cyber security act](https://odok.cz/portal/veklep/material/ALBSCSSFKU7S/) for government discussion. The act implements [the European NIS2 directive](https://osveta.nukib.gov.cz/course/view.php?id=145) and its submitter is NÚKIB, which also [informed about the new development](https://nukib.gov.cz/cs/infoservis/aktuality/2125-legislativni-rada-vlady-doporucila-navrh-zakona/) in the legislative process. The legislative council is an advisory body of the government and prepares opinions for it on new laws. In the previous draft, it mainly criticized the intention to concentrate powers in NÚKIB. More details on the developments around NIS2 are covered in [the series on Lupa](https://www.lupa.cz/serialy/regulace-podle-nis2/).
