# Dark mode saves up to 29% energy
> 

In the case of dark system mode, the energy saving can be up to 29 percent compared to when this mode is deactivated, writes Mobilmania. Activating dark mode on a smartphone with an OLED display can significantly extend battery life.

Thanks to the ability of OLED panels to turn off individual pixels when displaying black, the energy saving is proven, . The test showed that devices with a dark background last more than three hours longer than with a white one. However, this mode can worsen the readability of the user interface, which can be a disadvantage for some users.
