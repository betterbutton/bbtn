# AI search engine Perplexity reaches one hundred million queries per week
> 

The AI-powered search engine, [Perplexity now reaches 100 million queries per week](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/25/perplexity-says-its-now-serving-100m-search-queries-a-week/), which represents approximately 400 million queries per month. The company also plans to expand features for e-commerce and introduce a new model of advertising queries. Currently, it is negotiating with major brands such as Nike and Marriott.

[Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/), however, faces legal disputes with publishers, including News Corp and The New York Times, which accuse the startup of unauthorized use of content. The company also plans to secure investments of half a billion dollars at an estimated value of eight billion dollars.

Search engine optimization specialists have been wondering for some time how AI is mixing up search results and the search engine landscape. Could any of the new AI-based search services threaten Google's position?
