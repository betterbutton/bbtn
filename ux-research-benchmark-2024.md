# UX Research Maturity Benchmark 2024
> 

UXinsight has launched the [UXR Maturity Benchmark 2024](https://web.norstatsurveys.com/survey/selfserve/53c/2408572?list=100) survey, which focuses on the maturity of in-house user research in the UX field. The survey considers current trends and challenges, and aims to discover how practices in this area are changing and evolving.

By completing the questionnaire, you will gain valuable insight into your own practices and be among the first to receive the full report with the benchmark results. The survey covers topics such as research strategies, resources, methods, standards, and the overall impact of research on the organization.

Similar surveys were also conducted last year. It's worth recalling them:

- [UX Research Maturity Report 2023](https://maze.co/resources/research-maturity-report/)

  - user research stimulates business growth

  - shortens time-to-market

  - increases revenue

  - improves brand adoption

- [State of User Research Report 2023](https://www.userinterviews.com/state-of-user-research-2023-report)

  - US researchers earn \$100—\$200k annually

  - half of researchers work in decentralized teams

  - one ResOps supports 21 colleagues

  - 87% of research is conducted remotely

  - 51% of researchers work remotely

  - 65% of researchers rely solely on their own clients for recruitment

  - a common toolset for a researcher includes 13 items

  - one-fifth of researchers used AI in 2023

  - layoffs occurred
