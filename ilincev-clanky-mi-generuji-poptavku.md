Designer [Ondřej Ilinčev was a guest on the Upside Down podcast](https://www.vzhurudolu.cz/podcast/248-podcast-ilincev). In the interview, Ondřej focuses on how to optimize websites, applications, and e-shops. He describes his work, which involves improving user-friendliness and accessibility, often with minimal code intervention.

> "The added value is that I say: These ten things are absolutely fundamental, they will help you the most."\
> **— Ondřej Ilinčev, [Upside Down](https://www.vzhurudolu.cz/podcast/248-podcast-ilincev)**

He also discusses how accessibility is becoming an increasingly important topic. Ondřej emphasizes selecting the most crucial adjustments that have the greatest impact, allowing him to maximize efficiency and minimize developers' work.

> "Accessibility is another bogeyman that will appear in a year. We need to prepare for it."\
> **— Ondřej Ilinčev, [Upside Down](https://www.vzhurudolu.cz/podcast/248-podcast-ilincev)**

Later, Ondřej reveals how he combines content creation and newsletters with successful lead generation. He describes how every published article brings new opportunities, with the most successful articles generating dozens of inquiries.

> "I always try to find the tool that is the simplest possible."\
> **— Ondřej Ilinčev, [Upside Down](https://www.vzhurudolu.cz/podcast/248-podcast-ilincev)**

Ondřej also shares his process for selecting topics and mentions tools he uses to organize his ideas, such as MyMind and Notion. He discusses how he adapts his methods to be as effective as possible and minimizes the complexity of the tools he uses.
