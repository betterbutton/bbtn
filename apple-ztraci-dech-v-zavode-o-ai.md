# Apple is falling behind in the AI innovation race
> 
> "Result? Complete fiasco. The model is two years behind the competition."\
> **— [David Grudl](https://x.com/DavidGrudl/thread/1851689405444166097)**

Apple is significantly behind competitors such as Google, Meta, and OpenAI in the development of generative artificial intelligence. According to a report by Mark Gurman from Bloomberg, "[Apple is more than two years behind the industry leaders](https://www.cultofmac.com/news/apple-seemingly-knows-apple-intelligence-not-good-as-chatgpt)", although this year it introduced a suite of features called Apple Intelligence, which includes tools for writing, photo memories, and a smarter Siri. Users will have to wait until iOS 18.1 for key AI features, but even the best elements will arrive gradually until next year. Despite the delay, Apple has the potential to catch up quickly thanks to its tight integration of hardware and software.
