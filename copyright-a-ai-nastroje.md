Petra Dolejšová: The prompts themselves are mostly not copyrighted works. But I recommend archiving them.

## 

·Lupa.cz

Attorney [Petra Dolejšová in an interview on Lupa](https://www.lupa.cz/clanky/petra-dolejsova-samotne-prompty-vetsinou-autorskym-dilem-nejsou-doporucuju-je-ale-archivovat/) explains how it is with copyright when using AI tools. If a person uses an AI tool as an assistant, the copyright remains with them. In case the AI tool creates a work independently, it is not a copyrighted work. Prompts themselves are mostly not protected unless they are extremely detailed and creative. She recommends archiving the prompt history so that the creator can prove their invention in case of a legal dispute.
