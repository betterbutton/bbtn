# Alternatives to WordPress
> 

Given the current situation around WordPress, David Darnes has compiled an [overview of alternative content management systems](https://darn.es/wordpress-alternatives/) that may better suit various needs. He highlights systems that can be easily deployed on a server without complex setups.

He mentions his favorites, such as Ghost and Magic Pages, and recommends other options like Kirby, Craft CMS, or Wagtail. The text also touches on lesser-known CMSs and warns about potential pitfalls of some, such as low developer activity.

If you have a website on WordPress and recent events are making you consider migrating to another CMS, you don't have it easy. When it comes to the breadth of features and management convenience, no competitor is even close to being as developed. The same applies to support from web hosting providers.
