# The Magic of UX: How Uber Turned Its Failure into Success
> 

Uber has mastered several challenges since its launch in 2011 thanks to thoughtful UX design. In the early phase, they focused on a simple user interface, especially the *set destination* and *pickup* functions, which were easy to understand – as summarized by Punit Chawla in the article [How Uber Conquered UX Design](https://blog.prototypr.io/how-uber-conquered-ux-design-1d9c56788a41).

Uber also used clear explanatory messages and SMS bookings to simplify the process even for users without the app. Later, the team worked on interactions and animations that provided better feedback for users, including visual feedback on the route and price changes.
