Honza Sládek [in the robotic podcast](https://www.fakturoid.cz/blog/2024/10/03/podcast-contember) Fakturoidu predicts that instead of the current focus on writing code, the emphasis will shift to consultations and client work, with AI facilitating backend creation. Although AI will significantly change the development process, Honza emphasizes that it will not replace human creativity and decision-making.

> "Writing code will take about 30% of the time, the rest will be consultations... I think companies will be more consultancies than devshops."\
> — **Jan Sládek**

The conversation also returns to Honza's early career, organizing events like Barcamp, and the future of SaaS.
