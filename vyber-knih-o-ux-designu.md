# Selection of books on UX design, research, and accessibility by Stéphanie Walter
> 

Stéphanie Walter shares her [favorite books on UX design, research, accessibility](https://stephaniewalter.design/blog/bibliography-selected-books-about-design-user-experience/) and other topics. She divided the list into categories – for example, UX basics, research methods, psychology, information architecture and content, design ethics, mobile and web design, and accessibility.

Each book is supplemented with a short review and links to where it can be purchased. Stéphanie recommends books for both beginners and experienced professionals. She also notes that the article contains affiliate links and will be continuously updated with new titles.
