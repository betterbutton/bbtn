# Image Alt Text Generator
> 
Struggling to convince your colleagues to diligently add alt text to images? Perhaps you recall the GPT agent [Image Alt Describer](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-045n12ghj-image-alt-describer/c/47bf4383-4bb1-4b42-b73d-d487529c78f4) by Vašek Vančura, which we referenced [in newsletter \#03](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/03#:~:text=Popisujete%20obr%C3%A1zky%20na%20webu%3F). If you'd prefer a ready-made service that can be directly integrated with a CMS or e-shop, then here's [a tip from Tereza Koudělková from IDC](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7218572151656775680/) for [AI Alt Text Generator](https://alttext.ai/). You can compare outputs, for example, on the preview image [for the one-pager about the AI Act](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/ai-act-zacina-platit.
