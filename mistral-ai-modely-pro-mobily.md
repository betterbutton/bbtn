# Mistral introduces AI models for mobiles and laptops
> 

French startup [Mistral presented the first generative AI models optimized for devices like mobiles and laptops](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/16/mistral-releases-new-ai-models-optimized-for-edge-devices/). The series [The Ministers](https://mistral.ai/news/ministraux/) includes the Ministral 3B and 8B models, which support up to 128,000 tokens and offer effective solutions for private, offline applications.

The 8B model is currently available for research, while commercial licenses require contacting the company. Mistral is thus responding to the growing interest in smaller, powerful models for edge devices, where it must compete with established players like Meta and Google. The price for using the models ranges from 4 to 10 cents per million tokens.

It won't be long before we see the first meaningful applications built on AI models running on mobile.
