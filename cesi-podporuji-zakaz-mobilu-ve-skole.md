# Survey: Czechs support ban on mobile phones in schools
> 

Majority of Czechs support a ban on mobile phones in primary schools, as [shows the survey](https://www.irozhlas.cz/zpravy-domov/cesi-podle-pruzkumu-souhlasi-se-zakazem-mobilu-na-zakladnich-skolach-mysli-si-ze_2410010500_edr) by the Median agency for Radiožurnál. A total of 74% of respondents completely or somewhat agree with a blanket ban and over 80% definitely or somewhat believe that frequent use of mobile phones is harmful to children.

The attitude varies by level of education – most agree people with secondary education without a high school diploma. The topic is current also thanks to local bans, for example in Vsetín. Luboš Zajíc from the Association of Primary School Principals, however, according to Radiožurnál, prefers setting rules instead of a complete ban. In secondary schools, the ban seems less likely.
