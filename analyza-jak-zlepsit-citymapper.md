# Analysis: How Citymapper Could Improve Urban Transit Experience
> 

Dalila Maganinho attempted to [analyze the popular urban mobility app](https://medium.com/@dalilamaganinho/what-if-you-only-needed-one-app-for-urban-travel-872eceeba5b3) Citymapper. This app once served as inspiration for us at Seznam for [Schedules](https://www.seznam.cz/jizdnirady/), and it is therefore useful for us to reconsider what a modern trip planning app should be able to do.

[Citymapper](https://citymapper.com/?lang=en) plans to add ticket purchasing, which users still lack. Citymapper Pass, a pilot project from London in 2019, was discontinued due to scaling issues. User feedback points to the need for integrated ticketing, solutions for language barriers, and safer routes.

Proposed solutions include improving ticket accessibility with a feature for finding sales points or using augmented reality to aid navigation.
