# Ward Christensen, Pioneer of Digital BBS Forums, Has Died
> 

![Ward Christensen](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/49472e9d-bff2-47b9-9c03-842ec927d0a7_2048x975.jpeg "1456x693")

[Ward Christensen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ward_Christensen) together with Randy Suess created the first BBS in 1978. This inspired the creation of communities that preceded today's online world. BBS systems in the 80s and 90s brought the first forms of online games and discussion forums, and also influenced the development of shareware games.

Christensen, described as a modest innovator, never desired to be in the spotlight. Throughout his life, he pursued a career at IBM, while viewing his contributions to the digital world with humility, writes Ars Technica. He died at the age of 78.
