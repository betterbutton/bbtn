# UX Specialist Salaries in the USA: Inflation and Its Impact
> 

Jakob Nielsen [summarizes the recent survey of UX specialist salaries in the USA](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7219103397780557825/). It revealed that compensation for UX professionals with five years of experience fell by 12% in 2023 after accounting for inflation. Although this seems dramatic at first glance, it represents a return to a long-term trend after a period of an overheated market.

The situation is worse for fresh graduates – their salaries have dropped below the long-term average for the first time since 1998. It now remains to be seen whether this is a one-time fluctuation or a new standard.
