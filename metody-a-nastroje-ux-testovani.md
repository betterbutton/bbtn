# Methods and Tools of UX Testing
> 

UX testing helps understand how users perceive and use websites and applications, which is crucial for their success. [Various UX testing methods](https://medium.com/@quartedesign/user-experience-testing-methods-and-tools-329f163b40ad), such as laboratory testing, guerrilla tests, or card sorting, allow for improving the functionality and intuitiveness of products.

Tools like UserTesting, Hotjar, or Maze then streamline the testing process. The right choice of method depends on the project's goals, budget, and the demographics of the target group. User feedback facilitates the identification of problems and opportunities for improvement.
