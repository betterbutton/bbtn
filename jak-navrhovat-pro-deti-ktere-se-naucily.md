# How to design for children who learned to click before they talked?
> 

Generation Alpha, children born with a tablet in hand, are growing up in a world where technology is a natural part of daily life. For the youngest of them (2–4 years), digital interaction is play and a key to developing basic skills. [UX design for toddlers](https://medium.com/x-periment-asteroid/little-users-big-needs-designing-ux-for-kids-chapter-1-ae022d98fbaa) focuses on clear interfaces with bright colors and simple shapes, easily accessible elements, safety, and support for exploration. A functional and fun environment with an emphasis on positive feedback helps young users grow and learn, while offering parents peace of mind that the content is safe.
