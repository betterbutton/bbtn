Heydon Pickering, in his talk [The Folly of Chasing Demographics](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XntrwuBjE5c), warns about the dangers that arise from attempting to categorize users based on demographics or assumptions.

He recommends avoiding complexity and "design for effect" elements, and instead focusing on maximum simplicity that facilitates accessibility for everyone, regardless of specific needs or technology. The key message is that excessive effort to understand user demographics often leads to incorrect conclusions and unnecessary barriers.
