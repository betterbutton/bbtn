# Where to Find Skilled Designers
> 

Finding a web designer can be overwhelming, but with a few simple steps, you can manage it. [Mikoláš Voborský wrote a guide](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7229381266591158272/) that could instantly be one [of the tips in our book](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/kniha) about collaboration between clients and designers.

> „From social networks, I recommend LinkedIn. Use ‚web design‘ and ‚websites‘ as keywords.“\
> **— [Mikoláš Voborský](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7229381266591158272/)**

Start by asking acquaintances whose websites you like. Contact the owners of websites that caught your eye and find out who created them. Another option are portals and social networks like LinkedIn.

Use search engines as a last resort, as larger agencies and beginner firms dominate there. Don't forget to request offers from multiple designers so you can compare and make an informed decision.
