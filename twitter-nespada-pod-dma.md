# Twitter's users have dwindled: It no longer falls under DMA regulation
> 

[The European Commission has decided](https://ec.europa.eu/commission/presscorner/detail/en/mex_24_5324) that [Twitter does not meet the gatekeeper conditions under the DMA regulation](https://www.socialmediatoday.com/news/eu-commission-says-x-formerly-twitter-not-gatekeeper/730067/#:~:text=Today%2C%20the%20European%20Commission%20has,part%20of%20anti%2Dmonopoly%20regulations.). It will thus avoid strict rules regarding competitor access and cooperation with third parties.

The platform with 105 million users in the EU is declining in popularity, while competitors like Meta and TikTok have significantly more active users. Although Elon Musk's fans call this decision a victory, the finding that Twitter is not a significant market player confirms its loss of market relevance.
