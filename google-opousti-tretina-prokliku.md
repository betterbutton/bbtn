# Google lets only just over a third of searches proceed to the web
> 

Out of every 1000 Google searches, 374 result in a click to an external website in the EU – according to the [Zero-Click Search Study](https://sparktoro.com/blog/2024-zero-click-search-study-for-every-1000-us-google-searches-only-374-clicks-go-to-the-open-web-in-the-eu-its-360/). Nearly 60% of searches end without a click, while many others remain within Google's ecosystem (Maps, YouTube...). Only 1.4% of clicks go to ads.

Regulations in the EU have slightly limited Google's favoring of its own services, but the impact is limited. In the US, the share of clicks from searches to the web is even lower. Overall, Google continues to dominate search with a growing share of searches per user and maintains a high share of directing clicks from search results to its own projects.
