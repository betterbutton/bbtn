# Beeper: all chat services in one inbox
> 

*"Láďa wrote to me, but I discarded the notification and now I can't find out which service it was through,"* says Kuba sadly to Martin during a meeting at a cafe in the Smíchov Wine Food market. If you have a similar problem, using the [Beeper](https://www.beeper.com/) app might be a solution for you.

Beeper is an app that combines receiving and sending messages via WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Twitter, Android SMS, Google Messages (SMS/RCS), Telegram, Signal, Matrix, Slack, Google Chat, Instagram, IRC, Discord, and LinkedIn. So actually, among the major ones, only iMessage is missing.

Tomáš Sobel raises [a security question](https://x.com/sobeltomas/status/1808211471811465450) about an app like Beeper: *"How reasonable is it to cram all my communicators into one place and thus simplify any attack vector?"* Beeper addresses security concerns [on its website](https://www.beeper.com/faq#Security-and-Privacy), including a warning that using the native apps of specific services directly may be safer.
