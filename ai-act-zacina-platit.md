# The European AI Regulation will come into effect on August 2nd
> 

Already on August 2nd, the European [AI Regulation](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/cs/policies/regulatory-framework-ai) (AI Act), published in the EU Official Journal, will become applicable. Companies and authorities have two years to prepare; some parts will be enforceable after just six months. The regulation will also affect organizations outside the EU if they have an impact on EU markets or citizens. AI that manipulates people, exploits children, or uses biometric identification will be banned.

You can learn [more about the AI regulation](https://www.kapler.cz/evropsky-parlament-schvalil-novy-zakon-o-umele-inteligenci/) on the website of Tomáš Kapler or the [AI Association](https://asociace.ai/eu-ai-act-byl-dnes-zverejnen-v-urednim-vestniku-jako-narizeni-evropskeho-parlamentu-a-rady-eu-2024-1689-co-ted/).
