# Website Creation Step by Step with Jan Štráfelda
> 

We [recommend hiring a guide for the project](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/36?open=false#%C2%A7tip-z-nasi-knihy). A prerequisite for creating a successful website is not just choosing a supplier, but careful preparation of the brief and concept. [Jan advises dividing the project into two phases](https://www.strafelda.cz/pruvodce-online-projektem) – conceptual and implementation – similar to building a house with an architect.

„Most fundamental flaws on websites arise already during the creation of the brief.“\
— **[Jan Štráfelda](https://www.strafelda.cz/pruvodce-online-projektem)**

The correct brief determines the website's functionality and helps avoid common mistakes that suppliers often overlook. As a project guide, Jan helps with market analysis, brief creation, supplier selection, and subsequent supervision of the implementation.
