# CZ.NIC: The Most Expensive Auctioned Domain Cost 55,600 CZK
> 

> **Updated 2024-07-16:** The domain auction has a new record. We write about it in the article [Domain Auctions Have a New Record: 152,077 CZK](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/rekordni-cena-domeny-cz).

We write about the auction of Czech domains [in the current newsletter issue](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/24#:~:text=Dnes%20jde%20do%20aukce%20667%20%C4%8Desk%C3%BDch%20dom%C3%A9n.). The auction of expired Czech domains has been running since May and has so far raised 646,380 CZK. CZ.NIC has now [published some interesting figures](https://blog.nic.cz/2024/06/19/aukce-domen-mesic-po-spusteni/). The highest bid was for the domain [praguecityline.cz](http://www.praguecityline.cz/); the winner of the auction paid 55,600 CZK. There was also great interest in the domain virtualnisidlo.cz, which was auctioned for 95,001 CZK, but the auction winner has not yet paid for the domain.

A total of 684 domains were auctioned and paid for at an average price of 1,170 CZK, but in more than half of the cases, one bid of a hundred CZK was enough to win. 257 people from the Czech Republic, Poland, Slovakia, and Estonia participated in the auctions.

The domain administrator is preparing the option to set a maximum bid in advance and enter a visible bid.
