# Designing an Unforgettable User Experience
> 

[Great user experience design](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/design-principles-for-exceptional-user-experiences-ff2ea20de6b0) stems not only from functionality but also from emotional connection and adaptability. Emotional engagement, such as joy from interactive elements or a sense of achievement, increases memorability, reflects Mehedi Hassan.

Modern design systems, like Google Material, must also be adaptable and resilient to technological changes. A holistic approach, as shown by Apple, enables seamless interaction across products and channels. These principles encourage designers to gain a deeper understanding of users and create products that not only satisfy them but also captivate them in the long term.

These are [four principles of exceptional UX](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/design-principles-for-exceptional-user-experiences-ff2ea20de6b0):

1.  Cognitive Fluency

2.  Emotional Engagement

3.  Adaptability and Resilience

4.  Holistic Systems Thinking

If you can't picture this, check out the article [Design Principles for Exceptional User Experiences](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/design-principles-for-exceptional-user-experiences-ff2ea20de6b0) with examples.
