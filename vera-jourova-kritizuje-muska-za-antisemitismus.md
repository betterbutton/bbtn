# Věra Jourová criticizes Elon Musk for antisemitism on platform X
> 

European Commissioner [Věra Jourová strongly criticized Elon Musk](https://www.politico.eu/article/elon-musk-promoter-evil-vera-jourova-european-commission/) for spreading hate speech, including antisemitism, on the X platform (we call it Twitter). Jourová called Musk a "propagator of evil" in an interview and claims that Musk's platform has become the main hub for spreading antisemitism. Jourová really laid into Musk:

> "We have started to relativize evil, and he is actively contributing to it. He is a propagator of evil."\
> "Of all the bosses I have met, he is the only one who cannot distinguish good from evil."\
> "If he wants to know what evil looks like, he only needs to look in the mirror."\
> — **[Věra Jourová](https://www.politico.eu/article/elon-musk-promoter-evil-vera-jourova-european-commission/)**

[Musk denied the accusations](https://weeklyblitz.net/2024/10/19/eu-commissioner-calls-musk-promoter-of-evil-over-antisemitism/), stating that the platform has taken steps to curb hate, and called Jourová hypocritical. Musk did not spare Jourová either and [in a tweet called her](https://twitter.com/elonmusk/status/1846970499122823345) the embodiment of banal bureaucratic evil.

The dispute reflects a broader [debate between freedom of expression and the need to regulate digital platforms](https://www.irozhlas.cz/zpravy-svet/musk-kritizoval-jourovou-oznacil-ji-za-ztelesneni-banalniho-byrokratickeho-zla_2410181232_vdv), especially in Europe, which, due to its history, places greater emphasis on combating antisemitism and hatred.
