# Hrubešová: Getting paid podcasts from Forendors onto Spotify took a week. Communication took a year.
> 

Czech creators on [Forendors](https://www.forendors.cz/) now have the opportunity to make their [paid podcasts also available on Spotify](https://blog.forendors.cz/tipy-triky/nastaveni/plne-verze-podcastu-k-poslechu-i-na-spotify/). In [Ondřej Aust's podcast](https://herohero.co/aust), [Denisa Hrubešová](https://www.forendors.cz/diseasezoe), founder of the platform for digital creators, talked about it:

> „Getting our podcasts on Spotify took us a year. Navigating that communication. \[…\] And we didn't expect it to succeed at all. \[…\] Then it took a week to implement.“

At the end of the interview, the topic turned to [payment for series](https://www.forendors.cz/faq/hledat?r=s%C3%A9rie):

> „We have lots of creators who want to create, but don't have time and don't have capacity. And they don't actually want to create a video every week or every month. But they would really like to put together some thematic collections that you can buy from them. \[…\] We have quite good feedback and lots of people are thinking about how to use it.“

You can [listen to the first half-hour in the podcast](https://ceskepodcasty.cz/epizoda/437179), the rest on [HeroHero.com/Aust](https://HeroHero.com/Aust).
