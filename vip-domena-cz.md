# VIP domain protects and secures Czech domains
> 

The registration fee for a `.cz` domain is just over a hundred crowns depending on the registrar, but you can also pay a couple of million for it. The administrator of Czech domains, CZ.NIC, introduced the [VIP domain](https://vip.nic.cz/cs/) service at the beginning of the year. The domain is transferred from a commercial provider directly to the infrastructure of the Czech domain administrator, locked against administrative changes, and meets the requirements for critical information infrastructure under the European NIS2 directive.

CZ.NIC set the price at 2.5 million per year per domain and offers volume discounts for five and twenty domains. At the highest tier, which comes to 20 million per year, VIP domain offers an SLA setting of 1 minute and refunds 100% of the price if the downtime exceeds the limit. [The first known client](https://www.nic.cz/whois/domain/t-mobile.cz/) is a purple mobile operator. (source [CZ.NIC](https://www.nic.cz/page/4440/))
