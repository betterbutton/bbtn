# Google Docs allow electronic signing of a document
> 

Google in the summer update for paid Workspace plans activated [eSignature – the option for electronic document signing](https://workspaceupdates.googleblog.com/2024/07/release-notes-07-12-2024.html#:~:text=eSignature%20is%20now%20available%20for%20select%20Google%20Workspace%20customers%C2%A0). Users can request signatures on documents and manage electronic signatures directly in Google Docs. The feature allows requests for signatures from multiple users, including those without a Gmail account.

eSignature is available for customers with Business Standard, Business Plus, Enterprise, and Education Plus editions.
