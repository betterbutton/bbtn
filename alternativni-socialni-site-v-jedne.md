# Alternative Social Networks in One App: Openvibe
> 

[Openvibe](https://openvibe.social/) is an app focused on cross-posting between social networks. [It is expanding its support to Meta Threads](https://mastodon.social/@openvibe/113403233930516186). With the rise of alternatives like Threads, Bluesky, and Mastodon, users are struggling with being scattered across networks, [writes TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/11/01/cross-posting-social-app-openvibe-now-supports-threads-too/).

People scattered among new social platforms mainly after leaving Twitter following Elon Musk's acquisition. None of the new networks has yet gained a clear advantage, so users often maintain profiles on Threads, Bluesky, and Mastodon in addition to their original Twitter profile. [Threads recently adopted the ActivityPub protocol](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/threads-uz-jsou-ve-fediverse-jen), which also powers Mastodon, so users of both networks can connect directly.

The app allows following posts and interacting across networks. The new update includes support for Threads and an improved user interface. Openvibe remains free and is preparing a desktop version. A planned subscription is set to offer advanced features.
