Film [Making a Marketer 2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZtYT-Ojn49Q), on which [Jindřich Fáborský](https://blog.faborsky.cz/kontakt/), [Jan Kostera](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCUYYA22NowCLhHTk0xa3_TQ), and [Adam Francouz](https://adamfrancouz.com/) collaborated, evoked strong emotions. The film, on which both creators worked for three months, presents the marketing field without embellishments and emphasizes independence and a truthful reflection of reality.

The feature-length documentary presents today's marketing challenges and brings insights from top experts, such as [Prof. Byron Sharp](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Byron_Sharp), [Mark Ritson](https://www.marketingweek.com/mark-ritson/), or . Topics include classic debates about mass marketing versus [STP](https://www.forecom-solutions.com/cs/blog/jak-se-pripravit-na-spusteni-vykonnostniho-marketingu#:~:text=STP%20je%20zkratka%20pro%20Segmentaci,publikum%20na%20m%C3%ADru%20%C5%A1it%C3%BDmi%20sd%C4%9Blen%C3%ADmi.), discussion about brand building, return on investment, as well as the transformative impact of artificial intelligence on the future of marketing.

The documentary focuses on strategies for small and medium-sized enterprises that want to prosper, and offers practical advice on how to stay in the field.
