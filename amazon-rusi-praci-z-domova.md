# Amazon mandates return to office
> 

Amazon is mandating a full-time return to the office for employees from January, thereby ending the current hybrid work model. CEO Andy Jassy [told the BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czj99ln72k9o) that returning to the office will improve collaboration and connection among employees.

The company has long faced tension around this issue; employee protests began during previous changes in work-from-home policy. Jassy warns of the loss of corporate culture due to flexible working conditions. This move contrasts with the stance of the British government, which is set to enshrine flexible work as a default employee right.

And we just add that Amazon is only mandating the return to offices for its employees so far. Find out about the situation in your company from your managers.
