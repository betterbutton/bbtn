# Highlighted Focus Outline Improves Navigation Usability
> 

Focus outlines, or [highlighting the active element on a web page](https://medienbaecker.com/articles/focus-outlines), are crucial for keyboard users and people with motor impairments, but they also improve website usability for everyone. Yet they are often overlooked as a purely technical matter. The article advocates for an approach where focus outlines are part of the design, thereby improving both the website's visual identity and accessibility.

It recommends using them to distinguish between mouse and keyboard users and emphasizes contrast and outline readability. Testing and adapting to various backgrounds and interactions will ensure a better user experience.
