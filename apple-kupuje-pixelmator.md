# Apple acquires creator of photo editor Pixelmator
> 

[Pixelmator announced acquisition by Apple.](https://www.pixelmator.com/blog/2024/11/01/a-new-home-for-pixelmator/) Producer of popular apps Pixelmator Pro, Pixelmator for iOS and Photomator, which competes with Adobe's offerings. Pixelmator, based in Vilnius, Lithuania, focuses on user-friendliness and performance exclusively for Apple devices.

The Pixelmator team, excited about joining Apple, believes the merger will boost their global reach among creative professionals. Current Pixelmator apps will remain unchanged, but users can look forward to new updates. Financial details of the transaction were not disclosed.
