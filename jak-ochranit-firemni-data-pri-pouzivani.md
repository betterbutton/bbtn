# How to Protect Company Data When Using ChatGPT
> 

Using ChatGPT in companies can increase productivity, but it also brings risks related to data leakage. Every tenth conversation of employees with ChatGPT contains sensitive information that can be used for model training and made available to third parties.

> "ChatGPT may unintentionally re-generate your sensitive information in the responses of other users."\
> — **[David Kudrna](https://kudrnasobkova.cz/chatgpt-a-bezpecnost-prevence-uniku-dat/)**

[David Kudrna wrote a guide](https://kudrnasobkova.cz/chatgpt-a-bezpecnost-prevence-uniku-dat/) that emphasizes the importance of setting security rules, using strong passwords, multi-factor authentication, and limiting the use of plugins. The responsibility for data protection lies with the user, who should be cautious when uploading sensitive data to the system. It is advisable to regularly train employees and implement clear rules for using AI tools.
