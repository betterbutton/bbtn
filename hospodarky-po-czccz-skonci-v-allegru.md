# Business News: After CZC.cz, Mall.cz Will Also End Up in Allegro
> 

Michael Mareš writes in *Hospodářské noviny* that the Polish group [Allegro is ending the independent operation of Mall.cz](https://archiv.hn.cz/c1-67355340-potvrzeno-mall-cz-ceka-stejny-osud-jako-czc-hlavnim-cilem-je-uzdravit-nase-ceske-podniky-tvrdi-polske-allegro) and is transitioning to a marketplace model, similar to what happened previously with CZC.cz. This step is part of a strategy to unify technical and software systems with the aim of reducing costs and stabilizing Czech businesses.

> “The Polish owners have a dismissive, even arrogant attitude towards the Czech market and could completely destroy Mall.cz,”\
> **— Ondřej Fryc, founder of Mall.cz (originally Bílé zboží).**

Mall.cz, once strong but long-term loss-making e-shop, is now moving under Allegro.cz and its data will be merged into the structure of the new company from 2024. This development is a result of the decline in e-commerce sector trade and growing competition in the market.
