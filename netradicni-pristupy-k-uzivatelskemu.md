# Unconventional Approaches to User Research
> 

When you can't talk directly to users, there are [unorthodox alternatives for gaining valuable UX insights](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/when-traditional-user-research-isnt-an-option-non-traditional-ways-to-collect-insights-189b4ee2f462). M. Rijkers suggests listening in on sales calls, analyzing helpdesk queries, attending customer support sessions firsthand, or utilizing data from web analytics.

Even feedback from potential clients who ultimately chose a competitor can reveal key areas for product improvement. These methods not only uncover user needs but also concrete ways to better adapt the product to the market and win user favor.
