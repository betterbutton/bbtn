# UX design step by step: Free course from IBM
> 

Missing school? IBM has an educational portal where you can also find a [UX basics course](https://skillsbuild.org/adult-learners/explore-learning/user-experience-design#sb--adult-learners-journey-courses). The course provides an overview of key concepts and practical skills in user interface design. Participants will learn how to conduct research, create wireframes, prototypes, test usability, and incorporate feedback. The course also includes a case study on website redesign to help students apply the learned principles. By completing all seven lessons, you will receive a digital certificate from IBM that you can use to enhance your resume and LinkedIn profile.
