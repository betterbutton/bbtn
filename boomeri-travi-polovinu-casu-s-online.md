# Baby Boomers spend half their time with online media
> 

[Baby Boomers](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generace_Baby_boomers) (people born between 1946 and 1964) spend increasingly more time online, while their media behavior shifts towards digital versions of traditional media. [This follows from a survey](https://www.warc.com/content/feed/Baby_Boomers_in_big_shift_to_digital_content/9956) by Warc Media. While social networks remain on the periphery of their interest, streaming platforms like Netflix and YouTube are gaining popularity.

Although brands often target younger generations, Baby Boomers, the wealthiest demographic group, remain untapped. Their aversion to advertising poses a challenge for marketers, who must rethink their digital communication strategies – [writes MediaGuru](https://www.mediaguru.cz/clanky/2024/10/baby-boomers-travi-na-internetu-polovinu-sveho-casu-s-medii/).

Baby Boomers have spent half their lives with computers and the internet and are their savvy and demanding users.
