# Metrics for Design System
> 

Ljubljana-based designer Romina Kavčič asks which [metric for a design system](https://thedesignsystem.guide/design-system-metrics) we use, and [gives examples of metrics](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7222175497047183361/) used by well-known companies. Interesting tips appear in the discussion under the post. For example, the article [Five ways to make the most of your Design System Analytics](https://www.figma.com/best-practices/make-the-most-of-design-system-analytics/) by Figma, the article by Jerôme Benoit [Measuring Design System compliance of your teams](https://medium.com/doctolib/measuring-design-system-compliance-of-your-teams-ccbd718499f8), or the article by Cristiano Rastelli [Measuring the Impact of a Design System](https://didoo.medium.com/measuring-the-impact-of-a-design-system-7f925af090f7). And what metrics do you use?
