# Tinkerers Give New Life to Abandoned Vending Machines
> 

![Redbox DVD vending machine](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/0de6dfe2-98ad-4312-9c1b-edaa3c1a0613_1246x1804.jpeg "1456x1006")

[Mike Mozart: RedBox "Red Box" Video Movie Rental Vending Machine](https://www.flickr.com/photos/jeepersmedia/)

After the collapse of the company [Redbox](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Redbox), which dealt with video rentals, tinkerers and enthusiasts began taking its abandoned vending machines home and exploring their systems. Not only did some become sources of free DVDs, but some people also managed to program the kiosks for unexpected purposes – such as running the game Doom.

The bankruptcy caused stores like Walgreens and CVS to be left with thousands of unused vending machines, which [enthusiasts buy or receive for free](https://www.404media.co/tinkerers-are-taking-old-redbox-kiosks-home-and-reverse-engineering-them/). On platforms like Reddit or Facebook Marketplace, sales are starting not only for vending machines but also for hundreds of DVD discs from these devices.
