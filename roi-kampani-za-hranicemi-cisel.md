# ROI: Measure campaign success beyond the numbers
> 

In the jubilee 30th edition of the [Be Your Own Marketer](https://www.samsobemarketerem.cz/ newsletter, the focus is on [return on investment](https://www.samsobemarketerem.cz/p/roi-v-marketingu-cisla-i-smysl-investic) (ROI) in marketing. It offers a look beyond classic [financial metrics](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/N%C3%A1vratnost). Besides profit from campaigns, other benefits must be considered, such as customer satisfaction, brand reputation, or improvement of the customer experience.

Jan points out a common mistake of blindly following trends without considering benefits, using the example of unnecessary chatbot deployment in small businesses. ROI isn't just about money, but also about the value we invest and receive back in the form of growth and satisfaction.
