# Supreme Audit Office audited management of basic registers
> 

[NKÚ audited financing of basic registers.](https://www.nku.cz/cz/pro-media/tiskove-zpravy/nku-kontroloval-miliardy-vydane-na-zakladni-registry:-zastaravajici-infrastruktura-neni-obmenena-a-stale-chybi-bezodstavkovy-provoz-id14037/) The report concerns the previous Administration of Basic Registers, which was abolished. The registers now fall under the Digital and Information Agency. The NKÚ criticizes the Administration of Basic Registers for underinvesting in the service and being unable to ensure its operation without outages.

[DIA commented on the NKÚ report](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7205863958321246208/ on Linkedin: *"We are currently intensively working on restoring the hardware of the basic registers. It has already been purchased and its implementation is underway."*
