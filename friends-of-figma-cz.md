# Friends of Figma has a Czech branch
> 

Elham Kargar and Šimon Jůn founded the Czech branch of [Friends of Figma](https://friends.figma.com/czech-republic/). The fan club is the official user community supported directly by Figma. [Šimon promises](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7212138072153427968/) networking, experience sharing, news, and 😍 merch. Already on the first day, the community gathered 65 friends.
