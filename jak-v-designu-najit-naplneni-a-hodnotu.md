# How to Find Fulfillment and Value in Design
> 

Success in design is not just about metrics and numbers. The true meaning of work comes when [design positively influences people and reflects the creator's values](https://medium.com/@designgrapes/beyond-metrics-designing-for-impact-and-lasting-value-a3430f35370e). Instead of chasing productivity, it's important to spend time on quality and user care so that design truly makes life easier. Personal fulfillment then comes with a career that doesn't focus on external recognition, but on meaningful results and long-term impact.

> „The true measure of success lies in creating interfaces that empower users by enhancing accessibility, usability, and ease of use.“
