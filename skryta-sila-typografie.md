# The Hidden Power of Typography
> 

Have you ever wondered why [Microsoft invests in typography](https://microsoft.design/articles/the-hidden-power-of-typography?ref=sidebar)? Typography influences our perception and behavior more than we think. Studies show that well-designed documents have a greater impact and are more influential than those without quality design. For example, scientists Rolf Reber and Norbert Schwarz found that [text with high contrast is considered more credible](https://carlo-hamalainen.net/stuff/Reber_Schwarz_Perceptual_fluency.pdf) than text with low contrast. Similarly, Hyunjin Song and Norbert Schwarz found that [more complex fonts force people to be more critical](https://guilfordjournals.com/doi/10.1521/soco.2008.26.6.791).
