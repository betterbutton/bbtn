# Best UX Books for Free
> 

They say the best things in life are free. Does that apply to UX books? Maybe. When the publisher [A Book Apart](https://abookapart.com/) closed down this year, it returned licensed copyrights to the authors, and many of them then posted electronic versions of their books on their websites for free download. They are the best books in the field, published with extraordinary care and attention.

Another interesting collection is the [Design Better](https://www.invisionapp.com/inside-design/design-better-epub/) books from InVision. Adobe also publishes design books, and others contribute too, whether corporate publishers like Avocode, 37 Signals, Marvel, Sitepoint, or the authors directly. So you wouldn't have to search for books individually, people around Lapa Ninja compiled a [library of free design books](https://www.lapabooks.com/) for download. The books are sorted into categories like Design System, User Experience, Visual Design, Usability, Typography, Front-End Development, Startup, and Career, but even so, there are so many in each category that you'll have reading material for at least a year.

If you don't want to go through dozens of books but are only interested in the crème de la crème, then follow Stéphanie Walter's selection of her [10+ favorite books for UX designers](https://stephaniewalter.design/blog/10-free-ebooks-for-designers-and-a-trick-to-access-more-books-for-a-small-fee/).
