# Introduction to Design Systems – a course by Figma
> 

Are you constantly hearing about design systems around you but struggling to find your way and figure out where to start? Figma has prepared a free [five-part introductory course on design systems](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/sections/14548397990423-Introduction-to-design-systems-5-parts). In the course, you will learn to define your own design system, build it in Figma, document it, and set up rules for maintenance. As a bonus, you will learn to work with tokens, variables, and styles, which are useful Figma features for design systems.
