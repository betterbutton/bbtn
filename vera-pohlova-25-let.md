# These scandals just annoy everyone after 25 years
> 

> **Věra Pohlová, 72 years, retiree:**\
> – These scandals just annoy everyone. I would ban all those internets and computers.

It vividly [marked the quarter-century since the publication](https://www.zive.cz/clanky/ja-bych-vsechny-ty-internety-a-pocitace-zakazala/sc-3-a-200275/default.aspx) of the Metro newspaper survey from September 17, 1999, on the misuse of data from clients' savings accounts at Česká spořitelna. The response of the alleged respondent Věra Pohlová (then 72 years old, a retiree) almost immediately became an internet phenomenon and one of the first memes. The mythical retiree was never discovered\* and [there are doubts](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/J%C3%A1_bych_v%C5%A1echny_ty_internety_a_po%C4%8D%C3%ADta%C4%8De_zak%C3%A1zala) about the authenticity of the statement.

![Photograph of a newspaper clipping with a photo of an older woman wearing tinted glasses and bold lipstick, and text: Věra Pohlová, 72 years, retiree: These scandals just annoy everyone. I would ban all those internets and computers.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/dc476c04-80a8-4bea-8ead-b5d69b10b061_710x488.jpeg "710x488")

> **\*Update 2025-09-17:** Retiree Věra Pohlová may perhaps not be a completely mythical figure. Five years ago, Karolinum Publishing published a book [Věra Pohlová. Social Work and Life in the 20th Century](https://karolinum.cz/knihy/kodymova-vera-pohlova-23018) and the photo of a young woman with glasses on the cover could very well belong to the author of the folklorized statement.
