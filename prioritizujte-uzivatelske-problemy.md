# Prioritize user problems using a severity scale
> 

![Severity scale (see text below)](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/5397d223-b7c0-4d07-b06f-5a5e8161bdf9_1088x815.png "1088x815")

[Severity scale for user feedback](https://www.userinterviews.com/blog/user-feedback-severity-scale) is a tool that helps prioritize problems more effectively, based not only on their frequency of occurrence but also on their impact. When building your own scale, it's important to involve key teams, such as research or product teams.

A typical scale includes categories ranging from minor comments to severe problems that could threaten, for example, regulatory compliance. The key is evaluating the combination of severity and frequency, which leads to better decision-making and product development.

This type of scale is useful where you don't know the effort of changes and all you can assess is the impact of the findings on user-friendliness.
