# Small Details That Make a Big Difference in Design
> 

Daniel Fedorin likes [“micro-details” on blogs and personal sites](https://danilafe.com/blog/blog_microfeatures/). Although they are not essential, they can significantly improve the user experience. These include things like marginalia (side notes), an article outline, navigation for article series, headings that serve as link targets, and page progress indicators.

Daniel shares his own experience with implementing some of them and draws inspiration from other websites, hoping to motivate readers to improve their own sites.
