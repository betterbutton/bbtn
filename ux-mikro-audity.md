# UX micro-audits key to success of digital products
> 

Chirag Sharawat is an advocate of using UX audits. UX audits help improve usability, accessibility, and the overall effectiveness of digital products. Yet UX audits are often not conducted due to lack of time and stakeholder support. [According to Chirag, micro-audits can be the solution.](https://medium.com/@uxandyouti/ux-audits-and-their-need-036bcbfcd712)
