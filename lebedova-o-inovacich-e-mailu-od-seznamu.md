The product team for Email from Seznam is led by Štěpánka Lebedová. [In the WebTop100 podcast](https://www.webtop100.cz/inspirace/podcast-webtop100-stepanka-lebedova-email-cz) she says that Seznam still holds more than half of all Czech email inboxes. She explains this primarily by Seznam's earlier entry into the freemail market and user inertia. Štěpánka discusses with Michal Mládek how important it is to deliver content that truly interests users, and what practices Email.cz uses to ensure relevance and email deliverability.

Štěpánka also mentions the impact of cookie restrictions on the growing importance of email marketing and the need to tailor content for different market segments. At Seznam, they are preparing a modernization of the email environment, including new features and appearance, to attract new users. She emphasizes transparent communication, market education, and building a tool that is beneficial for everyone.

If you're curious how relevant the [Email Profi](https://emailprofi.cz/) service is, you might be surprised that Seznam.cz migrated employee mailboxes to it.
