Tereza Kosnarová Venerová hosted the podcast of the Poslepu server [Without Barriers](https://poslepu.cz/podcast-bez-barier/). The hosts of the show Radek Pavlíček and Ondřej Pohl and their guest T[ereza Kosnarová Venerová](https://www.uxworkshopy.cz/) are devoted to the topic of education in digital accessibility and inclusion. They discuss the motivation for learning accessibility, teaching methods, and challenges they face in imparting this knowledge. They share the successes of their students and emphasize the importance of incorporating accessibility into education from elementary school.

During the episode Tereza, Radek, and Ondřej mention specific resources and platforms where you can get more information about accessibility, and they encourage involvement in the community around inclusive design. If you prefer reading over listening, a [text transcript](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1iCqWyOTmQqhOPQsJvll2JcxfSfXSCC1Rq29vrD4OzeE/edit) ([archive](https://archive.ph/72CdN)) is also available.
