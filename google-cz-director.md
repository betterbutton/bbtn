# Google is looking for a successor to Taťána le Moigne to lead the Czech branch
> 

Taťána le Moigne led the Czech Google for an impressive 18 years. During those years, we in the Czech Republic have always gotten preferential access to new products and features, which is not very common for such a small market. On Google's website, there is already an [advertisement for Country Director](https://www.google.com/about/careers/applications/jobs/results/123438405110375110-country-director-czechia?location=Prague%2C%20Czech%20Republic) for the Czech Republic.
