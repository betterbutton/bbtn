# SEO experts on the new search GPT and its possibilities
> 

Experts Pavel Unger, Martin Žatkovič, Vojtěch Horák and Marek Lecián [debated the potential of the new Search GPT](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iwauOk9gV6A) by OpenAI. The discussion focused on the model's approach to content indexing, comparison with Google and Bing, and possibilities for measuring traffic.

The conclusion was clear: Search GPT does not yet replace Google, but represents another useful tool that offers SEO specialists an alternative approach to structured content and creates space for quality content and authentic branding. Experts recommend monitoring further development and adapting content to be easily accessible even for less technologically advanced browsers.
