# Fonts of Old House Number Plates
> 

Enamelled signs with street names and house numbers shape the visual identity of cities. Lettering patterns often date back to the era long before the digitization of typography. Although the templates for enamelled signs are now prepared on plotters, the original fonts, drawn by hand for each enamelled label, are still used.

In the Graphic Designers group on Facebook, a [font faithful to house number plates](https://www.facebook.com/groups/151639034871127/permalink/8759422294092715/) is currently being sought before the introduction of Arial.
