# In 2025, the GOV.CZ domain will unify the websites of state institutions
> 

From 2025, all Czech state institutions will be unified under the central domain `gov.cz`, which will improve the clarity and security of online services. This step, announced by the government in 2023, aims to increase protection against phishing and other attacks by ensuring that users know they are on trustworthy pages.

In the unified address system, for example [mze.gov.cz](https://mze.gov.cz) for the Ministry of Agriculture, it will be easier for citizens to navigate. This transition is part of the government schedule and gradually replaces older domains, enabling reliable access to information.
