# Father of the Russian Internet Behind Bars
> 

[Alexej Soldatov](https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%A1%D0%BE%D0%BB%D0%B4%D0%B0%D1%82%D0%BE%D0%B2,_%D0%90%D0%BB%D0%B5%D0%BA%D1%81%D0%B5%D0%B9_%D0%90%D0%BD%D0%B0%D1%82%D0%BE%D0%BB%D1%8C%D0%B5%D0%B2%D0%B8%D1%87) (72 years old), a pioneer of the Russian internet, was sentenced on Monday to two years in a penal colony for abuse of power. Soldatov created the academic network [Relcom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RELCOM) at the [Kurchatov Institute](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kurchatov_Institute), which he then connected to the internet in 1990. Thirty years later, he was arrested in December 2019 and is now being sent to prison by a Russian court. The court accuses Soldatov of diverting nearly half a million IPv4 addresses, worth about 600 million rubles (160 million crowns), from Russia to the Prague-based company [Reliable Communications](https://or.justice.cz/ias/ui/rejstrik-$firma?nazev=Reliable+Communications).

[The Russian Wikipedia states](https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%A1%D0%BE%D0%BB%D0%B4%D0%B0%D1%82%D0%BE%D0%B2,_%D0%90%D0%BB%D0%B5%D0%BA%D1%81%D0%B5%D0%B9_%D0%90%D0%BD%D0%B0%D1%82%D0%BE%D0%BB%D1%8C%D0%B5%D0%B2%D0%B8%D1%87#:~:text=%5B4%5D.-,%D0%A3%D0%B3%D0%BE%D0%BB%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%BD%D0%BE%D0%B5%20%D0%B4%D0%B5%D0%BB%D0%BE,-%5B%D0%BF%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B2%D0%B8%D1%82%D1%8C%20%7C) that the real reason for his persecution is the Russian state's desire to take control of the management of the [Soviet Union .su domain](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/.su), which was managed by the [Foundation for Internet Development](https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%A4%D0%BE%D0%BD%D0%B4_%D0%A0%D0%B0%D0%B7%D0%B2%D0%B8%D1%82%D0%B8%D1%8F_%D0%98%D0%BD%D1%82%D0%B5%D1%80%D0%BD%D0%B5%D1%82), co-founded by Relcom. His [son Andrei Soldatov claims](https://cepa.org/article/russia-jails-the-father-of-russias-internet/) that Alexej Soldatov is incurably ill and that the sentence is, with great certainty, a death sentence for him.
