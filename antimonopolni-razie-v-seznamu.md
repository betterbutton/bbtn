# Antitrust Authority Raids Seznam
> 

The Office for the Protection of Competition (ÚOHS) has launched a raid on the headquarters of Seznam.cz due to suspicion of anti-competitive behavior and possible abuse of dominant market position.

The raid has been ongoing since yesterday, when officials allegedly seized computers from some employees. Although the initiation of administrative proceedings or imposition of sanctions has not yet been confirmed, Seznam.cz is fully cooperating with the authority. Spokespersons for both ÚOHS and Seznam.cz confirmed the ongoing inspection, but details are not yet public.

The information about the raid was brought by the website [info.cz](https://www.info.cz/zpravodajstvi/cesko/v-sidle-seznamu-probiha-razie-antimonopolniho-uradu-uohs-proveruje-zneuziti-dominantniho-postaveni) and snippets of information are published by [Czech News](https://www.ceskenoviny.cz/zpravy/uohs-zasahuje-v-sidle-seznamcz-proveruje-zneuziti-dominance/2582863) and [iDnes](https://www.idnes.cz/zpravy/domaci/seznam-vyhledavac-dominance-uohs.A241016_093647_domaci_rapc).
