# Squarespace Trains Its AI Tools with an Emphasis on Taste and Customization
> 

In a few months, a human-built website will be something like custom-made shoes. Squarespace has introduced its new [Design Intelligence](https://www.squarespace.com/design-intelligence), an AI tool for website creation that enables the generation of designs, text, and images, with an emphasis on customization and quality.

Product director Paul Gubbay told TechCrunch that [Squarespace's goal is not just technology for technology's sake](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/06/cpo-paul-gubbay-says-squarespace-is-training-its-ai-tools-with-curation-and-taste/), but tools that help users stand out. The company focuses on aesthetics and content curation, using AI to achieve professional results without losing a personal touch. Squarespace believes AI will complement designers' work and simplify the creation process without replacing them.
