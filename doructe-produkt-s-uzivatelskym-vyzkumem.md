# Deliver a quality product with user research
> 

Research is a key factor in successful product development. The development process starts with generating ideas. This is followed by their evaluation and concept testing. After the concept is approved, the actual product development and its pre-launch testing follow. User research applies everywhere here and helps you guide and minimize risk, [writes Yoga Pratama](https://medium.com/@yogaproduct/delivering-product-excellence-with-product-research-f5c4eecbea58).

The post-launch phase plays an important role. Product performance is monitored, and its support and maintenance are ensured. Product management focuses on three main areas:

- product discovery,
- delivery, and
- go-to-market strategy

Together with the product strategy, they determine how the product will fulfill the company's business goals. In the article [Providing Good Product with Product Research](https://medium.com/@yogaproduct/delivering-product-excellence-with-product-research-f5c4eecbea58), you will learn how user research helps you in each of these phases.
