# Fastest Czech e-shops of 2024
> 

The year 2024 brought new results in the [evaluation of Czech e-shop speed](https://pagespeed.cz/blog/nejrychlejsi-e-shop-2024-detaily). The winner was iWant.cz, along with its sister site Smarty.cz, both with exceptionally fast loading and stable visual presentation. Other awarded e-shops are Hornbach.cz and Altisport.cz, which also meet the Core Web Vitals criteria.

[The overall PageSpeed overview was published in Google Sheets.](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13q5kqyVAbalg4Gc-umI1FKLE7sgOjsgdaLrJBHhPotk/edit?gid=0#gid=0)

The research showed that website speed influences both conversions and SEO, and thus business success. Some major Czech e-shops, such as Alza or Rohlík, are lagging behind, which could be an advantage for smaller players who focus on performance optimization.
