# How UX Adapts to Crisis and Why You Need to Know More
> 

A weak economy brings [pressure on UX designers to shift towards product design](https://dataanddesign.substack.com/p/product-design-is-overtaking-ux-and), which connects user experience with business goals and technical requirements. While UX design brings long-term value, during recessions, companies expect immediate results, which often means expanding their role to include skills in product design or data work.

> „UX is Dying, and Product Design is going to replace it.“\
> **— Christopher K Wong**

An alternative is to focus on more complex sectors, where quality UX is still indispensable. Adopting new skills helps UX specialists stand out in the job market, which requires more from them than just traditional skills.
