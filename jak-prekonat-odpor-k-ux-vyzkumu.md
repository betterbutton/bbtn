# How to Overcome Resistance to UX Research
> 

Resistance to UX research is common, especially among stakeholders who are concerned about budget, schedule, and changes. A survey by UXtweak showed that beginning [UX professionals struggle with lack of stakeholder interest](https://blog.uxtweak.com/how-to-deal-with-stakeholders-resistance-to-research/), while experienced ones struggle with time and budget pressure. The main obstacles are resistance to change, financial constraints, and disagreements in methodology.

> “The biggest impact and way to overcome resistance was to get stakeholders to participate in user testing and observe how users struggle.”\
> **— [UXtweak](https://blog.uxtweak.com/how-to-deal-with-stakeholders-resistance-to-research/)**

Recommended strategies include involving stakeholders in research, presenting case studies, ROI analysis, and gradually building trust through smaller research projects. Key is also building relationships and communicating in a language that resonates with stakeholders.
