# Guide to Setting Up First-Party Mode for Google Tag
> 

Google has released [a guide for deploying the Google tag in first-party mode](https://developers.google.com/tag-platform/tag-manager/first-party/setup-guide). This should provide a more resilient tag configuration. First-party mode, which is currently in beta, increases measurement accuracy and restores lost signals in line with privacy principles. You will need to host the tag on your own domain, which gives you additional control over user privacy protection – for example, completely masking IP addresses. The benefits of first-party mode are summarized on Facebook by [Marek Lecián](https://www.facebook.com/marek.lecian/posts/pfbid0YzL9hx1bvjt63rurMqv8L7NS4FVVzaNDcwxwbNX2ho6bRMsoB6r9bu36R9oocZbxl): *"Cookies will last longer, requests will be less blocked."*
