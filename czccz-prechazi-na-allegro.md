# CZC.cz is ending: e-shop moves to Allegro marketplace
> 

Allegro announced [the end of independent operation of the CZC.cz e-shop](https://cc.cz/hotovo-czc-v-soucasne-podobe-jako-samostatny-e-shop-konci-fungovat-bude-uz-jen-na-allegru/), which will from September operate exclusively on the Allegro platform. The reason is long-term unsatisfactory performance of the business and an effort to keep technological costs under control. Allegro plans to retain the CZC brand, its focus on electronics, and also the Czech team for support and warranty services.

The move to the new platform, however, means the loss of some features of the original e-shop. The CZC.cz domain will remain accessible only for handling complaints and warranties. The company has long been [struggling with financial problems](https://www.lupa.cz/aktuality/samostatny-e-shop-czc-cz-na-podzim-skonci-a-presune-se-na-trziste-allegro/), in 2023 it reported a loss of 356 million crowns.

CZC.cz was popular among fans of computing technology because it historically offered a very extensive discussion forum, which often had greater significance than the e-shop itself.
