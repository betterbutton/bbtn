# White House Does Not Plan to Limit Open-Source AI
> 

[White House expressed support for open-source artificial intelligence technology on Tuesday](https://www.wdtn.com/news/business/ap-business/ap-white-house-says-no-need-to-restrict-open-source-artificial-intelligence-at-least-for-now/) and stated that it is not yet necessary to impose restrictions on companies that make key components of their AI systems available. Alan Davidson from the U.S. Department of Commerce emphasized the importance of open systems. [NTIA report](https://www.ntia.gov/other-publication/2024/fact-sheet-ntia-ai-report-calls-monitoring-not-mandating-restrictions-open-ai-models) indicates that existing evidence is not sufficient to limit AI models with "freely available weights", but warns of the need for further monitoring of potential risks.
