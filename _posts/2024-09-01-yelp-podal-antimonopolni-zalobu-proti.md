# Yelp filed an antitrust lawsuit against Google
> 

Yelp is an American business directory with user reviews founded in 2004. [Yelp accuses Google of illegal monopoly abuse](https://blog.yelp.com/news/yelp-v-google/) in the field of general search to dominate the local search and advertising market.

According to Yelp, Google manipulates search results in favor of its own services, thereby limiting competition and worsening the quality of results for users. Yelp decided to act as soon as [Google suffered an antitrust defeat](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/google-nelegalni-monopol-usa), and now seeks remedy and compensation in the legal proceedings.

[The New York Times reports](https://www.nytimes.com/2024/08/28/technology/google-yelp-lawsuit.html) that last year [Yelp had revenue of 1.34 billion dollars](https://www.yelp-press.com/press-releases/press-release-details/2024/Investments-in-Product-Led-Strategy-Drove-Yelps-Strong-2023-Results/), while Alphabet, Google's parent company, [recorded 307 billion dollars](https://abc.xyz/assets/43/44/675b83d7455885c4615d848d52a4/goog-10-k-2023.pdf).
