# Visualbook – your brand's visual identity at your fingertips, whenever you need it
> Visualbook is a platform that transforms static brand manuals into live web documents for speed and consistency.

![Screenshot from the VisualBook.pro tool](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/4e2207af-75b5-45a5-bd27-cbba70c2abc3_993x934.png "430x404")

Brand manual in the [Visualbook](https://visualbook.pro) tool

[Visualbook](https://visualbook.pro/) is a tool that replaces traditional PDF brand manuals with an online version that is always up-to-date. For designers and development teams, this means certainty that everyone is working with the same and valid source. And not just for them – it's useful for anyone who works with the brand's visual identity, from print designers and social media managers to HR and event managers.

The main problem Visualbook solves is the obsolescence of static documents. These often lead to errors and inconsistent use of logos, colors, or typography. Because the manuals are hosted online, organizations can be sure that partners, agencies, and internal teams always see only the latest version. This saves time, reduces confusion, and strengthens brand unity across digital and print channels.

The platform is designed and maintained by [Jiří Chlebus](https://jirichlebus.cz/). His goal is for it to be simple and fast. Thanks to ready-made templates, teams can build a professional brand manual in just a few minutes. Visualbook focuses on key elements – logo rules, color palettes, typography, and usage standards – and eliminates repetitive document formatting tasks. This allows designers to focus on clarity and accuracy.

Compared to foreign alternatives like [Standards](https://standards.site), [Brandpad](https://brandpad.io), [Corebook](https://corebook.io), and others, Jiří chose a one-time payment. For under ten thousand (CZK), a designer can deliver both a new design and access to an online manual to the client, without the designer having to keep paying for the client or the client having to deal with recurring payments. After more than five years in operation, the service has gathered over 150 high-profile clients.
