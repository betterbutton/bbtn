# Design system stifles creativity
> 

Design systems bring clear benefits, including consistency and efficiency, but they can also limit creativity, says Artemis Georgala. They enable consistent user experiences and accelerate development through reusable components. However, there is a risk that [an overly strict system reduces designers' creativity](https://medium.com/workable-design/design-systems-killed-the-creativity-star-221ebbbc32c1), who then assemble ready-made elements instead of innovating.

When implementing a design system, it's crucial to maintain flexibility so that designers can step out of the framework as needed and respond to specific user needs. Development should support creativity through modularity, collaboration, and experimentation.
