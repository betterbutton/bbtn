# KardSort helps with card-sorting
> KardSort is a web tool for card sorting in information architecture. It allows various types of studies and data export for detailed analysis.

![Screenshot of the kardSort.com website.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/16cb176c-e745-4a71-8b1f-c0cb2f5f87cf_1900x1076.png "596x337")

The tool [kardSort.com](https://kartSort.com) helps with IA.

[KardSort](https://kardSort.com) is a free online tool that helps designers and researchers create [card sorting](https://kisk.phil.muni.cz/100metod/trideni-karet-card-sorting) studies. These studies serve to discover how people naturally group information. This is important for the proper organization of content on websites and in applications.

The tool supports open, closed, and combined sorting. It also offers pre- and post-study questionnaires. Results can be downloaded and further processed. Notes can be added to each card and category, which clarifies and refines the research. However, the control is simple and clear. The tool allows data export to formats suitable for further analysis.

Card sorting shows how people think about content. In open sorting, they create their own groups; in closed sorting, they assign items to predefined categories. Hybrid sorting combines both options.
