# EmojiDB for quick search of the right emoji
> A fast, freely available emoji search tool - great for inspiration 

![Screenshot of search results for the phrase "meaning of life" in Emoji DB.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/ece72ba7-a63c-4a32-8bbe-491796358a1c_1903x1078.png "1456x825")

Searching for the meaning of life in [EmojiDB](https://EmojiDB.org).

[EmojiDB](https://EmojiDB.org) is a database that allows you to search, browse, and copy emojis in a matter of seconds. It's fast, clear, and easy to use – ideal when you need to find the right symbol for an interface, prototype, or message.

The power of EmojiDB lies in its simplicity. You enter a term in English and immediately get an overview of suitable emojis, emoticons, and Unicode symbols. Click on a symbol to copy the result to the clipboard and continue working with it as you please.

There are no ads or distracting elements, and results are displayed instantly. It provides a convenient way to test tone and emotion using visual symbols. Emojis can be easily copied into interface designs, texts, or presentation materials.
