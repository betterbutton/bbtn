# 52 cards for creators: Creative warm-ups that inspire
> 

![Mockup of the Creative Warm-ups card set](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/2acbd5a9-825b-4612-a376-bb55f4e0a745_800x640.png "398x318")

Every Monday, Petr Bechyně prepares a Creative Warm-up – a fun activity to get our creative juices flowing. Now he is releasing the most fun ones as a set of [52 cards to support creative thinking](https://www.kreativni-rozcvicky.cz/ and break through creative block.

> **Update 2025-10-03:** Following the success of the first card set, [Creative Warm-ups 2](https://www.kreativni-rozcvicky.cz/#:~:text=K%20objedn%C3%A1vce-,Kreativn%C3%AD%20rozcvi%C4%8Dky%C2%A02,-52%20zbrusu%20nov%C3%BDch) are here. Another 52 challenges to last you through the new year. And if you'd like to gift someone from your English-speaking circle, [both sets are also available in English](https://www.kreativni-rozcvicky.cz/#:~:text=Do%20you%20speak%20English).

Creative Warm-ups are ideal for those seeking new ways to stimulate their imagination, silence their inner critic, and combat routine. The cards are useful for personal use as well as for group activities in workshops, consultations, or to spice up team building.
