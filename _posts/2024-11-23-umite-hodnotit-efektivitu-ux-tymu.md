# Can You Evaluate the Effectiveness of a UX Team?
> 

[Evaluating the Effectiveness of UX Teams](https://medium.com/@prithivkumarr_36680/measuring-ux-team-effectiveness-23ac202c9be6) is a way to demonstrate their value and optimize user experiences. Most commonly, three areas are tracked: user satisfaction (e.g., NPS, CSAT), their engagement (time spent on page, number of pages viewed), and business impacts (conversion rates).

In addition to tracking metrics, it is important to analyze user behavior and draw data-driven decisions from it. The newsletter emphasizes the importance of connecting UX and business goals and shows how measurement can demonstrate the benefit of UX on a specific example.
