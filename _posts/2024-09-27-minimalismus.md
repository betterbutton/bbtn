# Web Minimalism
> 

> "When you create an amazing reading experience, the reader will appreciate it. They will love your content and then decide whether to save it, email it, share it, or subscribe."\
> — **Leo Bababuta**, [minimalist](https://mnmlist.com/w/)

A minimalist website focuses on a [clean reading experience without distractions](https://mnmlist.com/w/). It removes ads, pop-ups, social sharing, comments, and other distracting elements so that the reader can absorb the content in peace.

Leo Bababuta emphasizes that websites should primarily serve readers, not marketing interests. Minimalism enables effective delivery of information and building trust without the nuisance of ads or analytics. When we focus on the reader's needs and create great content, growth and success will come naturally as a side effect.
