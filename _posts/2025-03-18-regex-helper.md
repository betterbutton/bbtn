# RegEx Helper – generator and explainer of regular expressions
> AI tool for generating, testing, and understanding regular expressions. Converts pattern descriptions into corresponding regular expressions with detailed explanations and instant testing capability.

![Screenshot of the RegEx Helper tool](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/22405ffe-cd9b-4163-a570-68fbbcbcf50c_1913x1076.png "1456x819")

RegEx Helper generates a regular expression based on the description

[RegEx Helper](https://regex.fav83.com/) is an AI tool that allows you to easily create, test, and understand regular expressions. Simply describe the pattern in plain language, and the tool will automatically generate the corresponding regular expression. It also includes a detailed explanation of the syntax.

In addition to generation, it also allows immediate testing and editing directly on specific text data. This helps eliminate errors and speed up development.

Regular expressions are a key tool for input data validation, information extraction, or text processing. However, for many developers, they represent a complex concept. RegEx Helper solves this problem by not only generating expressions but also explaining them in an understandable way. Thanks to this, it is suitable both for experienced programmers and for those who are just getting acquainted with regular expressions.
