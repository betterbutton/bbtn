# What do you call a pie chart?
> 

UX researcher Elisa Volpato found that [pie charts have many names in different countries](https://medium.com/@elisavolpato/how-do-you-call-this-chart-a7e495938f53), from *pie chart* to *gráfico de pizza*. After polling colleagues from Europe and South America, she created a meta-chart with names used in multiple languages. It turns out that names often derive from local foods, such as cakes, cheeses, or pizzas.

Elisa reflects on cultural differences in how people understand visualization and labels for similar objects. Ultimately, she agrees that a pie chart is often not the most suitable data visualization tool.

- 🥧 **Pie chart (pie chart):** United States, England, Spain, Italy, Argentina, Romania, Germany, Canada

- 🔴 **Circular chart or circle chart (gráfico circular):** Spain, Poland, Germany

- 🎂 **Cake chart (Tortendiagramm):** Eastern Europe, Venezuela, Germany

- 🧀 **Cheese chart (camembert):** Spain and France (specifically “Camembert diagram”).

- **🛞 Wheel chart (wykres kołowy):** Poland

- 🍕 **Pizza chart (gráfico de pizza):** Brazil
