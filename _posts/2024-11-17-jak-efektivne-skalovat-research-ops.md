# 

Kate Towsey | Scaling Research Ops | #47

## 

·UX Research Geeks

Kate Towsey, an expert on ResearchOps, in the UX Tweak podcast with host Tina Ličková presents her approach to scaling research and building infrastructure for effective team functioning.

Kate discusses her new book [Research That Scales](https://katetowsey.com/book), which reflects not only growth but also sustainable development of research processes. She compares ResearchOps to urban planning, emphasizes the need for prioritization and creating strategies tailored to team size. For both teams and individuals, she recommends focusing on solving specific challenges and drawing inspiration from areas such as librarianship, systems thinking, or pedagogy.
