# ORCA: The Third Diamond in UX Design
> 
> "ORCA is a 15-step process that ensures clarity, structure, and everything needed for confident interaction design."

[ORCA presents a new approach to UX design](https://www.ooux.com/resources/introducing-orca-the-third-diamond-in-your-ux-process), which adds a third diamond between research and design. This process structures complex problems using four pillars: Objects, Relationships, Calls to Action, and Attributes. In four iterative steps – Discovery, Requirements, Prioritization, and Representation – [ORCA helps designers better understand user models](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/introducing-orca-the-third-diamond-in-your-ux-process-23a1babb0389), evaluate business goals, and create an intuitive user environment. ORCA offers a flexible and enjoyable method that eliminates blind spots in design and prepares a solid foundation for subsequent work.
