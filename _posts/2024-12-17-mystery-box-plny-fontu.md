# Mystery Box Full of Fonts
> 

The Czech type foundry Renegade Fonts, run by designers Jan Charvát, Zuzana Konečná, and Tereza Turková, offers a unique collection of fonts for creative projects. For 99 euros, you can [purchase the complete collection](https://www.renegadefonts.com/font_box.php) or [try your luck with the Mystery Box](https://www.renegadefonts.com/font_box.php) for 29 euros. The Mystery Box contains a random font from the offer.

([The Font Box](https://www.renegadefonts.com/font_box.php) in the teaser comes from the Renegate Fonts website.)
