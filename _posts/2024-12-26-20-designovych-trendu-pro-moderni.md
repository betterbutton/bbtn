# 20 design trends for modern websites in 2025
> 

According to Mark Walker-Ford, web design in 2025 will bring [20 trends that will highlight user-friendliness, interactivity, and sustainability](https://red-website-design.co.uk/web-design-trends-2025/). Among the key ones are dynamic cursors, brutal design, natural motifs, AI personalization, 3D experiences, and dark mode. Designers will focus on minimalism, bold typography, mobile optimization, and micro-interactions. Sustainable and accessible websites will play an increasingly important role. Small business owners can leverage these innovations to strengthen their brand and create a unique online experience. The trends reflect the evolution of technology and user expectations, emphasizing functionality and aesthetics.
