# ChatGPT Introduces Canvas: More Effective Writing and Coding
> 

ChatGPT presents [Canvas](https://openai.com/index/introducing-canvas/), a new tool designed for users working on writing and coding who need more than just a chat. Canvas opens possibilities for editing, adding feedback, and targeted revisions of text or code. It enables direct collaboration, where the user controls the results and can easily track the changes made.

This new feature is now available for ChatGPT Plus and Team users, with expansion to Enterprise and Edu accounts expected within a week. Its full rollout for the free version is anticipated gradually.

Competitor Anthropic has also released Artifacts for non-paying users. A very similar product for writing texts and programming code.
