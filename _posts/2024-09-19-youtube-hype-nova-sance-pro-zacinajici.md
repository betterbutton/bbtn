# YouTube Hype: A New Chance for Budding Creators
> 

> „The first original and humanistic feature of YouTube (and probably Google) in many years👏“\
> **— [Tomáš Kafka](https://x.com/tomaskafka/status/1836499355123224684)**

[YouTube has introduced the Hype feature](https://blog.youtube/news-and-events/youtube-hype/), which gives users the ability to support smaller creators. Each user gets three hypes per week, which they can use to promote videos with less than half a million subscribers and younger than one week.

> „In just the first four weeks of our beta tests in Turkey, Taiwan, and Brazil, users hyped over five million times across more than 50,000 unique channels.“\
> **— YT Team**

More hypes increase the chance of a video making it to a special leaderboard. The goal is to return the internet to a time when it was creative and unpredictable, and to give small creators the opportunity to shine. Testing is currently underway in Turkey, Taiwan, and Brazil with great success.
