# Google now crawls websites only with smartphone bots
> 

Rumors are circulating that Google has stopped indexing non-responsive websites. In reality, [Google announced](https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2024/06/mobile-indexing-vlast-final-final.doc?hl=en) that it will no longer crawl websites separately with a mobile and desktop bot, but in line with the mobile-first approach will index all websites with its smartphone crawler. As [Zdeněk Dvořák](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7221826359562498049/) noted on Linkedin: *„Google is just saving its resources and simplifying its work. It no longer needs a Desktop Crawler.“*
