# Microsoft claims Edge is sufficient
> 

[Microsoft is now pushing back against the label that Edge is merely for downloading other browsers.](https://www.zive.cz/clanky/edge-je-dobry-tak-akorat-na-stazeni-chromu-microsoft-se-proti-tomu-zacal-branit/sc-3-a-231149/default.aspx) When you search for alternatives like Chrome or Vivaldi in the integrated Bing, a banner appears claiming that Edge is sufficiently good. Microsoft limits this promotion to specific browsers, while ignoring others like Firefox or Brave.

This exclusive ad format recalls Google's earlier tactics when launching Chrome. Microsoft thus emphasizes that Edge can compete, even though its share is far from Chrome's strength on desktops.
