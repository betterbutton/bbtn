# 12 metrics that help you understand users
> 

A list of [12 UX metrics offers a comprehensive view](https://medium.com/@jhoncamiloux/as-12-principais-m%C3%A9tricas-de-ux-para-avaliar-e-melhorar-a-experi%C3%AAncia-do-usu%C3%A1rio-1d87208f133f) of tools that evaluate user experience. For example, SUPR-Q focuses on trustworthiness and usability, SUS measures ease of use, and NPS assesses customer loyalty. Other metrics like SEQ or NASA-TLX examine specific aspects, such as task difficulty or cognitive load. These methods are invaluable for developers and companies—from SaaS platforms to e-commerce—in identifying and prioritizing improvements that have the greatest benefit for users.
