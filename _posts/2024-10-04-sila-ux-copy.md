# The Power of UX Copy: How Small Words Change the User Experience
> 

Writing text for user interfaces means guiding users effectively with clear, concise, and empathetic messages. The article [Crafting Words That Guide](https://creatogarm.medium.com/crafting-words-that-guide-our-thought-process-behind-ux-writing-challenges-c0ed8ca06bc0) describes several obstacles encountered when creating UX text, including crisis alerts, help with recovering lost work, or solving payment issues.

Each of these situations requires a different approach, but the common denominator is simplicity and empathy. Well-written UX copy builds trust and makes the user's journey easier at various moments of their online experience.
