# French court blocked pornographic websites for insufficient age verification
> 

Parisian [court ordered the blocking of some pornographic websites](https://www.euronews.com/next/2024/10/17/foreign-porn-websites-blocked-in-france-for-lack-of-age-control-checks) for insufficient age verification of visitors, to protect minors. Websites have 15 days to implement an effective age verification system.

This step follows broader European efforts to increase online child safety, which include labeling four pornographic websites as particularly risky. Similar interventions have recently affected other platforms, [including Telegram](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/telegram-da-uradum-data-uzivatelu), whose founder [Pavel Durov faces charges in France](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/sef-telegramu-durov-zadrzen-ve-francii) related to child pornography.
