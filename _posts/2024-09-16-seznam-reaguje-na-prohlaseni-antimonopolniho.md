# Seznam reacts to the antitrust office statement
> 

Seznam.cz [sharply criticizes](https://blog.seznam.cz/2024/09/stanovisko-spolecnosti-seznam-cz/) the Office for the Protection of Competition [for public accusations](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/antimonopolni-urad-proveruje-seznamcz). It is reacting to [the antitrust office's statement](https://uohs.gov.cz/cs/informacni-centrum/tiskove-zpravy/verejne-zakazky/3962-uvadime-na-pravou-miru-seznam-zpravy-sef-antimonopolniho-uradu-spolupracuje-s-obzalovanym-expertem-na-losovacky.html), which accuses Seznam Zprávy of attempting to intimidate the office due to its inspection activities against Seznam.cz.

> "We consider it unacceptable for a public authority, without any evidence, to publicly accuse another entity of revenge, media abuse, and creating pressure..."\
> **— [Seznam.cz](https://blog.seznam.cz/2024/09/stanovisko-spolecnosti-seznam-cz/)**

Seznam.cz emphasizes that the editorial team of Seznam Zprávy is independent and not subject to its management. The company considers the office's statement to be intimidation and a violation of the principles of impartiality and legality. Seznam.cz is prepared to defend its name through legal means.
