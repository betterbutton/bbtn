In Czech e-shops, the shop window model prevails, where the customer has to do most of the work themselves, says [in the CX Space podcast Lukáš Pítra](https://www.proofreason.com/blog/lukas-pitra-o-stavu-cx-v-ceskych-firmach). Access to customer experience (CX) is often missing or ineffective, and CX has a fundamental impact on stabilizing and growing businesses.

Solving customer journey problems should, according to Lukáš, start with identifying pain points and prioritizing based on data that can come from various sources, including customer feedback. Companies should systematically analyze customer expectations and service reality, with open communication and willingness to involve all team levels being key. Stable CX improves decision-making processes and business resilience.
