# More than 100 resources for content professionals
> 

Content design is often perceived as mere content decoration or a technical role in publishing. In reality, it is a key skill for effectively reaching users.

Unlike UX writers who focus on text editing, content designers take a broader perspective: they explore the language users communicate with and create content that meets their needs. Their work includes not only texts but also formats such as videos, audio, or posters. [Well-designed content](https://uxwritinghub.com/best-books-for-content-designers/) speeds up understanding and minimizes the room for misinterpretations.

[Plain English Club](https://www.plainenglish.club/bookmarks/) maintains a [collection of quality resources in plain English](https://raindrop.io/plainenglishclub) in a well-organized bookmark directory. In the library, you will find over a hundred categorized and tagged resources that are expanded every week.
