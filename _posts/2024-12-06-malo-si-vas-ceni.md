# Undervalued?
> 

Many designers feel their work lacks sufficient impact and recognition. The problem often lies in companies not understanding how design contributes to the business. Greater influence comes from highlighting design's benefit in changing user behavior, attitudes, and emotions, which leads to concrete results like higher conversion or retention.

[Ryan Scott shares a story about DoorDash](https://www.dive.club/ideas/you-re-not-getting-enough-credit-for-your-design-work) showing that even a seemingly unsuccessful project can be turned into a success if we correctly explain its value. Designers should be able to communicate their contribution better in a language leadership understands.
