# Dopamine loop controls our brains
> 

Modern UX design uses dopamine loops to keep us engaged longer, often without our awareness. These loops build on variable rewards, infinite feeds to stimulate our brains similarly to gambling. Thanks to elements like feedback, FOMO, and tactile stimuli, the user experience becomes almost addictive. The key is conscious use of these principles – ethically they can enrich the experience, but misuse leads to addiction and burnout. UX designers have the opportunity to create responsible products that contribute to authentic connection and meaningful interaction.
