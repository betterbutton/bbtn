# Passkeys – Promising Technology or Missed Opportunity?
> 

[Passkeys](https://www.irozhlas.cz/veda-technologie/technologie/passkeys-prihlasovani-bez-hesla-kyberneticka-bezpecnost_2305061503_ara) were meant to replace passwords. Originally a promising idea that was supposed to use biometrics to replace the need to create usernames and passwords for individual services and applications. William Brown [is disillusioned with implementing this idea](https://fy.blackhats.net.au/blog/2024-04-26-passkeys-a-shattered-dream/). According to him, passkeys struggle with issues like key deletion, poor user experience, and restrictions imposed by large tech companies.

The author criticizes the effort to lock users into specific platforms. He concludes that passkeys will likely fail for average users and recommends using a password manager. For corporate environments, he still sees potential in verified security keys but is considering alternatives like device certificates and smart cards.

Unlike William Brown, we are enthusiastic about the rollout of passkeys and believe these growing pains will be overcome.
