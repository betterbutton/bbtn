# Canvas as the key to more effective collaboration
> 

Hermen Lutje Berenbroek is, as they say, a visual thinker. He believes in the power of properly visualized thoughts. His [blog is full of examples and case studies](https://www.artifizer.com/blog) of using visual schemas for better communication and collaboration.

Hermen is convinced that creating visual canvases – canvases – tailored to the audience's needs can significantly improve team efficiency. The key is not in designing the result itself, but in creating a framework that facilitates the path to the goal. A well-designed canvas structures discussions, directs thinking, and keeps the team on the right path. It is a tool that allows focusing on important things and improving collaboration.
