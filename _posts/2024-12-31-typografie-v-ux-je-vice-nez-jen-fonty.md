# Typography in UX is more than just fonts
> 

Typography in UX design is more than just font selection – it is a crucial tool for improving readability, creating visual hierarchy, and connecting the brand with the user. Good [typography not only facilitates navigation and content comprehension](http://Typografie%20je%20v%20UX%20designu%20více%20než%20jen%20výběr%20fontů%20–%20je%20to%20zásadní%20nástroj%20pro%20zlepšení%20čitelnosti,%20vytváření%20vizuální%20hierarchie%20a%20propojení%20značky%20s%20uživatelem.%20Dobrá%20typografie%20nejenže%20usnadňuje%20orientaci%20a%20srozumitelnost%20obsahu,%20ale%20také%20vyvolává%20emoce%20a%20nastavuje%20tón%20celkového%20zážitku.%20Důležitost%20typografie%20tkví%20i%20v%20její%20schopnosti%20zajistit%20přístupnost%20pro%20všechny%20uživatele.%20Konzistence,%20testování%20a%20respektování%20pravidel%20inkluzivity%20zajišťují,%20že%20design%20působí%20profesionálně%20a%20je%20funkční%20na%20všech%20zařízeních.), but also evokes emotions and sets the tone for the overall experience. The importance of typography also lies in its ability to ensure accessibility for all users. Consistency, testing, and respecting inclusivity rules ensure that the design looks professional and functions well on all devices.
