# Design at Speed: How Startups Handle Pressure
> 

The work of designers in startups requires a fast pace and adaptability. Startups often change their vision, which emphasizes clear priorities and planning. The key is to focus on the main product functions and ensure their smooth operation with quality UX and UI. Avoiding feature overload is crucial for gaining user loyalty. Success lies in keeping promises and providing value that inspires users and enables growth. The design team, according to Anastasia Limonova, must [balance between ambition and pragmatism](https://medium.com/@limonova/design-fast-improve-slowly-magic-ffformula-for-startup-design-teams-3d6d43bbde80) to create a product that captivates and retains an audience.
