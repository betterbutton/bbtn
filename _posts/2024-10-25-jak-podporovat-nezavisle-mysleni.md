# How to Support Independent Thinking in a Team
> 

Groupthink is a situation where a group prioritizes consensus and harmony over critical thinking and independent decision-making. In work teams, especially in UX design, [groupthink can stifle innovation](https://uxdesign.cc/avoiding-groupthink-at-work-6bd820c3dd40) and lead to decisions that do not reflect the actual needs of users.

> „Groupthink can stifle innovation, leading to suboptimal design choices that fail to meet user needs.“\
> — **[Victor Yocco](https://uxdesign.cc/avoiding-groupthink-at-work-6bd820c3dd40)**

To prevent this, it is essential to promote diversity of opinions and create an environment where people feel safe to express their views, even when they differ from the majority.
