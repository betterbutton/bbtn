# Document stakeholder interviews effectively
> 

Stakeholders are individuals or groups essential for project success, from management to field employees. Divyanshu Tomar [explains in his guide](https://medium.com/@divyanshutomar78/documenting-stakeholder-interviews-a-comprehensive-guide-cf283bd9a397), how to identify and engage stakeholders, when to end information gathering, and what interview documentation should include.

The process includes project goals, success metrics, scope, and completion criteria. Proper documentation not only supports collaboration and trust but also facilitates achieving measurable results. Thanks to careful planning, conflicts can be prevented and the project can be ensured to align with the organization's broader goals.
