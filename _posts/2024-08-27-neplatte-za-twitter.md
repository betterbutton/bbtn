# Dočekal: Don't Pay for Twitter
> 

Daniel Dočekal [summarizes his experiences with a paid account on Twitter](https://365tipu.cz/2024/08/26/tip2788-neplatte-za-ucet-na-twitteru-prinos-je-mizivy-ale-pojdme-to-trochu-probrat/) and leaves no stone unturned.

> „Musk wants you to pay for ads, so he has no reason to show you。“\
> **— [Daniel Dočekal](https://365tipu.cz/2024/08/26/tip2788-neplatte-za-ucet-na-twitteru-prinos-je-mizivy-ale-pojdme-to-trochu-probrat/)**

Dan directly says that paying for an account won't bring you better post distribution on Twitter and proves it with a declining readership graph from this social network's statistics. He complains that serious posts with links outside Twitter are suppressed. He gradually breaks down all the benefits of a paid account and is skeptical about all of them.

Jakub (Premium) and Martin (Basic) have paid accounts on Twitter. And what is their experience with it? *„The ability to write longer posts is nice, but Twitter doesn't display them in full. I don't use the other benefits. I still like Twitter the most among all social networks and I'm happy to support it with a subscription，“* Martin shares his experience.
