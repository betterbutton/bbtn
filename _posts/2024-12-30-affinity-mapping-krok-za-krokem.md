# Affinity mapping step by step
> 

Harsh Arbooj describes the redesign process of the analytical section of the Recobee platform, focused on film enthusiasts. The existing features did not meet user expectations due to low personalization and generic overviews. [Harsh used affinity mapping](https://medium.com/@harsh.designsuiux/mastering-affinity-mapping-a-ux-designers-guide-to-structuring-information-architecture-db6f6b7b9e3b) in a workshop to solve information architecture problems without direct user involvement. The process included UX research, competitive analysis, and discussions with stakeholders. The workshop helped reduce bias and create new designs based on a consensual understanding of user needs.
