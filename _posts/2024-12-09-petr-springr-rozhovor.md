# How Petr Špringr transitioned from marketing to user research
> 

Petr Špringr is a UX researcher at Česká spořitelna. With his colleague Tomáš Kamenc, he talked about his journey from marketing research to digital and UX research. The semester is ending, and this is the period when KISK students submit their interviews and articles on Medium. [Tomáš's interview with Petr](https://medium.com/design-kisk/petr-%C5%A1pringr-cesta-od-marketingov%C3%A9ho-v%C3%BDzkumu-k-digit%C3%A1lu-a-ux-30ffee443a79) is the first in this winter semester.

Petr sees the main benefit of his career in a holistic approach to problem-solving. He presents the differences between working in an agency and in-house, where he emphasizes the possibility of deeper implementation of findings. Petr describes the challenges in engaging stakeholders, effectively sharing insights, and using AI. For the future of UX research, he proposes dynamic video formats for sharing results. He advises students not to be afraid to work with users and to develop the ability to understand their needs.
