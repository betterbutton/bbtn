# Custom Picture Fonts
> 

![Sample of letters generated in a theme based on the prompt](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/60640cb3-fb30-4c49-9963-358716120261_1243x916.png "530x390")

GenType

Google Labs is a space full of interesting experiments. One of them is [GenType – a tool for generating picture fonts](https://labs.google/gentype based on a text prompt. How about a font made of pumpkins, ladybugs, or silver tubes? But why not, there are no limits to imagination. Just write the prompt and wait a moment, and within an instant you get 26 generated images with the theme based on the prompt shaped as uppercase letters of the Latin alphabet. Punctuation, numbers, and diacritics are missing, but even with just the basic Latin alphabet letters you can create a lot of interesting creations.
