# Web Disability Simulator lets you experience the web in the skin of disabled users
> A Chrome and Edge browser extension that shows how people with various disabilities perceive the web – and why accessibility matters.

![Screenshot of a webpage with the simulator panel](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/c5285101-8fa7-4d41-a736-542022cf73c6_1902x1079.png "628x356")

[Web Disability Simulator](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/web-disability-simulator/olioanlbgbpmdlgjnnampnnlohigkjla?hl=cs) activates accessibility barrier simulations in your browser.

[Web Disability Simulator](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/web-disability-simulator/olioanlbgbpmdlgjnnampnnlohigkjla?hl=cs) is an extension for Google Chrome and browsers based on it. It allows you to try out how people with disabilities use websites. By simulating various types of disabilities – for example color blindness, tunnel vision, hand tremors, dyslexia, or concentration difficulties – it helps uncover and understand problems that can hinder accessibility.

The extension doesn't stop at simulation. It also offers short and understandable explanations of individual disorders, practical advice on how to make the web more accessible, and links to further resources where you can learn more about digital accessibility.

You can test how a website behaves with visual impairments, in very bright light, with motor difficulties, or when having trouble understanding text. By simply switching between modes, you can verify whether your website is understandable and usable even for users with limitations.

The goal of the extension is to raise awareness about accessibility and bring the reality closer to people who use the web differently than most. It's not just about meeting standards, but about genuine understanding and better digital services for everyone.

You can install the [Web Disability Simulator](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/web-disability-simulator/olioanlbgbpmdlgjnnampnnlohigkjla?hl=cs) extension for free from the Chrome Web Store and start discovering all the things that can affect the usability of your website.
