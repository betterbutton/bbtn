# 60fps – gallery of micro-interactions and effects
> A platform that preserves and presents the most interesting details of motion and interaction in digital design.

![Screenshot from 60fps.design website](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/47865fee-9941-495c-8823-3e8c93c881bc_1900x1079.png "620x352")

[60fps.design](https://60fps.design/) is a gallery of effects and micro-interactions

[60fps](https://60fps.design/) is a carefully curated archive that celebrates motion, details, and emotion in digital design. It was created to capture and showcase those thoughtful moments from applications – subtle transitions, animations, and gestures that humanize the digital world.

The project started in 2022 as a collection of short clips featuring product features. The creator was driven by a simple frustration – once the promotion for a new product ended, the promotional spots disappeared and could not be found anywhere. From that need emerged a space where such creativity is preserved and remains accessible to all designers and developers.

However, it soon became clear that the same problem existed within app design itself. The most interesting details often go unnoticed or get lost in the pressure to release new versions quickly. 60fps therefore expanded its scope – it highlights what makes digital experiences exceptional.

At its core, 60fps is a source of inspiration. It helps rediscover the magic of craftsmanship that gives interfaces their soul. By preserving these moments, 60fps reminds us that design is not just about how things look, but also about how they move and feel. It is a place that invites observation, discovery, and a return to the joy of meticulous work on details.
