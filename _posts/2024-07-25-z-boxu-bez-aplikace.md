# Z-Boxes No Longer Require the Packeta App
> 

Picking up packages from Zásilkovna boxes was a pain because you needed their app for that. So your partner on the way from work or your kids on the way from school couldn't easily pick up packages for you. That has changed, [writes Michal Dragan on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7219662129526681601/)! Z-boxes now have a keyboard and display, so you only need to enter a code to open them. Thus, other companies besides Packeta can now deliver to Z-boxes, revitalizing competition in the market.
