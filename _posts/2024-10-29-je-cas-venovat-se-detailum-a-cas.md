# It's Time to Focus on Details and Time to Let Them Be
> 

We often hear about the importance of details, but we forget about proper timing, [reminds Jason Fried](https://x.com/jasonfried/status/1848404994342694926). Details can bring beauty and precision, but they can also cause delays and stagnation if we focus on them too early.

> “How many times have you realized that the progress you made today wasn't real progress?”\
> — **[Jason Fried](https://x.com/jasonfried/status/1848404994342694926)**

We often lose a day on trifles without achieving real progress. The key is to focus on functionality and basic elements at the beginning of the process, and only then, as the project takes shape, does the time come for fine-tuning and precision.
