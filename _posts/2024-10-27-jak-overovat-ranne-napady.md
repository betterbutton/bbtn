# How to validate early ideas 
> 

In UX design, it is crucial to evaluate early ideas through an iterative approach that combines divergent and convergent thinking, [recalls Silas Silikhe](https://silikhesilas.medium.com/evaluating-early-ideas-in-ux-design-f1245d63ce3d).

> „Testing in the early phase allows you to quickly verify if your idea resonates with users. Early feedback is key to preventing costly mistakes later.“\
> — **[Silas Silikhe](https://silikhesilas.medium.com/evaluating-early-ideas-in-ux-design-f1245d63ce3d)**

The divergent phase supports unrestricted idea generation, while convergent thinking narrows these ideas down to viable concepts. Proper understanding of the problem is the foundation for effective idea evaluation, which must meet criteria of viability, desirability, and feasibility.

Testing with rough prototypes helps uncover errors and allows for quick adjustments without high costs. Success in UX design lies in continuous refinement and iteration.
