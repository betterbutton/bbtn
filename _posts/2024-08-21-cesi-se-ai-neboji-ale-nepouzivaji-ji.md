# In the Czech Republic, we are not afraid of AI, but we don't use it
> 

[ITbiz writes about the survey](https://www.itbiz.cz/zpravicky/pruzkum-k-umele-inteligenci-ma-negativni-postoj-36-procent-cechu/) NMS Market Research for Raiffeisenbank has 36% of Czechs with a negative attitude toward AI, with trust in AI decreasing with age. Only six percent of people use AI regularly, mainly the younger generation. A positive attitude toward AI is more often held by men and people with higher education. In banking, where AI is becoming more common, 36% of respondents do not believe in its safety. Czechs would most often use AI for spam filtering (57%), but they do not prefer it for investment decisions or financial advice.
