# What works and what spoils the experience in Netflix UX
> 

Purity Udeh analyzes [good and bad user experiences on Netflix](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/the-good-and-bad-ux-practices-by-netflix-b0b1cffdd66b). Among good practices are the ability to protect profiles with a password, the 'Skip Intro' feature, and a simple user interface that makes navigation easier for users.

The bad side of Netflix UX is password sharing without sufficient protection, lack of notifications on new logins, inability to access via mobile browser, and automatic deletion of accounts after prolonged inactivity. Netflix could better protect user privacy and improve inclusivity for all device types.
