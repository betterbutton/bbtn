# User Task Canvas helps understand users
> 

![User Task Canvas](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/5e97ca77-7003-46d5-b0ae-45d3c529a541_1400x718.png "1400x718")

[User Task Canvas](https://www.creativejuiz.fr/blog/en/resources-tools/user-task-canvas-collect-users-activities-tasks-needs) is a simple and practical tool for gathering user needs. Authors Geoffrey Crofte and Stéphanie Walter developed cards that help map user tasks, decisions, tools, interactions, and problems. The process includes a workshop with cards and the option to use an adapted guide for individual interviews.

This methodology has proven effective in gaining insights in an agile environment. The cards are freely available for download under the CC SA-BY 4.0 license, which supports modifications and sharing while adhering to attribution conditions. Thanks to its simplicity and flexibility, this tool has the potential to bring useful results in other areas as well.
