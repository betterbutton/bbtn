# A game for checkbox lovers. A million checkboxes!
> 

Who among us has lived to such a ripe old age that they remember the (and commercially successful!) internet phenomenon [Million Dollar Homepage](http://www.milliondollarhomepage.com/)? Well, Nolen spends his time on similar tomfoolery and has created a similar page: [One Million Checkboxes](https://onemillioncheckboxes.com/). It's basically a social network for those who love checkboxes!

Nolen's blog is full of similar games. If you spent your youth breaking brick walls in arcade games like Arkanoid or Breakout, today as adults, instead of bricks, you can [demolish meetings in your Google Calendar](https://eieio.substack.com/p/breaktime), play [Flappy Bird inside Finder](https://eieio.games/nonsense/game-11-flappy-bird-finder/) on macOS, or measure strength against a random stranger over the phone in a game of [rock, paper, scissors](https://eieio.substack.com/p/talk-paper-scissors).
