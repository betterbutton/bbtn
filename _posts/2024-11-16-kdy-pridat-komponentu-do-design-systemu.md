# When to add a component to the design system
> 

Adding a new component to the design system requires careful consideration of its purpose, alignment with design principles, and technical feasibility. We must consider its necessity, flexibility, usability, and impact on system users. It is essential to ensure that the new element is consistent with the visual language and accessible to all users, including those with assistive technologies. Documentation is also important to facilitate implementation and maintenance. The entire process should support system development without unnecessary complexity or overlaps with existing elements.

Luis Ouriach compiled a [useful decision tree](https://www.figma.com/community/file/989121626983620108/new-component-decision-tree) that guides us in adding new components to the design system.
