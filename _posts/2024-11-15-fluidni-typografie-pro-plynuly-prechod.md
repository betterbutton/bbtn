# Fluid Typography for a Smooth Transition Between Different Screen Widths
> 

Fluid typography allows you to design [a unified design for different screen sizes](https://clagnut.com/blog/2428/) without using media queries. The system uses the principles of Utopia: it defines sizes and spacing for the smallest and largest screen, with the rest automatically adjusting. A key role is played by the flexible setting of text and space scales – for example, text on a smaller screen starts at 17px, on a larger one ends at 20px. Utopian typography thus minimizes design debt and allows for simpler development and a consistent experience across devices.
