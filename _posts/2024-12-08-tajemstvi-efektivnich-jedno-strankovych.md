# The Secret of Effective One-Page Websites
> 

One-page websites, or [one-pagers](https://medium.com/@adhithyaux/one-pager-websites-because-nobodys-got-time-for-a-novel-b8d1c8b8f635), are designed to quickly grab attention and guide visitors toward a clear action. For conversion-focused websites, brevity is key—too much information discourages clicks. Conversely, informational one-pagers can tell a story, but even here, less is more.

Clients often want to cram everything onto one page, but professional creators know that clarity and focus on the goal are essential. Success lies in simplicity, whether for sales or sharing key information. [A well-crafted one-pager is like a good joke](https://medium.com/@adhithyaux/one-pager-websites-because-nobodys-got-time-for-a-novel-b8d1c8b8f635)—quick, punchy, and memorable.
