# 21 strategies to retain users
> 

The article by Taras Baukusevych presents [21 UX strategies that help increase user engagement without exploitation](https://uxdesign.cc/21-ux-strategies-to-maximize-user-engagement-without-exploitation-a39428cd66c5). It mentions popular behavioral models like the Hook Model or Habit Loop and explains how to use motivation, triggers, and rewards to maintain interest.

> “Trigger, Action, Variable Reward, and Investment... how to build habit loops by leveraging rewards and user investment.”\
> — **[Taras Baukusevych](https://uxdesign.cc/21-ux-strategies-to-maximize-user-engagement-without-exploitation-a39428cd66c5)**

The focus is on an ethical approach that ensures apps do not lead to unhealthy habits like doomscrolling. Strategies include personalized notifications, boosting intrinsic motivation, and algorithm transparency, which helps maintain long-term engagement without negative consequences.
