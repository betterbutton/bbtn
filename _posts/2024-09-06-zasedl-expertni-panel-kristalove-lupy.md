# Crystal Magnifying Glass 2024 to Award Influencers for the First Time
> 

![Folder with materials for the decision-making of the Crystal Magnifying Glass expert panel members](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/11168251-3a82-467f-a386-fddbf205d2c6_1240x1240.jpeg "1240x1240")

Which projects and personalities will appear on the Crystal Magnifying Glass 2024 shortlists?

Shortly after the nomination phase ended, during which the public could propose projects and personalities for the [Crystal Magnifying Glass 2024 award](https://kristalova.lupa.cz/2024/), the expert panel met yesterday at the Louvre gallery to compile shortlists for public voting and the professional jury.

One of the expert panel members, [Adam Zbiejczuk, reflects on this year's edition](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7237785387618770945/). This year features a new Influencer category, in which Adam played a more active role than in others. Nominations in individual editions reflect the changes the Czech internet has undergone over the past nearly 20 years. Adam gives a few hints that the favorites of this year's edition are clear in several categories.

The internet public will decide on the rankings in these categories:

-  Online Commerce
-  Tools and Services
-  One (wo)man show
-  Online Video
-  Podcast of the Year
-  Interest Websites
-  News and Journalism
-  Influencer

The professional jury will decide on the rankings in these categories:

- Project of the Year
- Personality of the Year
- Global Projects by Czech Creators
- Young Blood
- E-commerce Inspiration
- Marketing and Content Inspiration
- Public Service – Czech Television Award
- Antiaward
- Star

Whatever the case, the expert panel only prepares the shortlists. The winners will be decided by public voting and, in selected categories, by the professional jury.
