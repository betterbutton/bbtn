# 365 Tips for Better Sales Copy
> 

Michaela Weikertová compiled and offers the [e-book 365 copy tricks](https://michaelaweikertova.cz/know-how/copy-triky/) for free.

> "I believe that the more good texts you read, the sooner you'll start trying them yourself."\
> **— [Michaela Weikertová](https://michaelaweikertova.cz/know-how/copy-triky/)**

It's a practical guide for anyone who wants to improve their writing. It contains hundreds of examples of headlines, labels, and buttons that Michaela collected daily over the course of a year. Instead of theory, it offers micro-examples for immediate use.
