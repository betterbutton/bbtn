# StoryTribe: Create storyboards without unnecessary hassle
> A free online storyboard tool that requires no drawing skills – for video creators, UX teams, and marketers.

![StoryTribe interface demo.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/56227090-4cae-471c-a15c-4f57718d4408_1440x900.png "636x397")

With [StoryTribe.com](https://storytribeapp.com/), even the most drawing-inept can create a storyboard.

[StoryTribe.com](https://storytribeapp.com/) is an online storyboard tool that lets you visualize scenarios without needing drawing talent. It's designed for video creators, marketers, and UX designers, allowing you to quickly and clearly translate ideas into visual form—right in your browser.

You can customize characters, add props and backgrounds, and thanks to the new freehand drawing feature, add a personal touch to your storyboard. The interface is simple and friendly, so even beginners can get started in minutes. Nothing needs to be installed, and sharing is as easy as sending a link.

StoryTribe's uniqueness lies in its emphasis on inclusion and flexibility. The library contains a wide range of characters of different cultures, ethnicities, and body types. The drawings are high-quality enough for client presentations, teaching, or product workshops.

New features include object locking, which helps prevent unintended edits. StoryTribe supports export to common formats. Thus, StoryTribe helps both professionals and beginners express their ideas quickly, simply, and with a clear visual style.
