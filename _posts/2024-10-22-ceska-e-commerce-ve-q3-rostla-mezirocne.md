# Czech e-commerce in Q3 grew year-on-year by 5 percent
> 

Czech e-commerce recorded year-on-year growth of 5% in the third quarter of 2024 [year-on-year growth of 5%](https://heureka.group/cz-cs/o-nas/tiskove-centrum/tiskove-zpravy/heureka-ecommerce-insider-optimisticky-rust-ceske-e-commerce-ve-tretim-kvartalu-roku-2024/), despite a seasonal decline during the summer months – this follows from statistics published by the comparator Heureka. The September recovery was supported by discount campaigns. Demand for solar panels and air conditioners increased significantly. On the other hand, consumer electronics, especially gaming consoles and smartwatches, declined. The average order value fell to 1389 CZK.

![E-commerce insider Q3 2024 +5% year-on-year development of e-commerce turnover for Q3 95.99% is customer satisfaction with e-shops 1398 CZK is the average order value +462% Quarter's top performer: Solar and photovoltaics -48% Quarter's worst performer: Gaming consoles source: Heureka Group](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/fb9d0bf2-5f89-4a46-a6df-d3af50f3fab2_3840x2160.jpeg "1456x819")

Customer satisfaction with e-shops remains stably high. Specific circumstances, such as weather and government subsidies, which stimulated growth in certain categories, had a significant influence on consumer behavior.
