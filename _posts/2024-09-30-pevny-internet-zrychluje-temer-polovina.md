# Fixed internet is accelerating, nearly half have 100 Mbit/s
> 

According to the new [ČTÚ report for 2023](https://ctu.gov.cz/tiskova-zprava-internet-stale-hlavne-pres-wi-fi-skoro-polovina-pristupu-uz-ma-rychlost-pres-100), the volume of data transferred over fixed and mobile internet is growing, although not as sharply as before. The highest increase (+170.8 %) was recorded for 5G transmissions, but they account for only 7.8 % of the total volume. The average fixed internet user consumed 332 GB per month, with nearly half of connections reaching speeds over 100 Mbit/s. Mobile internet was used by 11.5 million SIM cards, with an average monthly consumption of 9.67 GB per card. Wi-Fi remains the most common connection method.
