# Designing for Foldable Displays
> 

[Foldable screens are revolutionizing mobile device design](https://medium.com/@jimmyfranco1993/designing-for-foldable-screens-89cd516e4f6a) thanks to their flexible shapes and multitasking capabilities. These technologies require adaptable layouts that transition smoothly between folded and unfolded states, addressing challenges like performance optimization or new touch interactions.

We've had responsive layouts for over a decade now, but the resolution typically associated with tablets is still largely neglected in both designs and coding. With foldable phones, perhaps the time has come when we'll encounter them more frequently.
