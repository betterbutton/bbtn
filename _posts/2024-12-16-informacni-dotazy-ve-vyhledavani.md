# Informational Queries Lead in Search
> 

Study by Rand Fishkin [analyzed 332 million Google search queries](https://sparktoro.com/blog/new-research-we-analyzed-332-million-queries-over-21-months-to-uncover-never-before-published-data-on-how-people-use-google/) over 21 months (January 2023—September 2024). It showed that 56% of queries are general, 44% contain brands. Over half of searches (51%) have an informational nature, 33.8% navigational, 14.5% commercial, and only 0.69% transactional. The most common categories are arts & entertainment (25%), games, and education. Only 148 queries account for 15% of all searches, while 58% of queries are unique and recur only once per month.
