# ČTÚ funds 5G signal in trains
> 

Familiar situation: in trains, the coating on windows simply blocks mobile signal propagation, and generally it's a Faraday cage – but only up to the borders with Germany. There, physics no longer applies. This is about to change! [ČTÚ published a grant call in April](https://ctu.gov.cz/narodni-plan-obnovy#:~:text=V%C3%BDzva%20%E2%80%9ETechnick%C3%A9%20prost%C5%99edky%20pro%C2%A05G%20v%C2%A0%C5%BEelezni%C4%8Dn%C3%ADch%20vozech%E2%80%9C) for improving 5G signal propagation in railway cars as part of the National Recovery Plan with money from the EU.

![Infographic says that by the end of 2025, mobile 5G network signal will be available in 670 train cars of Czech Railways, Leo Express, and RegioJet.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/8f64321d-dc66-468c-ba7a-ade0c9798d38_867x605.png "867x605")

Infographic from [Monitoring Report 9/2024](https://ctu.gov.cz/sites/default/files/obsah/ctu/monitorovaci-zprava-c.-9/2024/obrazky/mz-2024-9.pdf) by ČTÚ

[Applied for the funds](https://ctu.gov.cz/sites/default/files/obsah/ctu/monitorovaci-zprava-c.-9/2024/obrazky/mz-2024-9.pdf) Czech Railways, Leo Express, and RegioJet:

- Czech Railways will buy 50 signal repeaters for over 130 million, with which they will cover 151 cars.

- Czech Railways will then, for 33 million, have a grid created by laser on the coating of 233 windows, through which the signal will be able to pass into the car.

- Leo Express will buy signal repeaters for 23 million for 25 cars.

- Regiojet will replace windows in 260 cars for 101 million.

We will have 5G network signal in trains by the end of next year.
