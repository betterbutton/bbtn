# Don't force users to download another app
> 

Today's world is flooded with apps – every little thing requires downloading a new one. Apps, originally designed to make life easier, have become necessities for everyday tasks, from shopping to parking to communicating with work and school.

> „There’s an app for that? If only there weren’t.“

While [they promised simplicity, they brought chaos](https://www.theatlantic.com/technology/archive/2024/10/too-many-apps/680122/) – constant logins, updates, and figuring out which app is used where. Although it might seem that resistance to this invasion could grow, apps have already infiltrated every area of life – including how we deal with death. The future? More apps, less freedom.
