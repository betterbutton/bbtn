# Design Tokens Simplify Work for Designers and Developers
> 

Design Tokens are the building blocks of design systems, representing properties such as colors, typography, or spatial arrangement through named values. They are organized into hierarchical layers: from basic raw values through global abstractions to specific applications in components. Thanks to them, changes can be implemented consistently and efficiently. They bring benefits like easier maintenance, better communication between designers and developers, and minimization of errors. Design Tokens are the path to scalable and sustainable design systems because they [unify the language and ensure the flexibility of the entire system](https://medium.com/@marcelestevescorradi/o-que-s%C3%A3o-design-tokens-439a1d0d1601).
