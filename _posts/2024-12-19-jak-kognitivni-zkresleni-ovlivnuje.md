# How cognitive biases influence UX design
> 

Cognitive biases, also known as [mental shortcuts, influence how users perceive and respond to digital products](https://medium.com/@atnoforuiuxdesigning/how-cognitive-biases-impact-ux-design-68266c4c1175). UX design can leverage these psychological principles, such as anchoring, confirmation bias, or loss aversion, to create more intuitive and user-friendly interfaces. For example, highlighting a discount anchors users to a higher price, while familiar elements like the hamburger menu increase comfort.

Find a balance — excessive focus on biases can seem manipulative. Well-designed UX, however, helps users achieve their goals without cognitive overload.
