# Say it with a song: UX Researcher
> 

![Typical User Researcher playlist on Spotify](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/0714f1b8-4368-43e8-8c1b-6d935300a5d2_1523x963.png "1456x921")

Every profession has its clichés. From designers, you'll hear *[It depends](https://open.spotify.com/track/4NDp4Zq888W1DEB6oaVwY8?si=4d1cc8ecc20e4ec4)* and *[Trust me](https://open.spotify.com/track/6yXn9rLLN5znHGOe75yfl8?si=f662b5fe31c64970)*. A typical UX researcher is more likely to say *[Tell me more](https://open.spotify.com/track/1FC2VBtt9dWeXZoXIYVvbh?si=cdcc465a492a444d)* or *[Tell me why](https://open.spotify.com/track/47BBI51FKFwOMlIiX6m8ya?si=c08a039f522640c0)*. If you're on vacation right now and missing all those words, [Anton Krotov](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7219621735166287872/) has put together the playlist *[Typical User Researcher](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/2v53TBGfm94O7qE54PtDA4?si=6a78d9b963d647dc)* for you, where each of those phrases is sung by a song.
