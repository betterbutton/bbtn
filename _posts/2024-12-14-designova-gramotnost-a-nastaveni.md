# Design Literacy and Mindset
> 

Design in the era of intelligent automation gains new meaning: it's not just about outputs, but about an approach that connects creativity, intuition, and strategy. The author emphasizes that design is a universal ability transcending traditional professional boundaries. With AI facilitating creation, it's key to focus on meaningful questions, aesthetics, and purpose.

[Design Literacy is becoming as important as reading](https://vanlancker.substack.com/p/design-literacy), enabling the cultivation of originality and to excel in a world overloaded with content. Success requires courage, curiosity, and the ability for orchestral leadership of teams, where AI serves as a tool, not a replacement for human intuition.
