# Test your email and web security
> 

[Internet.nl](https://internet.nl/) is an online tool that checks whether your internet, email, or web meet modern standards. These standards are important for security, reliability, and scalability of the internet.

The project was created thanks to cooperation between the Dutch government and the internet community, including organizations like RIPE NCC. Many current users and providers still rely on outdated protocols, which poses a risk to individuals and the economy. Users should demand the implementation of new standards from their providers and thus contribute to a better internet.
