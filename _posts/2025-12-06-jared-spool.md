# Jared Spool as the Moral Compass of UX
> Jared Spool – a pioneer of usability testing and an advocate of evidence-based design who shaped modern UX.

![Jared Spool – drawing generated from a photograph](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/14fc6984-2af8-4049-b154-b1bfe63f091b_1536x1024.webp "502x334")

“Everyone’s a designer, but not everyone is a good designer.” — [Jared Spool](https://userdefenders.medium.com/072-everyones-a-ux-designer-with-jared-spool-transcript-b1166aac4854#:~:text=everyone%E2%80%99s%20a%20designer%2C%20but%20not%20everyone%20is%20a%20good%20designer)

*[Jared Spool](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jmspool/)* (*December 8, 1960) is among the most influential figures in user experience design. As the founder of *[Center Centre – UIE](https://centercentre.com/)*, he has dedicated over forty years to helping organizations understand what it truly means to measure design quality. His research debunked the popular claim that *[“five users are enough”](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/200553186_Testing_web_sites_five_users_is_nowhere_near_enough)* and turned usability testing into a precise, data-driven discipline.

Books like *[Web Site Usability](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/1763938.Web_Site_Usability)* or *[Web Anatomy](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/6355182-web-anatomy)* developed the idea that good design must stem from actual human behavior, not a designer's intuition. His research on [information scent](https://aycl.uie.com/virtual_seminars/the_scent_of_information_getting_users_to_their_content), navigation, and task success became foundational for web usability.

> “Design is the rendering of intent.”\
> **— [Jared Spool](https://www.uie.com/wp-assets/transcripts/great_time_to_be_a_ux_designer.html#:~:text=Design%20is%20the%20rendering%20of%20intent.)**

Since the early 2000s, Jared expanded his focus from micro-level usability to strategic UX—how design influences organizational outcomes. He urged teams to use outcome-focused metrics and to view UX as a strategic partner, not a service department.

As an educator and speaker, he spread his ideas through project-based teaching at Center Centre, hundreds of articles, podcasts, and lectures. His message remains constant: design is only successful when it improves the lives of the people who use it.

From an empirical researcher to a mentor for the entire industry, *Jared Spool* helped define UX as both a profession and a moral commitment—built on curiosity, evidence, and respect for users.

> “Intuitive design happens when current knowledge is the same as the target knowledge.”\
> **― [Jared Spool](https://www.goodreads.com/quotes/917553-intuitive-design-happens-when-current-knowledge-is-the-same-as)**
