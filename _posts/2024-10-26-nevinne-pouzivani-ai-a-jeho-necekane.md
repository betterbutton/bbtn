# Innocent Use of AI and Its Unexpected Consequences
> 

 encountered an unwanted modification of her photograph using an AI tool designed to add the upper half of the body to a portrait so she could use it in promotional materials for the [Upscale conference in Málaga](https://www.upscaleconf.com/). The innocent use of artificial intelligence caused her profile picture to look as if Elizabeth had an unbuttoned shirt. This led her to [reflect on how we use AI.](https://elizlaraki.substack.com/p/ai-is-harmless-or-is-it)

[Elizabeth described the situation on Twitter.](https://x.com/elizlaraki/status/1846252781851890026) The post garnered over two million views and hundreds of comments. The experience opened her eyes to the importance of proper handling of technologies, from selecting appropriate tools to critically evaluating their outputs. She emphasizes that AI users should be aware of potential risks and consequences, even in seemingly mundane tasks.
