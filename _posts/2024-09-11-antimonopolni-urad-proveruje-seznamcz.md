# Antitrust Authority probes Seznam.cz
> 

We know more than what is written in the headline about the case. [Jana Klímová writes for iRozhlas](https://www.irozhlas.cz/ekonomika/antimonopolni-urad-se-zajima-o-seznamcz-zverejnil-v-reakci-na-clanky-o-sefovi_2409110620_ava), that alongside the non-public investigation of Seznam by the antitrust authority, a parallel dispute is still ongoing between the Authority and the investigated company, which is taking place publicly.

[Seznam wrote about contacts](https://www.seznamzpravy.cz/clanek/domaci-kauzy-sef-antimonopolniho-uradu-spolupracuje-s-obzalovanym-expertem-na-losovacky-258107) between the director of the authority and an entrepreneur suspected of subsidy fraud. [The Authority published a detailed response](https://uohs.gov.cz/cs/informacni-centrum/tiskove-zpravy/verejne-zakazky/3962-uvadime-na-pravou-miru-seznam-zpravy-sef-antimonopolniho-uradu-spolupracuje-s-obzalovanym-expertem-na-losovacky.html), in which there is quite unusual speculation: *"It may, however, also be a warning gesture from the company Seznam.cz towards the authority, which has recently begun to show interest in the company's operations as part of its activities."*

At the end of the article, iRozhlas gives space to [Daniel Dočekal](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniel_Do%C4%8Dekal), who points out that Seznam is indeed the Czech internet number one, but in the key markets of search and advertising, it faces global competition from companies like Google and Meta, and in the media sector also from public service entities.
