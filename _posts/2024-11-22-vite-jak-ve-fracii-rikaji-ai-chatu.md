# Do you know what they call AI chat in France? Le Chat.
> 

Mistral AI introduces artificial intelligence in a conversational interface: [Le Chat](https://chat.mistral.ai/), now available for free in beta. Le Chat offers new tools such as a web search with citations, a canvas for content creation and editing, image generation, or document analysis with the help of the multimodal model Pixtral Large.

Additionally, it adds automation agents that will facilitate recurring workflows. Performance is supported by faster responses thanks to speculative generation. Le Chat aims to make cutting-edge AI technologies accessible without the need for high costs, thus becoming a competitor to paid tools.
