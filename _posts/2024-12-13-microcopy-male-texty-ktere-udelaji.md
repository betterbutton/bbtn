# Microcopy: Small texts that make a big difference
> 

Microcopy is short text in the user interface that guides, reassures, and facilitates interaction. The key to effectiveness is clarity, empathy, and conciseness. Microcopy can also add personality, but never at the expense of understandability. Important places for microcopy include error messages, buttons, and onboarding.

“Be clear, not clever.“\
**— [RamshaQ](https://medium.com/@Ramshaq/ux-writing-how-to-create-user-friendly-microcopy-dc78297a1134)**

Writing great microcopy requires researching user needs, testing, and collaborating with design. Examples, like Mailchimp’s “High five!”, show how small text can improve the user experience. Avoid vagueness, excessive complexity, and inappropriate tone – [good microcopy is intuitive and memorable](https://medium.com/@Ramshaq/ux-writing-how-to-create-user-friendly-microcopy-dc78297a1134).
