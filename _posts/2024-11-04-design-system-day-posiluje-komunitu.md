# Design System Day strengthens the GOV.UK community
> 

[Design System Day](https://design-system.service.gov.uk/community/design-system-day-2024/) is the annual event of the GOV.UK Design System, which revitalizes the community around this open-source project after the summer break. The conference connects community members and supports participatory creation, where people not only contribute ideas but also shape the main topics.

The event reflects the team's core values, such as openness, support, and empowerment. [At Design System Day, creators from various fields come together](https://gds.blog.gov.uk/2024/10/31/how-design-system-day-makes-our-community-stronger/) to share their experiences and ideas and bring innovation to public services. This November will also feature a thematic hackathon that will engage a wider audience.
