# Information for UX writers in one place
> 

Are you responsible for texts for websites, apps, transactional emails, and the like? Lenka Stawarczyk shared her links and resources. Now you'll find them nicely gathered in one place in the [UX writing hub](https://dune-harbor-fc1.notion.site/uxwritinghub) on her Notion page. Lenka recommends books, articles, examples of great writing guidelines, and also people from abroad and from the Czech Republic who are worth following.
