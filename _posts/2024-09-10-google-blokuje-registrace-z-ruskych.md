# Google blocks registrations from Russian phone numbers
> 

![Screenshot of the registration of a new user account on Google with the interface in Cyrillic.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/d608eec5-61f4-43b2-b750-693282f7e311_1280x466.jpeg "1280x466")

Google has stopped allowing [account registrations using Russian phone numbers](https://x.com/nexta_tv/status/1833129417188487303), probably in connection with new US sanctions against Russia. The company has not issued any official statement on this, but it is another step within the technological sanctions that Google imposed after the Russian invasion of Ukraine.

Other measures include blocking Russian state media on YouTube, restrictions on Google Pay for some Russian banks, and a ban on downloading paid apps from Google Play in Russia.
