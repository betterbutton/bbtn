# How to Talk to People on Social Networks – Vyhnánková and Losekoot
> 

![Mockup of the ebook How to Talk to People on Social Networks](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/e94c5933-73ab-4936-8935-ea14ae2dfd6d_720x720.png "720x720")

Eliška Vyhnánková and Michelle Losekoot, authors of the 2019 bestseller *How to Networks*, present the ebook [How to Talk to People on Social Networks](https://digitalni.jaknasite.cz/jak-mluvit-s-lidmi-na-sitich) as part of their project [Digital How to Networks](https://digitalni.jaknasite.cz/). They offer a guide to social media communication with 71 pages of practical tips and over 50 real-world examples. You'll gain valuable insights into content creation, community building, and customer care. No unnecessary theory, just practical, data-backed advice.
