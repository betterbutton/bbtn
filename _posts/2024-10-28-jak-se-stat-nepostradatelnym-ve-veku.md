# How to Become Indispensable in the Age of AI
> 

UX design is changing under the influence of AI, but that doesn't mean designers are redundant. On the contrary, those who can combine traditional design with technologies like AI will have an advantage. This hope is given to UX designers by Parinaz Kassemi in the article [The UX Designer’s Guide to Staying Essential in the Age of AI](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/the-ux-designers-guide-to-staying-essential-in-the-age-of-ai-5f013318eee9) and she adds a four-leaf clover of advice:

1.  Embrace hybrid skills.

2.  Double down on human-centered design.

3.  Stay active in UX communities and educational platforms.

4.  Focus on adaptability and growth.

> “The key designers are not those with the most experience, but those who never stop evolving.”\
> — **[Parinaz Kassemi](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/the-ux-designers-guide-to-staying-essential-in-the-age-of-ai-5f013318eee9)**

The key, according to Parinaz, is to adopt hybrid skills that combine creativity and new tools. The focus on empathy, creative solutions, and the human perspective remains irreplaceable. Those who follow trends, engage in communities, and never stop learning will lead a constantly evolving field.
