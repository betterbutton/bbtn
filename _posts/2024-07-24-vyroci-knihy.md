# A year ago, the book '75 tips on how to get a website' was published
> 

![Staged photo showing Jakub Goldmann standing over Josef Ježek (seated at a computer) as they display a two-page spread from the book '75 Tips on How to Get a Website'. In the background, David Švejda and Martin Kopt are goofing around.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/a7469153-f260-437a-b1b1-da5681c3446b_6142x4096.jpeg "1456x971")

Photo: David Inneman, [Social Lions](https://www.sociallions.cz/)

Twelve months ago, the book [75 tips on how to get a website](https://reknisioweb.cz/p/kniha), written by [Jakub Goldmann](https://www.jakubgoldmann.cz/), [Martin Kopta](http://www.martinkopta.cz/) and Josef Platil, went on sale. It all started with a phone call from [Michal Bláha](https://cz.linkedin.com/in/michalb): *"Guys, you should do training for civil servants."* At the time, Michal was working on the formation of [DIA](https://www.dia.gov.cz/) and noticed the vast gap between how the public administration and the commercial sector approach website procurement.

![Slide from a presentation for publishers showing where on the axes Business—Technology and Concrete—Abstract our book falls, and where other books are available on the market.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/bcf8cbb2-9875-4fa5-bf33-c4c1f3e51527_1400x785.webp "1400x785")

Screenshot from a presentation for publishers

We approached writing the book like a startup. We prepared a pitch deck and sent it to four publishers. Two responded with interest, and we eventually shook hands with Computer Press. Jakub described the book's creation in the article [How we wrote the book](https://medium.com/svejda-goldmann-webdesign/jak-jsme-psali-knihu-2959e4888f4d).

Our trio was soon joined by illustrator [Kateřina Čápová](https://www.katerinacapova.cz/), whose illustrations gave the book its face, and the studio [Ginger & Fred](https://gingerafred.cz/) with Lukáš Honzák, thanks to whom the book has a distinctive look and glows yellow from bookshelves.

You can [buy the book online](https://knihy.heureka.cz/75-tipu-jak-si-rict-o-web/) or in bookstores.
