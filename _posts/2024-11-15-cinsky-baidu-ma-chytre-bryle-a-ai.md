# Chinese Baidu has smart glasses and AI chat
> 

A while ago, the [Chinese video generator](https://hailuoai.video/) stirred the waters, and now the Chinese search engine [Baidu has introduced smart glasses](https://www.ft.com/content/9e0f5f13-22e3-470e-b329-79c11dc8b5d2), which aim to compete with [Meta's Ray-Bans](https://www.ray-ban.com/usa/ray-ban-meta-smart-glasses), and a few days later came with improved technology [I-RAG image generation and the Miaoda tool](https://www.reuters.com/technology/artificial-intelligence/baidu-bolsters-ai-lineup-with-text-to-image-generator-no-code-app-builder-2024-11-12/), which allows app creation without coding. Its [AI chatbot Ernie](https://chat.baidu.com/) processes 1.5 billion queries daily.

The glasses are built on the Ernie language model. They will offer AI assistant features including calorie tracking and video recording. Although China lags behind the US in the most powerful AI models, its tech sector could succeed in integrating AI into consumer electronics.
