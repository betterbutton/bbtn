# UX design influences our everyday experiences
> 

User Experience design focuses on how people interact with products, systems, or services. It's not just about aesthetics, but primarily about functionality, accessibility, and the ability to solve problems. UX is present everywhere — from apps to the layout of a café. Well-designed UX increases user satisfaction, while poor UX frustrates and discourages.

> „Good UX can feel invisible because everything just works.“\
> **— [Aman Kumar](https://medium.com/@amankrsharma64/what-is-ux-design-really-ab928f033848)**

Today it is more important than ever because quality UX supports inclusivity, increases loyalty, and brings better results to companies. Everyone, regardless of profession, can benefit from [understanding UX design](https://medium.com/@amankrsharma64/what-is-ux-design-really-ab928f033848).
