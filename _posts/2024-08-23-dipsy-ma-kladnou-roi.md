# eGov: DiPSy now has a positive ROI
> 

Pirate deputy minister for legislation [Ondřej Profant on his Twitter](https://x.com/ondrej_profant/status/1821533798384693331) calculated that if parents sent each of the 173,500 applications submitted this year for secondary school by registered mail, they would pay just over twelve million crowns in postage alone. The total costs of acquiring and operating [the DiPSy digital application system](https://dipsy.cz/prihlaska/intro) did not exceed ten million.

This is great news for Czech e-government! Now the ball is in the court of the regions to adjust the offer of secondary schools to the demographic curve and demand for general education, and with the government, which should fulfill its program declaration in which [it declared education a priority](https://vlada.gov.cz/cz/programove-prohlaseni-vlady-193547/#:~:text=zajist%C3%AD%20dlouhodobou%20perspektivu.-,Vzd%C4%9Bl%C3%A1v%C3%A1n%C3%AD,-Budoucnost%20na%C5%A1%C3%AD%20zem%C4%9B).
