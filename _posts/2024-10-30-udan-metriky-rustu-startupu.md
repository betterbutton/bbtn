# Miroslav Uďan: The Most Important Metrics for Startup Growth and Investment
> 

> „You will have an overview of how your business is developing and will also convince investors that you have your business under control and understand the key aspects of growth.“\
> — **[Miroslav Uďan](https://www.miroslavudan.cz/blog/jake-jsou-nejdulezitejsi-metriky-ve-startupu/)**

Tracking the right key metrics is crucial for startup growth and securing investment. Among the most important indicators are customer acquisition cost ([CAC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Customer_acquisition_cost)), payback period ([Payback Period](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Payback_period#:~:text=Payback%20period%20in%20capital%20budgeting,a%20two%2Dyear%20payback%20period.)), customer lifetime value ([LTV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Customer_lifetime_value)), churn rate ([Churn Rate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Churn_rate)), and monthly recurring revenue ([MRR](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Revenue_stream#Recurring_revenue)) – plus five more you can read about directly [in Miroslav Uďan's article](https://www.miroslavudan.cz/blog/jake-jsou-nejdulezitejsi-metriky-ve-startupu/). Each of these metrics impacts a company's scalability and financial sustainability. Startups should track marketing efficiency, optimize the customer experience, and improve retention to ensure long-term growth and stability.
