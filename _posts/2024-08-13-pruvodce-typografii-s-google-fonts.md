# Guide to Typography with Google Fonts
> 

You probably know Google Fonts. But did you know that besides its catalog and web font hosting, it also offers high-quality [educational content about fonts and typography](https://fonts.google.com/knowledge/)? You'll learn how to choose a suitable font, how to work with web fonts, about the Open Type format and the use of variable fonts, about font readability and accessibility, something about non-Latin fonts, and plenty of other reasonably dosed information.
