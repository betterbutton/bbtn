# Adobe makes painting app Fresco free in battle against Procreate
> 

Adobe has made its drawing app [Fresco completely free](https://www.adobe.com/products/fresco.html) in an effort to compete with popular Procreate. [Warning from 365tips](https://bsky.app/profile/365tipu.cz/post/3l7ax3t5jdo2g) came just in time. This afternoon, autumn holidays begin, so we can spend a lot of time discovering the new app.

Previously, premium features, such as a wider selection of brushes or access to the entire Adobe Fonts library, were available only with an annual subscription. Now [Fresco unlocks all features for free](https://www.theverge.com/2024/10/23/24277533/adobe-fresco-painting-app-free-availablility-procreate) for all users on [iPads and iPhones](https://adobefresco.app.link/0yALs8tZYZ) and [Windows](https://creativecloud.adobe.com/apps/download/fresco-desktop) devices. Adobe hopes that with this move it will attract new users, although Procreate remains popular due to its one-time payment and clear rejection of generative AI integration.
