# Mattel modifies games for the color-blind
> 

The manufacturer of the Barbie doll, but also of popular games such as [card game Uno](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uno_(karetn%C3%AD_hra)), announced that by the end of the year [it will modify eighty percent of its games for the color-blind](https://www.fastcompany.com/91146946/mattel-is-making-its-games-colorblind-accessible). Next year, it will then make another ten percent of games accessible to the color-blind.

Mattel is not forced to take this step by any European regulations or American laws. Legislation on accessibility does not yet apply to board games and card games. That's why we applaud this step.

And who will be next? What about distinguishing between open and closed cash registers in Lidl?
