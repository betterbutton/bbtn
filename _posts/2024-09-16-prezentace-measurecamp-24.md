# MeasureCamp ’24 Presentation
> 

Last weekend, an unconference about web analytics [MeasureCamp ’24](https://czechia.measurecamp.org/) was held in Radlice. If you didn't make it to the event, don't miss out on [the presentations that participants share](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1vcR0w1x3TiRyx5fmDs_7WRxtB0Tz2HfO).

- Piotr Gruszecki shared his [set of analytical tools](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1LwEUwyjrxyeAkLWfZjbzRfMMUN_vCMTA/view?usp=drive_link).

- Tomáš Komárek presented [the possibilities of using Privacy Sandbox](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1E_Mity5Up5Do3DIbmycEgISgNPWKU7Mscwl8cL2V3xk/edit?usp=drive_link), which is meant to replace [third-party cookies in Chrome](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/privacy-sandbox-results?utm_source=publication-search).

- Dominik Jirotka offered his [article on data visualization](https://igloonet.cz/blog/datove-vizualizacni-minimalismus-a-jak-ho-aplikovat/) in the form of [a presentation](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1SrLhundA_p0x9R3-ttYyfXUoE884pK2fbECEM_nZkgM/edit?usp=drive_link).

- Michal Nováček showed [how to measure WebView](https://drive.google.com/file/d/10Ak8E9xWSEDfWSn9MOwne9C3tDflK2li/view?usp=drive_link) in Google Analytics.

- Michal Blažek talked about how, as an analyst, one sometimes must [get by without developer support](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1qXFnTwqwdtX5CazZcaPghlP1Wl-3h85YUliBe0I4kXk/edit?usp=drive_link).

- André Wehr compared [the tools Piwik Pro, Matomo, and Google Analytics](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1SjhBdRW6lp2fWGFSlh9iqVnMGz8xLDfR/view?usp=drive_link).

- Anna Horáková and Vašek Jelen exported [data on online advertising to BigQuery](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1RygZJFr9Qhh2dVUP6zXIZsmi1ccg_IWs/view?usp=drive_link).
