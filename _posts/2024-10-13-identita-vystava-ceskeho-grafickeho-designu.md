The exhibition [Identity – The Story of Czech Graphic Design](https://www.museumkampa.cz/vystava/identita-pribeh-ceskeho-grafickeho-designu/) at the Kampa Museum offers an overview of key works of Czech graphic design from the early 20th century to the present, from October 12, 2024 to February 2, 2025. Iconic works – posters, books, logos, state symbols, navigation systems, and examples from audiovisual media – will be presented to visitors on three floors.

The exhibition is [part of a broader project](https://projektidentita.cz/) that includes a [TV series](https://www.ceskatelevize.cz/porady/13917580517-identita/), a [film](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pp3OfzDVN60), and a [book](https://www.paseka.cz/produkt/identita/), and focuses on the cultural and social impact of design.
