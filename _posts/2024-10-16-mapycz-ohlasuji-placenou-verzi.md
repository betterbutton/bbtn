# Mapy.cz Announce Paid Version
> 

Mapy.cz is announcing upcoming paid services to users of the application. As [already written on their Twitter profile in May](https://x.com/ondrej1974/status/1788545875179925767 by then-member of Seznam's supervisory board Ondřej Procházka, Mapy.cz is introducing a paid tariff.

After updating the application, users of Mapy.cz are shown a screen with the following message:

![BIS<> M From the next version of the app, you can support the further development of Mapy.cz by purchasing the premium version. Our premium supporters will get new advanced features and unlimited offline map downloads. The benefits of Mapy.cz Premium will also be gradually unlocked for the most active members of the community. The free version of the app The app will continue to work for free. Only offline map storage will change. You will now be able to save an offline map of one state including individual regions and update it regularly. Thank you for your support! The Mapy.cz team Go to map](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/e2479d16-bba8-431e-8d4e-d3433db0f8fe_750x1850.png "296x730")

Seznam does not include Mapy.cz in its [consent or pay](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/i/143207248/linkodrom) program, where users can opt out of tracking across services. The subscription for premium features is thus an alternative way for Mapy.cz to generate revenue.

The news was brought by [Lupa.cz](https://www.lupa.cz/aktuality/mapy-cz-chystaji-premiovou-verzi-zpoplatni-stahovani-offline-map-vice-statu/ already in the afternoon.
