# Linkodrom

**Seznam started selling** ***digital opt-outs***. As we wrote in [\#09](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/09), Seznam began offering users the possibility to opt out of tracking personal data on the website. For 79 CZK per month, it promises not to track device and user location data. Some users [are not happy](https://x.com/AdinaFXV/status/1778526012575473980). Seznam [tested a new approach](https://blog.seznam.cz/2024/03/seznam-cz-nabizi-uzivatelum-stale-vice-moznosti-jak-v-online-svete-pracovat-se-svym-soukromim/) to user data since March. For users, it prepared a page explaining [how it all works](https://o.seznam.cz/souhlas/). Simultaneously, Seznam [began refusing to serve](https://twitter.com/MarekLecian/status/1779159562404196399) users with their browser set to a higher privacy protection level. For Seznam, which is dependent on advertising revenue, the new approach to user data is important also with the approaching [end of support for third-party cookies](https://www.lupa.cz/clanky/jak-muze-seznam-cz-vydelat-na-konci-cookies-tretich-stran/) in browsers. Therefore, in this case as well, it guides users to also sign up for a user account.

**Websites have worsened in barrier-free accessibility.** [96% of web pages do not meet accessibility standards](https://webaim.org/projects/million/), says a study of the million most visited websites by WebAIM. [Martin Hassman writes on Zdroják](https://zdrojak.cz/zpravicky/stav-pristupnosti-webu-v-roce-2024/), that page accessibility over time is deteriorating in contrast, alternative image texts, form field labels, or link texts. With the approaching effective date of the European directive [on accessibility requirements for products and services](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/CS/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32019L0882) next June ([we wrote in #03](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/i/140739411/na-cem-prave-pracujeme)), attention to accessibility will need to increase. Fortunately, you can build [WordPress to be accessible even blind](https://poslepu.cz/stavime-pristupny-web-na-wordpressu-zaznam-webinare-z-22-3-2024/).

**Zásilkovna will no longer have exclusivity in pickup locations.** [Lupa reports](https://www.lupa.cz/aktuality/zasilkovna-uz-nebude-vyzadovat-po-vydejnach-exkluzivitu-reaguje-tak-na-tlak-uohs/) that Zásilkovna will yield to [pressure from the antitrust office](https://uohs.gov.cz/cs/informacni-centrum/tiskove-zpravy/hospodarska-soutez/3856-diky-zasahu-uohs-umozni-zasilkovna-sdilet-vydejni-mista-konkurenci-zvysi-se-tak-vyber-pro-spotrebitele.html) and open its contracted pickup points to competition. Will Czech Post seize the opportunity? 🤔

**Allegro (Mall.cz) is building [its own pickup boxes](https://www.mediaguru.cz/clanky/2024/04/allegro-zacina-rozvijet-vlastni-samoobsluzne-one-boxy/).**

**Do you have an e-shop? And could we see your MFA?** At the Polish web agency TheStory, they are considering [the impacts of the European PSD2 directive](https://thestory.is/en/journal/psd2-what-does-an-online-store-owner-need-to-know-we-explain-and-suggest/) on e-commerce. We thought it would be enough if payment systems introduced strong customer authentication, but increasingly, online entities that handle their clients' money (like credit) find that European regulators are requiring the introduction of multi-factor authentication and biometrics even outside the financial segment.

**What does AI need to control search?** Joan Westernberg raises her eyebrows significantly regarding [the connection of the future of search with AI](https://joanwestenberg.com/blog/the-future-of-search-not-so-much) : *"I’m not anti-AI. But we can’t treat it as a magic wand that will let us wish away all the problems of traditional search."* She mainly points out that current AI is prone to errors, biases, and is easily manipulable.

**100 tips for effective work with Figma.** Nitish Khagwal compiled [an overview of hundreds of tips](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1161621065197114331) for better productivity in Figma.

**Problems with higher salaries in the public sector** are not just in IT. To the voices of [Karel Večeřa](https://www.lupa.cz/clanky/kvalitni-statni-it-silny-interni-tym-odborniku-to-je-to-oc-tu-bezi/) on the situation at the Office for Government Representation and [Lukáš Kintr](https://www.lupa.cz/clanky/nukib-trapi-velke-mnozstvi-odchodu-malo-placenych-zamestnancu-situaci-zachranuji-srdcari/) from NÚKIB, [Martin Bunček](https://twitter.com/martin_buncek/status/1775577111626977726) from TA ČR adds: *"It's basically unmanageable now – any 'managerial' tools are being cut back and you just watch quality people leave."*

**What to do with it?** The AI dětem association [won't help us with old straw](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XdjkQLoY9jE&t=42s), but they are organizing [summer suburban camps](https://aidetem.cz/letni-primestske-tabory-2024/) for children, writes [Martin Michálek on Twitter](https://x.com/machal/status/1779575269356863573).

We guided participants in creating [an innovative environment](https://www.2fresh.design/work-2/spinn) and in [innovation management](https://www.youngfoundation.org/our-work/publications/the-open-book-of-social-innovation/) we focused on [practicing methods](https://kisk.phil.muni.cz/100metod/) using our favorite double diamond. People from libraries, but also from museums and galleries, attended the training. It was the last third term of training within the [Culture in the Curriculum](https://kulturavkurzu.cz/) program. More interested parties are still contacting us, so it is likely that we will return to Ústí nad Labem with the training in the autumn. Perhaps even with an offer for commercial entities. If you are interested, [get in touch](https://kulturavkurzu.cz/https-kulturavkurzu-cz-kontakt/).
