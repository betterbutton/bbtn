# Invitation: From Specification to Prototype in Two Hours
> 

> **[From Specification to Prototype in One Day](https://budoucnostjedigitalni.gov.cz/#workshop-od-napadu-k-prototypu)**\
> November 20, 2024, 9:00—11:00\
> [Hrzánský Palace](https://mapy.cz/s/fofazubepo), Loretánská 177/9, Prague 1-Hradčany
>
> [Free registration](https://budoucnostjedigitalni.gov.cz/#workshop-od-napadu-k-prototypu)

We are organizing a free workshop [From Specification to Prototype in One Day](https://budoucnostjedigitalni.gov.cz/#workshop-od-napadu-k-prototypu). We offer practical insight into the complete UX design process. The workshop is prepared for the general public and no prior experience is required. During one day – actually just in two hours – you will learn to quickly create and test interactive prototypes. We will start from initial research and end with user testing.

> Thanks to practical exercises and expert feedback, you will gain skills that will allow you to deliver fast and quality results.

We have designed the workshop flow so that you can speed up and adapt the process based on feedback. The workshop is led by the two of us: Jakub Goldmann and Martin Kopta. You will learn techniques of wireframing, creating empathy maps, and other skills that will make your design practice more effective.
