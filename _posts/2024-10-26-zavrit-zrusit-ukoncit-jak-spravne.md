# Close, Cancel, Exit: How to Choose the Right Words for Ending Interactions
> 

Choosing the right words for [ending user interactions](https://css-tricks.com/close-exit-cancel-how-to-end-user-interactions-well/) is crucial for ensuring clarity and easy understanding. Words like "cancel", "quit", and "exit" can be interchangeable in certain contexts, but their nuances are essential. While "cancel" may imply an attempt to correct an error, "quit" represents a final termination. "Exit" sounds softer and is suitable for human interactions. The goal is to ensure that every action is clearly understood and does not lead to unintentional user errors.
