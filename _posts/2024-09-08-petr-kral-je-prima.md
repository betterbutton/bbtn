# Petr Král will lead Prima+
> 

[Petr Král](https://www.linkedin.com/in/petrkral/) took over the role of director](https://www.skupinaprima.cz/petr-kral-prichazi-do-vedeni-streamingove-sluzby-prima/) of the [VOD](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Video_on_demand) and [FAST](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_ad-supported_streaming_television) segment of the Prima+ streaming service in early September. Petr replaces the previous director, Josef Beneš, and brings with him rich experience from online projects such as TV Nova or, before that, Seznam.cz, where we met him while working on projects. Under Petr's leadership, Prima plans to expand the platform's content, including offerings for children and younger generations.

We're a bit sorry that Petr probably won't have as much space to write short observations on LinkedIn as before, but we're excited that Prima+ will take off similarly to Voyo or Stream before it. Break a leg, Petr!
