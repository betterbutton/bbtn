# Seznam.cz without ads for 179 CZK monthly
> 

Seznam.cz is launching a subscription [Seznam without ads](https://napoveda.seznam.cz/cz/login/produkt-seznam-bez-reklam/) ([archive](https://archive.ph/I7DiS)), which for 179 CZK per month will allow users to browse websites without banners, video ads, and podcast advertising. You can try Seznam without ads for two weeks for free. The product will replace the existing [Seznam Premium](https://premium.seznam.cz/) ([archive](https://archive.ph/ODFqH)) service and will expand to more than twenty websites, including news, email, and real estate.

Users can activate the service on a special page or within Seznam Account. Additionally, Seznam is also reducing the price of the “Seznam without targeted advertising” service to 49 CZK per month, which originally [launched with a price thirty crowns higher](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/i/143207248/linkodrom). This offer is a response to the changing digital market and the search for new ways to fund quality content.

> *“If we do not display ads to users as part of the Seznam without ads product, it is obvious that we do not collect any data about them for targeting purposes.”*\
> **— Jiří Udatný, Director of the Identity and User Profile Division ([Lupa.cz](https://www.lupa.cz/aktuality/seznam-cz-spousti-predplatne-ktere-skryje-reklamy-vyjimkou-jsou-vysledky-vyhledavani/))**

Seznam without ads, as is typical with similar subscriptions, is not completely ad-free: *“You will still see advertising in Search results, commercial content created jointly with partners and Seznam Native, or hear audio ads that are part of the soundtracks of our podcasts,”* [Lupa cites](https://www.lupa.cz/aktuality/seznam-cz-spousti-predplatne-ktere-skryje-reklamy-vyjimkou-jsou-vysledky-vyhledavani/) Seznam.

Various Seznam representatives explained over the past six months [the introduction of the subscription as a necessity](https://x.com/ilblog/status/1784595305737908407), driven by the European Union's pressure to protect user privacy and the related expected [end of third-party cookie support](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/privacy-sandbox-results) in the Chrome browser. Chrome currently holds a 65.2% market share.
