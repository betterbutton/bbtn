# The Need for Belonging and Working with It in Design
> 

Digital products influence our [basic need for belonging](https://medium.com/mikewcurtis/ux-and-the-psychology-of-belonging-3f494a366e3b). Our brains perceive social pain just as intensely as physical pain, which has a fundamental impact on user behavior.

> “Evolution programmed us so that belonging meant survival.”\
> **— Mike Curtis**

Examples – from a teenager seeking validation on Instagram to a grandma learning video chat – show how technology satisfies a deep desire for connection.

Designers have a responsibility to look beyond metrics and understand that every digital decision touches the emotional core of users. Evolutionary foundations, like survival through social bonds, explain why connection is so critical for our behavior online and offline.
