# Accessibility: Practical Free Ebook
> 

Products with accessibility barriers are defective. The first step towards remediation is interest. Whether your interest in accessibility is driven by social responsibility or expectations of the effectiveness of the European accessibility law, the book [Giving a Damn about Accessibility](https://www.accessibility.uxdesign.cc/) with the subtitle a candid and practical guide for designers by Sheri Byrne-Haber and UX Collective is a good place to start learning more about accessibility. The book is available [free as PDF](https://www.accessibility.uxdesign.cc/s/Giving-a-damn-about-accessibility.pdf) or [in audio version](https://soundcloud.com/uxcollective/giving-a-damn-about-accessibility).
