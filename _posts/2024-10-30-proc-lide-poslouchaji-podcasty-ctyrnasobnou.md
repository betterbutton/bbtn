# Why do people listen to podcasts at 4x and half speed?
> 

A surprising request for support for playing podcasts at 4× speed in the [Metacast](https://metacast.app/) app revealed interesting user needs. Metacast CEO [Ilya Bezdelev writes about it on Linkedin](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7252694903854452736/). People with visual impairments, thanks to their enhanced listening abilities, navigate content much faster. Also, some users without visual impairments can process information at high speeds, helping them efficiently browse large amounts of content.

“Users learn foreign languages by listening to podcasts in foreign languages—slowing down playback helps them better understand speech.”\
— **[Ilya Bezdelev](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7252694903854452736/)**

On the other hand, people learning foreign languages or those who want to understand more complex speech prefer slower listening. Metacast now offers playback speed settings from 0.5× to 4× with improved controls.
