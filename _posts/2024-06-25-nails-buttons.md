# Consider users with long nails when designing buttons
> 

Current design is full of biases. That sometimes manifests in the smallest details. Harsh Vijay noticed that [women with long nails don't use the tip of their finger to tap on the touchscreen](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7209424068662464512/), but the pad of their finger. They need much larger button areas and also larger spacing between active elements on the screen.
