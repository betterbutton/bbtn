# HSL Color Space as the Best Friend of Designers
> 
HSL model (Hue, Saturation, Lightness) [makes it easy to work with colors for designers](https://medium.com/@mesupriyomahato/why-the-hsl-color-model-is-a-designers-best-friend-710f84d96280) by allowing simple adjustments of color shades and lightness without changing other properties.
> HSL makes it easy to change just the shade or brightness without affecting other attributes.
Hue determines the color, Saturation its intensity, and Lightness adjusts the lightness, which is ideal for creating consistent design in applications or websites. The advantage of HSL is also the ability to use variables, which is practical for coding in SCSS and maintaining color consistency across different states of UI elements.
