# Šlerka: Learn About, these will still be interesting months
> 

Google introduced [Learn About](https://learning.google/), a tool combining an AI chatbot and a search engine, tailored to the needs of users. This free experimental feature within [Google Labs](https://labs.google/) facilitates learning through interactive lists and clearly organized topics.

Semiotician Josef Šlerka from Charles University examined the tool and [reacted on Linkedin with the words](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7267269591976787968/): *„\[T\]hese will still be interesting months.“*

The service focuses on educational content, [connects artificial intelligence with human resources](https://www.searchenginejournal.com/googles-ai-search-experiment-learn-about/532840/), and simplifies access to complex information. The service offers a customizable interface with the option to deepen or simplify information. [ZDNET calls the tool an innovative study aid](https://www.zdnet.com/article/this-google-ai-tool-could-be-your-new-favorite-study-aid-and-its-free/), available for users over 18, and we add that you will still need a VPN with a US IP address.
