# AR changes the future of UX
> 

Augmented Reality (AR) fundamentally changes the way we think about user interface/user experience (UI/UX). From virtual shoe try-ons to real-time home planning, AR brings depth, context, and interactivity into design.

> „Forget flat screens and 2D layouts — AR brings depth, context, and immersion into the equation.“\
> **— [Zahidul Hossain](https://medium.com/@zahidul_ui/augmented-reality-ar-edda32dabdb7)**

[Designers must redefine UX principles](https://medium.com/@zahidul_ui/augmented-reality-ar-edda32dabdb7), including new interaction patterns, adapting to the physical environment, and balancing between innovation and simplicity. The future belongs to AR wearable devices, inclusive design, and real-time collaboration tools. For designers, AR is not only a technological challenge but also an opportunity to create more natural and personal experiences.
