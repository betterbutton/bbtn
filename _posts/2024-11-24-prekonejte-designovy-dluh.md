# Overcome design debt
> 

Design debt arises when compromises are made during product development, such as ignoring usability testing or failing to maintain design consistency, [writes Sanchita Srivastava](https://medium.com/@sanchita.sri/design-debt-the-hidden-barrier-to-exceptional-user-experience-b26e5a2ae356). It is divided into intentional (conscious circumvention of problems) and unintentional (neglecting changing user needs).

This debt has hidden costs: it reduces user trust, burdens teams, and increases maintenance costs. Solutions include implementing design systems, regular UX audits, and active work with the backlog of issues. Key is to gain leadership support and integrate a culture of quality into team processes.
