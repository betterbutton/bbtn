# UX Research for Generative AI
> 

Generative AI, such as digital assistants or information creation tools, presents new challenges for UX research. Classic prototype testing focused on predictable user journeys is not easily applicable. Every interaction between the user and AI is unique and depends on data quality, the user's prompting skills, and the system's accuracy.

[Testing can be optimized using UX audits and heuristic evaluations](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/ux-research-methods-for-testing-generative-ai-3dfbe74b28ec), assessment of output accuracy, and study of user expectations. These methods reveal areas for improvement and help teams adapt onboarding and increase AI adoption.
