# Design as Both Science and Art
> 

Design is not just about aesthetics or technical skill, but also about the ability to solve problems effectively with the user at the center of attention. Its essence lies in the combination of art and science, which distinguishes designers from artists—while art is introspective, design is focused on the needs of others.

The real [goal of design is to create solutions](https://medium.com/@symbiote.dev.io/thinking-design-creativity-1c1fb9b2918f) that are functional, long-lasting, and adaptable. Designers combine creativity, analytical thinking, and empathy to meet the diverse needs of users and develop products or services that have an impact. Design is thus a bridge between people's problems and their solutions.
