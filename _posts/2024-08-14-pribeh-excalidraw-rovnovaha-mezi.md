Miloš Vetešník, co-founder of the [Excalidraw](https://excalidraw.com/) tool, in an interview for the Robotí podcast [describes the journey](https://www.fakturoid.cz/blog/2024/08/07/podcast-excalidraw) from failed projects to the success with Excalidraw. The key to their success was the ability to create a simple but functional product that solves specific user problems.

> "We created Excalidraw as open source, which allowed us to get feedback from the community and iterate on the product quickly."\
> **— Miloš Vetešník,** [Robotí podcast](https://www.fakturoid.cz/blog/2024/08/07/podcast-excalidraw)

Excalidraw, a tool for drawing diagrams and sketches, has found its way to millions of users worldwide thanks to its focus on simplicity and efficiency. The startup began without investors and was successfully bootstrapped through the correctly applied methodology of a minimum viable product (MVP), which allowed them to quickly test and validate their ideas.
