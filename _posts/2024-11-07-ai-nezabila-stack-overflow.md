# AI Didn't Kill Stack Overflow
> 

You've probably also seen on social media [depressive graphs of traffic](https://x.com/i/bookmarks?post_id=1854238332534108188) showing Stack Overflow's visits halved after ChatGPT's launch. [Stack Overflow refutes false reports](https://stackoverflow.blog/2023/08/08/insights-into-stack-overflows-traffic/) about a dramatic drop in its traffic. The statistics were skewed by a change in traffic measurement, where the update to Google Analytics 4 and stricter privacy settings caused traffic to appear lower than it actually is.

The actual drop in traffic is around 5%. Stack Overflow is preparing for a new era of technology, including generative AI, which is changing how developers solve problems and ask questions. The focus is shifting toward content quality, trust, and support for community interactions on the platform.
