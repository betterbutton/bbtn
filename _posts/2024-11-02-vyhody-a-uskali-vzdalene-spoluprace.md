# Advantages and Pitfalls of Remote Collaboration in UX
> 

Jonathon Juvenal describes [how his team dealt with the transition to remote work](https://medium.com/@jjuvenal/rebuilding-our-ux-team-collaboration-in-a-remote-world-e6409dd99235) and how they have stuck with it. The pandemic interrupted the natural collaboration of UX designers working in an open space. Jonathon therefore restored contact by introducing weekly online meetings on Zoom. Every Monday, the team meets to share what each did last week, their plans, and provide feedback.

The "last week, this week" format contributes to shared accountability, prioritization, and inspiration among designers. Thanks to regular critique and praise for successes, the team moved forward and gained space for creative development. This approach kept the team engaged and supported their growth despite remote work.
