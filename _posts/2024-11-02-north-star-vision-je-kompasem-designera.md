# North Star Vision is a designer's compass
> 

[North Star Vision](https://www.statsig.com/perspectives/steps-to-define-and-achieve-your-north-star-goal) represents the fundamental direction that every project should follow. Rupali Jain explains that a clear vision, which is understandable and inspiring, unites the team and motivates it to achieve a common goal.

„In UX, there is one golden rule: when you can show it, don't say it.“\
**— [Rupali Jain](https://medium.com/@rupalijain_/north-star-is-every-designers-compass-dc8a9006fc76)**

For examples, such as Netflix's vision ("Movies with one click, anytime, anywhere") or AirBnB ("Belong anywhere"), this clear idea simplified decision-making and directed product development in the right direction. According to Jain, designers should not only create visuals but also build [the future that others can enter](https://medium.com/@rupalijain_/north-star-is-every-designers-compass-dc8a9006fc76) and get excited about it.
