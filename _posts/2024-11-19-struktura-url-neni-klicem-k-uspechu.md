# URL Structure Is Not the Key to SEO Success
> 

John Mueller from Google [warns against over-focusing on URL structure](https://www.seroundtable.com/google-over-focus-on-url-structure-38398.html) when optimizing for search engines. He emphasizes that if a page's content is not clear and relevant, even an ideal URL structure won't save it. He recommends minimizing unnecessary URL changes, as these can temporarily disrupt SEO, and instead focusing on user-friendliness and long-term sustainability.

Website administrators should primarily use URL structure for performance monitoring, such as for analytics or conversion tracking. An exception where URL structure can significantly impact SEO are specific cases like pages with content filtering for adults.
