# (Updated) OpenAI's Advanced Voice Won't Comply with EU AI Act
> 

> **Update 2024-10-22:** It seems OpenAI just needed more time to ensure compliance with European regulations. As of today, the [Advanced Voice Mode is also available under the jurisdiction of the European AI law](https://x.com/OpenAI/status/1848575114578657290). ([Lupa.cz](https://www.lupa.cz/aktuality/openai-zprovoznila-rozsireny-hlasovy-rezim-v-chatgpt-i-uzivatelum-v-eu/))

OpenAI is expanding [Advanced Voice Mode](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/8400625-voice-mode-faq) for paying ChatGPT Plus and Teams users. Enterprise and Edu clients will get it next week. The feature comes with a redesigned interface and five new voices: Arbor, Maple, Sol, Spruce, and Vale.

Overall, ChatGPT now has nine nature-inspired voices. Greater customization is enabled by the Custom Instructions and Memory features. Although there have been improvements in understanding accents, video and screen sharing features are still missing.

![Paragraph 44 of the AI Act with a highlighted passage: The marketing, deployment, or use of AI systems intended to detect the emotional state of individuals in work and education-related contexts should therefore be prohibited. ](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/cc873b01-16ee-40f6-bfe5-fe665f033ee1_857x390.png "857x390")

[AI Act, paragraph 44](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/CS/TXT/HTML/?uri=OJ:L_202401689#:~:text=Uv%C3%A1d%C4%9Bn%C3%AD%20na%C2%A0trh%2C%20uv%C3%A1d%C4%9Bn%C3%AD%20do%C2%A0provozu%20nebo%20pou%C5%BE%C3%ADv%C3%A1n%C3%AD%20syst%C3%A9m%C5%AF%20AI%20ur%C4%8Den%C3%BDch%20k%C2%A0pou%C5%BEit%C3%AD%20za%C2%A0%C3%BA%C4%8Delem%20zji%C5%A1%C5%A5ov%C3%A1n%C3%AD%20emo%C4%8Dn%C3%ADho%20stavu%20jednotlivc%C5%AF%20v%C2%A0situac%C3%ADch%20souvisej%C3%ADc%C3%ADch%20s%C2%A0prac%C3%AD%20a%C2%A0vzd%C4%9Bl%C3%A1v%C3%A1n%C3%ADm%20by%20proto%20m%C4%9Blo%20b%C3%BDt%20zak%C3%A1z%C3%A1no.)

AVM is not yet available in the EU, the UK, and other countries. In the European Union, it likely runs into [paragraph 44 of the AI Act](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/CS/TXT/HTML/?uri=OJ:L_202401689#:~:text=Uv%C3%A1d%C4%9Bn%C3%AD%20na%C2%A0trh%2C%20uv%C3%A1d%C4%9Bn%C3%AD%20do%C2%A0provozu%20nebo%20pou%C5%BE%C3%ADv%C3%A1n%C3%AD%20syst%C3%A9m%C5%AF%20AI%20ur%C4%8Den%C3%BDch%20k%C2%A0pou%C5%BEit%C3%AD%20za%C2%A0%C3%BA%C4%8Delem%20zji%C5%A1%C5%A5ov%C3%A1n%C3%AD%20emo%C4%8Dn%C3%ADho%20stavu%20jednotlivc%C5%AF%20v%C2%A0situac%C3%ADch%20souvisej%C3%ADc%C3%ADch%20s%C2%A0prac%C3%AD%20a%C2%A0vzd%C4%9Bl%C3%A1v%C3%A1n%C3%ADm%20by%20proto%20m%C4%9Blo%20b%C3%BDt%20zak%C3%A1z%C3%A1no.), which restricts AI that detects user emotions. The question is whether OpenAI will solve the problem or leave part of the market without advanced voice features.

Tomáš Kapler advises that if you want to try Advanced Voice Mode, you should [use a VPN](https://www.kapler.cz/openai-chatgpt-advanced-voice-ai-asistent/#:~:text=Jak%20rozchodit%20Advanced%20Voice%20i%20v%20%C4%8Cesku%3F).
