# UX design in decline
> 

UX design is experiencing a paradoxical period: [popularity of the field is growing, but job opportunities are stagnating](https://medium.com/@tanlisarah/is-the-age-of-ux-design-over-4af4259b2920), which leads many designers to uncertainty and reflections on the meaning of their role. Sarah Tan Li analyzes the causes of disillusionment and highlights hope as a key designer trait.

„Being a UX designer kinda sucks right now — are things going to change?“\
**— [Sarah Tan Li](https://medium.com/@tanlisarah/is-the-age-of-ux-design-over-4af4259b2920)**

Sarah claims the current crisis is temporary and that design has the ability to adapt and expand its reach. The challenge for the future is to move beyond today's understanding of UX and drive change not just at the product level, but also within organizations and societal systems. Designers should be bold and redefine their role in a changing world.
