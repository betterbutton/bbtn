# Quality product from the start instead of a weak MVP?

> 

Traditional startup practices, such as rapid MVP launches and aggressive outbound, are [being replaced by more effective strategies](https://www.growthunhinged.com/p/the-emerging-startup-playbook). Modern startups, like Copy.ai or Jam, focus on a quality product from the start, authentic storytelling, and building an audience even before launch.

Key is retention capability and word of mouth, not just achieving a million dollars in annual recurring revenue. The focus shifts to automation, AI, and resource optimization, which reduces the need for large teams and capital. This new approach allows startups to scale sustainably, remain independent of external funding, and achieve long-term success.
