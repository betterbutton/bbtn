# Creative Compass of Michelle Losekoot
> 

> „The creative compass will help when you want to create but don't know what.“\
> **– [Michelle Losekoot](https://www.forendors.cz/p/749724343185052151)**

Michelle is celebrating her birthday and for the celebration, she gifted herself a [Creative Compass](https://www.forendors.cz/p/749724343185052151). The compass serves as a guide. It is meant to help everyone who dreams of realizing creative projects, from writing books to making ceramics. The compass offers support in finding ideas, motivation, and time. The goal is to turn creative dreams into reality, overcome fears, and find joy in creating.

We won't find out what the compass that Michelle filled out for herself looks like, but you can [download the template and fill it out](https://www.forendors.cz/p/749724343185052151) with your own creative direction.
