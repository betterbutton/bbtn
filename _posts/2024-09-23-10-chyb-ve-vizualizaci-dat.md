# 10 Data Visualization Mistakes That Can Catch You Off Guard
> 

When we have this post-election Monday, let's focus for a moment on data presentations. Data visualization is meant to transform raw data into clear and actionable information. Yanisa Treesak has compiled [an overview of the ten most common mistakes in data visualization](https://medium.com/agoda-engineering/10-common-data-visualization-mistakes-and-how-to-avoid-them-e3896fe8e104) and offers a way to avoid them:

1.  Confusing color selection

2.  Overwhelming charts with too much data

3.  Omitting the baseline and truncating the scale

4.  Non-objective text adjustments

5.  Choosing an unsuitable visualization method

6.  Correlation without context

7.  Highlighting data that suits you

8.  Common human visual associations

9.  Incorrect use of 3D graphics

10. Not every insight needs to be displayed in a data visualization
