# How Nielsen's Usability Heuristics Came to Be
> 

Jakob Nielsen, a pioneer of user-friendliness, created his final [list of 10 usability heuristics](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/i/144652153/zivot-s-technologiemi) in 1994 based on a statistical analysis of a database of problems from real projects. The [original 1989 list of heuristics](https://dl.acm.org/doi/pdf/10.1145/77481.77486), compiled with Jakob Nielsen by [Rolf Molich](https://www.dialogdesign.dk/about-rolf-molich/), served for teaching but was not research-based.

After five years of research and developing methods, Nielsen distilled hundreds of principles and problems using factor analysis into 10 key heuristics that have stood the test of time and are still used today. Although some terms (like "heuristics") may seem outdated, Nielsen believes in their enduring relevance.
