# How small improvements enhanced the volby.cz website
> 

![Screenshot of the volby.cz website with results of the 2024 regional council elections](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/a413789f-ea44-4911-a307-e245bbc32642_1895x1077.png "1456x827")

At our place in Smíchov and Dejvice, no voting took place over the weekend, so we had the opportunity to focus better not only on the election results but also on how news websites serve us the results. The big leap was made directly by [the volby.cz website](https://www.volby.cz/)!

Klára Scholleová [said on our behalf](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7243261532883533825/): *“None of this is a redesign in the sense that many might imagine — i.e., some revolution from the ground up including visuals to make it prettier. No, in the end it's not a revolution, but a well-chosen and, I believe, not insanely expensive evolution.”*

A few adjustments in typography, larger active area for links and buttons, and mobile-first forms were enough, and the website became much more usable, especially on mobile. The improvements are decent and not a revolution, but a thoughtful and cost-effective evolution, for which ČSÚ deserves praise. The overall impression of the improvements is very positive.
