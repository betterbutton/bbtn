# Secrets of perfect dashboards
> 

Creating visualizations that can attract and inspire is the way to effectively convey data information. [The author emphasizes](https://medium.com/@aashutoshrai85/creating-dashboards-that-dazzle-a-guide-to-effective-visualizations-bddb3085e763) the importance of knowing the audience, simplicity, consistency, and design relevance. The foundation is choosing the right visualizations - from graphs to interactive elements like filters.

Design tips include strategic use of colors, simple fonts, and plenty of white space. Recommended tools include Tableau, Power BI, and Looker. Finally, the author warns against data overload, ignoring feedback, and neglecting mobile optimization.
