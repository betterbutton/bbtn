# Does it matter for SEO whether content is created by a human?
> 

*"Is there a difference in SEO between AI content and what your copywriter writes?"* [asks Zdeněk Dvořák on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7218567483169902592/).

Currently, there is often discussion about whether there is a difference in SEO between content created by AI and content written by a human copywriter. The answer is mostly no, especially regarding topic synthesis. AI can process a topic faster and cheaper than a human, and will soon be able to handle tone as well, if you focus on creating the right prompt.

The added value of human output lies in the details: unique data, opinions, perspectives, user experience, page design, and text structure. These factors can influence Google's evaluation, either directly or indirectly through user behavior.

AI can significantly improve the structure and readability of manually written text. It's time for content creators to learn these techniques, whether they write themselves or with AI assistance.
