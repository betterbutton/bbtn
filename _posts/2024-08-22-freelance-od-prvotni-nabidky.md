# Freelance Designer: From Initial Offer to Final Project Proposal
> 

Kristina Volchek explains [how to properly proceed with creating an initial offer and pricing a project for clients](https://blog.kristi.digital/p/freelance-designers-initial-quote-project-proposal). The first offer should be flexible and serve to align expectations, while the final offer contains a detailed breakdown of work, a schedule, and a fixed price.

Kristina recommends first preparing the initial offer, which can be sent to the client after an introductory meeting, and preparing the final proposal only after the client agrees to the basic terms. This approach saves time and increases the chance of the proposal being accepted.
