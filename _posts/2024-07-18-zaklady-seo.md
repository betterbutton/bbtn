# Basic Orientation in SEO and a Few Fun Memories
> 

Honza Barbořík [introduces SEO to people](https://www.samsobemarketerem.cz/p/jak-na-seo-jednoduse), who are just trying to get into it or simply need to orient themselves so they can cooperate with specialists. It touches on [technical optimization](https://taste.cz/ebook-technicke-seo), [page factors](https://www.strafelda.cz/on-page-faktory), and [environment factors](https://www.marketingminer.com/cs/blog/off-page-seo.html).

In the introduction of the article, he recalls the beginnings of content search on the internet, when full-text search engines did not serve for discovering new web pages, but [catalogs](https://www.lupa.cz/clanky/seznam-chce-konkurovat-zlatym-strankam/). Catalogs were often sorted alphabetically, and thus as a fun reminder of that time, we have companies that started optimization for first positions in catalogs and phone directories right from their name: for example, AAA Auto Praha secured the first position with three A's at the beginning of the name.

After reading some instructions on search engine optimization, SEO might seem complicated, but as [Honza's article](https://www.samsobemarketerem.cz/p/jak-na-seo-jednoduse) also shows, at its core, it's about pages being relevant, understandable, user-friendly, and having authority.
