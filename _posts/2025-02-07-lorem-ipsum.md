# Lorem ipsum
> Lorem ipsum is a term for dummy text used in web and app design to test layouts. It helps visualize the design where content is missing.

**Lorem ipsum** is the best-known variant of [dummy text](https://cestina20.cz/slovnik/slepy-text/) (also called [filler text](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Filler_text), dummy text, or placeholder text) for designing websites and applications. It helps test visual hierarchy and allows you to focus on the design rather than the content.

The use of dummy text in design is considered a major faux pas among designers. Content creation for a website should precede design, and designers should work with real text. Designs created with dummy text cause problems for copywriters because dummy text rarely accurately reflects real needs regarding text length and structure.

Dummy text appeared with the rise of printing in the 16th century. [Type foundries](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/P%C3%ADsmolijectv%C3%AD) used it for font specimen sheets. Typesetters would grab random text they had on hand in the [print shop](https://bara.ujc.cas.cz/psjc/search.php?heslo=saz%C3%A1rna&zobraz_ps=ps&zobraz_cards=cards&pocet_karet=50). Over time, a Latin text based on Cicero's work [De finibus bonorum et malorum](https://archive.org/search?query=De+finibus+bonorum+et+malorum) became standard. The text served only as an example for typesetters, who were often not proficient in Latin, causing Cicero's text to become corrupted over time.

Lorem ipsum, based on Latin, looks unnatural at first glance. It contains no diacritics and doesn't reflect the length of Czech words. If you need a more accurate text simulation, use a generator like [Dummy CZ](https://wellstyled.com/tools/dummy-cz/), which is adapted for Czech. You can also create various dummy text variants using the [Blind Text Generator](https://www.blindtextgenerator.com/). For more creative designs, something like [Hipster Ipsum](https://hipsum.co/), which uses modern and informal phrasing, works well.

A relatively new option is to use artificial intelligence. It can generate texts tailored to a specific topic and in the required length and structure. Tools like [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/) or [Deep AI Text Generator](https://deepai.org/chat/text-generator) allow you to generate meaningful excerpts as needed. With AI-generated dummy text, there's a higher risk that no one will ever replace it with real content.

If dummy text remains on a website in production, it looks unprofessional. It can even harm SEO or make testing real content difficult. Such errors happen more often than you might think. Lorem ipsum is often overlooked in metadata, such as image alt text, meta descriptions, tooltips, footers, and the like.
