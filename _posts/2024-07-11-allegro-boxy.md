# WeDo is changing to One by Allegro and is setting up delivery boxes
> 

WeDo couriers are rebranding to [One by Allegro](https://www.wedo.cz/onebyallegro), thereby following the brand unification across markets. Allegro is simultaneously launching its own delivery boxes under the name [One Box](https://allegro.cz/kampan/one). They already have 140 of them. [Media Guru writes more about it.](https://www.mediaguru.cz/clanky/2024/07/allegro-spousti-v-cesku-vlastni-vydejni-boxy-wedo-rebranduje/)

The Allegro Group, originally from Poland, operates e-shops Mall and CZC in the Czech Republic.
