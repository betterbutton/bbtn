# Design Sprint and Design Thinking: How to Choose the Right Approach?
> 

Design Sprint and Design Thinking are [two methods for innovative problem solving](https://medium.com/@sgynaaa/design-sprint-vs-design-thinking-choosing-the-right-approach-for-innovation-8992105bdfde), each with a different focus. The Design Sprint, lasting five days, is a structured and fast-paced process focused on testing ideas with real users. Design Thinking, on the other hand, is an iterative approach that allows for a deep understanding of users and the redefinition of problems.

Design Sprint is ideal for rapid validation under time constraints, while Design Thinking excels at solving complex challenges requiring radical innovation. The choice depends on the project's goals, timeline, and available resources.
