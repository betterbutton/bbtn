# Advantages of Design Tokens and Their Implementation
> 

[Design tokens](https://www.lightningdesignsystem.com/design-tokens/), first mentioned by Salesforce in 2016, are the foundation of modern design systems. They are used to store values that define the visual description and placement of objects and allow maintaining design consistency even during its expansion. [They are useful from components up to entire pages.](https://medium.com/@xd_community_eng/working-with-design-tokens-94519ba9cf23)

They are divided into global, alias, and component levels, which simplifies changes and collaboration between designers and developers. For effective work with them, the correct structure, naming, and documentation are key. Tools like [Token Studio](https://tokens.studio/) facilitate the management and synchronization of tokens across teams and platforms.
