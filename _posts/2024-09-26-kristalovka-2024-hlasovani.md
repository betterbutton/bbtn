# Crystal Loupe 2024 Voting Has Started
> 

The Crystal Loupe 2024 poll has started and you [can vote](https://kristalova.lupa.cz/hlasovani) for the most popular internet projects in eight categories. Another nine categories will be judged by an expert jury.

Voting runs until November 1, and the winners will be announced on November 28 at the Palmovka Theatre. The Crystal Loupe brings not only an overview of the best internet projects but also personalities of the Czech internet.
