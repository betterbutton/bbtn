# Will Seznam Go to the Prague Stock Exchange?
> 

Owner of Seznam Ivo Lukačovič stirred up capital market commentators when he wrote on Patreon that even though it's not rational, [he would like to list Seznam's shares on the Prague stock exchange](https://www.patreon.com/posts/seznam-ipo-meho-110458385). The decision about [the Initial Public Offering](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/Initial_public_offering) of Seznam is reportedly one of the hardest steps in Ivo's career. Despite almost 30 years of business experience, he realizes that both intuition and reason can fail. He mentions his successes, but also the risks that an IPO brings, because there's no going back. He admits that even though Seznam's IPO might seem like a good step, its success cannot be guaranteed.

Ivo's post on Patreon was commented on by almost all media, and a mention of it appeared even in stock market news.
