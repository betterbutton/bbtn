# A Guide to Selecting Methods for UX Research
> 

UX research strategy is key to designing products that resonate with users. Its goal is to systematically uncover user needs, behaviors, and pain points, delivering actionable insights for designers. This strategy encompasses methods such as in-depth interviews, usability testing, or a combination of qualitative and quantitative research. Without it, there is a risk of wasted resources, poor decisions, and unsuccessful products. A focus on research facilitates understanding users and ensures the design meets their expectations and cultural context. The result? [Better, more effective, and more successful products.](https://medium.com/@jesus.ortegads/create-a-ux-research-strategy-db5264328d12)
