# Career change from UX to product management
> 

Josephine Palberg describes her [transition from UX design to product management](https://medium.com/@josephine.keil/from-co-pilot-to-pilot-my-journey-from-ux-design-to-product-management-5af1a04c0827), where she discovered a new level of control over product development. While previously as a UX designer she helped users navigate their journey, as a product manager she now sets the goal and takes care of the overall strategy.

> „As a UX designer, I helped with navigation, but I wanted to determine where we're flying.“\
> — **[Josephine Palberg](https://medium.com/@josephine.keil/from-co-pilot-to-pilot-my-journey-from-ux-design-to-product-management-5af1a04c0827)**

Although the new role brings unexpected challenges, Josephine finds greater satisfaction and a sense of fulfillment in product management.
