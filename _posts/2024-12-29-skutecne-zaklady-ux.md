# The Real Basics of UX
> 

UX design isn't just about wireframes and usability — its true core lies in emotions and psychology, writes [Veera Kumar](https://x.com/VeerakumarRama2). Instead of repeating tired rules like user-friendliness and accessibility, focus on creating deep emotional connections with users. The best UX isn't just functional, but also evokes a sense of satisfaction and fulfillment. The real basics of UX begin outside the product and end in the user's mind, [bringing joy and connection](https://medium.com/@ramakrishnanveerakumar/ux-fundamentals-66a3ebd94429).
