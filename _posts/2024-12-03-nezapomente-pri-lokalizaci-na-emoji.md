# Don't Forget About Emojis When Localizing
> 

Localizing content goes beyond mere text translation – it requires respecting cultural customs. This includes visual elements like icons or emojis. Symbols like thumbs up 👍, thumbs down 👎, or the OK gesture 👌 can have fundamentally different meanings across cultures – from misunderstanding to offense.

> „In Australia, the v-for-victory gesture means the same as giving someone the middle finger in the United States.“
> **— [Eric W. Baily](https://ericwbailey.website/published/dont-forget-to-localize-your-icons/)**

For example, the OK gesture 👌 can be perceived as a threat due to its misuse by extremist groups. Poorly chosen icons can undermine trust and provoke negative reactions. Careful research and adaptation of visuals to specific cultures can [significantly improve user experience and show genuine care.](https://ericwbailey.website/published/dont-forget-to-localize-your-icons/)
