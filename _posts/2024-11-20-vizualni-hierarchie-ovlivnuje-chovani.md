# Visual Hierarchy Influences User Behavior
> 

Visual hierarchy is a fundamental principle of UI design that [guides the user's attention to the most important elements on the screen](https://medium.com/@pjay200230/the-role-of-visual-hierarchy-in-ui-design-guiding-user-attention-143a9ab571d7). A well-chosen hierarchy increases efficiency and user satisfaction. The basic tools for creating visual hierarchy are size, color, and contrast: larger elements attract more attention, contrasting colors emphasize important information, and proper use of contrast improves readability. Emphasis on accessibility is crucial to ensure the design works for all users.
