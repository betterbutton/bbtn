# Report suspicious SMS to 7726
> 

All three main Czech telecommunications operators [O₂](https://www.o2.cz/podpora/volani-z-mobilu/linka-pro-preposilani-podvodnych-sms), [T-Mobile](https://www.t-mobile.cz/microsites/klik-pro-klid/index.html#bezpecnostni-desatero-2), and [Vodafone](https://www.vodafone.cz/pece/muj-ucet-cislo/bezpecnost/spoofing-podvrzene-hovory/) have already introduced the number 7726 for reporting fraudulent activities in their networks. If you notice suspicious calls or text messages, **forward the details to 7726** about the sender or caller, time of the event, and content of the message. They will verify the activity and possibly prevent its further spread. You can report activities even if the originator is abroad.
