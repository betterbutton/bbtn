Young entrepreneur Jiří Rostecký invited Dalibor Pulkert to a podcast two weeks ago [invited Dalibor Pulkert to a podcast](https://mladypodnikatel.cz/psychologie-v-podnikani-dalibor-pulkert-t50631). [Dalibor Pulkert](https://www.linkedin.com/in/daliborpulkert/?locale=cs_CZ) is a managing partner at [Outboxers](https://www.outboxers.com/) and recently published the book [30 hours](https://www.melvil.cz/kniha-30-hodin/). According to Dalibor, this is the time you need to validate a business idea.

> "I took on the role of curator of individual practical tools. And I put them together with some examples from practice, so that if I were reading it 15 years ago, it would help me."
> **— Dalibor Pulkert, [Psychology in Business: How Our Head Reduces Chances of Success?](https://youtu.be/d7Vm1PW3_io?t=98)**

Jiří and Dalibor discuss the importance of psychology in business, the obstacles that starting entrepreneurs face, and how to overcome them. Dalibor advises on how to surround yourself with the right people and learn from diverse entrepreneurs at different career stages.
