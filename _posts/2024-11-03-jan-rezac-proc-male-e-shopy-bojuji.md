Jan Řezáč, an expert on Czech e-commerce, highlights in the Socials podcast the [challenges that Czech e-shops will face](https://podcasters.spotify.com/pod/show/socialspodcast/episodes/44---Jan-ez---Vlka-e-shop--trit-a-budoucnost-e-commerce-e2oikht). According to him, the key is how they manage to create a competitive advantage, e.g., a brand or unique products. Growing marketplaces like Alza, Heuréka, or Amazon, which change the rules of the game, have a significant influence on the market. Řezáč also warns against blind trust in quick website redesigns: "A nice e-shop alone won't bring orders." He sees the future in adapting to new technologies, including AI assistants, and in focusing on customer experience and thorough strategy.
