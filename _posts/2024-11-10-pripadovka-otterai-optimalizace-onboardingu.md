# Case Study Otter.ai: Optimizing Onboarding and Increasing User Activation
> 

Rosie Hoggmascall analyzes how the tool [Otter.ai](https://otter.ai/) effectively [works with onboarding](https://uxdesign.cc/reducing-time-to-aha-moment-in-b2b-saas-9b5ccdf93658) to engage new users more quickly and lead them to the *aha moment*. The main practices include step-by-step task assignment during setup, something Rosie calls a monetization sandwich with billing at both the beginning and end of onboarding, and an emphasis on calendar synchronization as an indicator of activation and retention.
