# UptimeRobot: Fast and Accessible Website Monitoring
> A tool for monitoring website availability with fast alerts, a generous free plan, and easy setup for developers and businesses.

![Screenshot of UptimeRobot's homepage.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/41894046-732b-45f8-a47b-3236f8c88485_1894x1077.png "610x346")

[UptimeRobot](https://uptimerobot.com/) monitors server downtime and performance.

[UptimeRobot](https://uptimerobot.com/) is a popular service for monitoring website availability. It offers HTTP, HTTPS, ping, port, keyword, SSL certificate, and domain expiration checks. On the free plan, it checks every five minutes; on higher tiers, as often as every thirty seconds.

The interface is clear and intuitive, making monitoring setup a matter of moments. Alerts are sent via email, mobile app, Slack, Telegram, Microsoft Teams, and more. Paid plans add SMS, voice calls, multi-user support, and the ability to create a custom status page.

Even the free plan allows monitoring up to fifty services, outperforming most competitors. Basic availability overviews and incident history are available. However, in the free version, data is retained for a shorter period, and advanced analytics are only available in paid versions.

Users often praise its reliability, simplicity, and excellent value for money. Compared to competitors like [BetterStack](https://betterstack.com/) or [StatusCake](https://www.statuscake.com/), UptimeRobot is significantly cheaper and offers more features even in its basic version.

A drawback is that it does not offer real user monitoring or simulated transaction checks. For frequent checks or frequent SMS alerts, upgrading to a paid plan is necessary.

For developers, support teams, and small businesses needing reliable downtime alerts without complex setup and high costs, UptimeRobot is an excellent choice.
