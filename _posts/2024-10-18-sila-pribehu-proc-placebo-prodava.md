# The Power of Stories: Why Placebo Sells Better Than Reality
> 

The placebo effect is not just a medical phenomenon, but also a powerful tool that marketing uses to influence our decisions, [writes Ondřej Ilinčev on the blog](https://www.ilincev.com/brand-jako-placebo). Psychology convinces us that a more expensive or more colorful product works better, even if its effects are identical to a cheaper alternative.

> "Placebo is the story by which we change the way we perceive reality."\
> — **[Ondřej Ilinčev](https://www.ilincev.com/brand-jako-placebo)**

The higher we are on [Maslow's hierarchy of needs](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maslowova_pyramida#:~:text=Maslowova%20pyramida%20(pot%C5%99eb)%20is%20the%20hierarchy,lower%20in%20terms%20of%20its%20value.), the more we care about how we feel rather than what we truly need. Luxury brands and regular products influence us with precisely crafted stories and rituals. Marketers know how to make good use of people's tendency to believe in stories that bring us satisfaction and a sense of exclusivity.
