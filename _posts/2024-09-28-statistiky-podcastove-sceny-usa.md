# US Podcast Scene Statistics
> 

[This year's US podcast scene](https://www.edisonresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/The-Podcast-Consumer-2024-Presentation.pdf) reached a record number of listeners – up to 67% of the American population over 12 has listened to a podcast at some point. Regular monthly podcast listening increased to 47%, while weekly reached 34%. Podcast listeners are spending more time listening, with the share of podcasts in total time spent on audio listening rising from 2% in 2014 to 20% in 2024.

Podcasts appeal to all generations and are becoming increasingly diverse – the number of women and listeners from various ethnic groups is growing. This is a highly attractive group for advertisers.
