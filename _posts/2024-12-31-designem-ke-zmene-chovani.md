# Design for Behavior Change
> 

Behavior change strategies in design are key to creating intuitive and effective user experiences. Doni Kusuma emphasizes [five proven approaches](https://medium.com/@kusumadoni77/behavior-change-strategies-practical-insights-for-everyday-design-1c272b8657f6):

- simplifying tasks (reducing friction),
- using social proof,
- rewarding progress,
- setting clear goals and
- active reminders (nudge).

These techniques help users achieve their goals naturally and with a sense of reward. It is important to avoid overloading users or offering inappropriate motivations. All strategies aim to support users on their path to what they already want to achieve and to create friendly and valuable interactions.
