# FontJoy pairs fonts left rear
> Fontjoy is a free online tool that uses artificial intelligence to automatically design harmonious font combinations for design, web, and branding.

![Screenshot of the FontJoy tool pairing three fonts: heading, subheading, and text. On the scale, you can choose how much the selected combinations should contrast stylistically.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/f4ee2617-fb2f-477b-a893-3ddca3932fa1_1901x1077.png "1456x825")

[FontJoy](https://fontjoy.com/) combines fonts

[Fontjoy.com](https://fontjoy.com/) is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to find font combinations. Using machine learning, it analyzes font properties and suggests pairings that balance contrast and cohesion. You can generate automatic suggestions, lock a specific font, manually select alternatives, and customize the [lorem ipsum](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/lorem-ipsum) for a realistic preview. With the slider, you can decide for yourself whether you want the fonts to harmonize or contrast more.

Fontjoy is ideal for both web design and print graphics. It has a simple interface and smart suggestions, making the selection of the right fonts easy and fast.
