# Easy UTM Tag Generator for clear tracking of campaign performance in Google Analytics
> Easy UTM Tag Generator helps create campaigns with UTM parameters without complications, with just clean and clear data.

![Screenshot of the Easy UTM Tag Generator tool](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/acb4fa09-d197-49ad-be7c-ed3d9eec3be1_1900x1075.png "1456x824")

[Easy UTM Tag Generator](https://gearside.com/utm/) adds campaign labels to the link.

[Simple UTM tag generator](https://gearside.com/utm/) helps marketers, developers, and analysts quickly create properly labeled campaign links. Just enter the basic data – for example, source, medium, and campaign name – and the tool will generate a ready-to-use link, prepared for measurement in Google Analytics and other platforms.

The tool simplifies work by providing explanations and examples for each field. Required fields, such as the target address or traffic source, are clearly marked. Optionally, you can add additional parameters like content, search terms, or new tags for GA4 – for example `utm_id` or `source_platform`.

Just click and you have a ready link with parameters. If you want consistent labeling, you can automatically convert text to lowercase, replace spaces with underscores, or unify names for email campaigns. This significantly reduces the risk of inaccuracies in the outputs of analytics tools.

UTM tags are crucial for determining where traffic actually comes from. Without them, it often gets grouped into direct traffic or other, making campaign effectiveness evaluation much harder.

Whether you manage a single link or an extensive campaign across multiple channels, this tool will help you set everything up correctly – simply, quickly, and without errors.

If you care about clear and usable data from your campaigns, this tool should be part of your toolkit.
