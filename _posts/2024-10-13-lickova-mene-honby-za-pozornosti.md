# Ličková: Less Chasing for Attention and More Content
> 

Tina Ličková [critically comments](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/less-spotlight-more-substance-my-call-uxr-community-tina-li%C4%8Dkov%C3%A1-l3e9f/?trackingId=jWA6KJzrSg6ReF%2BUc%2FVHtg%3D%3D) on the current state of the UXR community, which, in her view, focuses too much on self-promotion and chasing attention instead of providing real value. The number of *UXR gurus* and repetitive superficial content is growing.

> „I don’t want to be part of this whole '𝘐’𝘮 𝘢 𝘜𝘟𝘙 𝘴𝘶𝘱𝘦𝘳𝘴𝘵𝘢𝘳, 𝘭𝘦𝘵 𝘮𝘦 𝘵𝘦𝘭𝘭 𝘺𝘰𝘶 𝘩𝘰𝘸 𝘵𝘰 𝘥𝘰 𝘳𝘦𝘴𝘦𝘢𝘳𝘤𝘩' trend. It’s exhausting…“\
> — **[Tina Ličková](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/less-spotlight-more-substance-my-call-uxr-community-tina-li%C4%8Dkov%C3%A1-l3e9f/?trackingId=jWA6KJzrSg6ReF%2BUc%2FVHtg%3D%3D)**

Tina wants to focus on authentic connection with local communities and give space to lesser-known experts, rather than feed algorithms with content that is more for commercial purposes. She appeals to the community to exercise greater caution when choosing educational resources and to adopt a critical approach to UXR markets focused on the USA.
