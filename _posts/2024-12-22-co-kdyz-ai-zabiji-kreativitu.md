# What if AI kills creativity?
> 

With the development of generative AI tools, designers face the risk of losing key aspects of their work, such as creating deep insights and building team alignment. Tools like Figma and Miro accelerate the data synthesis process, but according to the author, they are superficial shortcuts that lack the depth of human understanding. The key value of design – finding breakthrough ideas and the ability to create stories that inspire action – may disappear. The author emphasizes the need to adopt AI tools with caution to avoid the 'doorman effect', where [efficiency overshadows irreplaceable human contributions](https://medium.com/learning-in-the-open/what-if-generative-ai-tools-are-gradually-killing-design-99fdb0d859a0).
