# Augmented analytics with AI changes data work
> 

Artificial intelligence in analytics enables significantly simplifying and speeding up data work. [Tomáš Kapler summarized his lecture in an article](https://www.kapler.cz/augmented-analytics-ai-v-datove-analytice/) from the Data Restart conference. He presents several practical examples of using AI for data cleaning and preparation using the Gemini model or data analysis with ChatGPT.

These tools can identify deficiencies in data sets, suggest visualizations, and perform customer segmentation according to RFM analysis. Tableau Pulse is also discussed. It is an AI tool with prediction and explanation of key metrics. AI enables quickly understanding trends and making decisions based on current data.
