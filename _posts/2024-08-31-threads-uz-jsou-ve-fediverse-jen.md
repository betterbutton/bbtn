# Threads are already in the Fediverse. Just not in the EU.
> 

In recent days, users of the social network [Threads by Meta](https://threads.net/) have reported that reactions from users of decentralized social networks using the [ActivityPub](https://activitypub.rocks/) protocol—popularly known as the [Federated Universe](https://fediverse.party/) or Fediverse—are now appearing under their posts.

People from social networks like Mastodon, Pixelfield, PeerTube, Pleroma, Tumblr, and [others](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ActivityPub) can now follow profiles from Threads. [Just not in the EU](https://help.instagram.com/760878905943039), where Meta has not rolled out this feature.
