# The Rise of Emotional Design
> 

Emotional design has become an important element of UX, as it emphasizes how [users feel when using digital products](https://medium.com/@iharsharoyal/the-rise-of-emotional-design-crafting-experiences-that-connect-cea47d0c404e). According to [Donald Norman's theory](https://www.nngroup.com/books/emotional-design/), there are three levels of emotional design: visceral, behavioral, and reflective.

> "In today's competitive digital world, it's not enough for things to just 'work'; people also want to feel something when using them."\
> — **[Harsha Royal](https://medium.com/@iharsharoyal/the-rise-of-emotional-design-crafting-experiences-that-connect-cea47d0c404e)**
