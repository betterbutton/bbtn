# What UX Will Bring in 2025
> 

The end of the year is approaching. You know this by the fact that articles predicting trends for 2025 are starting to appear. For example, Ivan Medina claims that in 2025, UX will become a key element in [the success of digital products, where technologies like AI and AR will play a key role](https://medium.com/@medinamedia/the-future-of-user-experience-ux-what-to-expect-in-2025-81935a69639c). This time for real.

> “The shift to AR, VR, and voice interfaces will require UX designers to think beyond visual elements and optimize auditory and spatial interactions as well.”\
> **— [Ivan Medina](https://medium.com/@medinamedia/the-future-of-user-experience-ux-what-to-expect-in-2025-81935a69639c)**

According to Ivan, artificial intelligence will significantly enhance user interactions by predicting needs and enabling real-time personalization. UX design will expand into multi-sensory areas encompassing voice, gesture, and spatial elements.

Mass-scale personalization will bring challenges in ethics and privacy. Investing in quality UX will be essential for higher customer satisfaction, conversions, and loyalty, giving companies a competitive edge.

Antony S. adds that the future of UI will be shaped by artificial intelligence and virtual reality. While VR offers hyper-realistic environments, AI will enable interactivity that learns and adapts in real-time, boosting efficiency and user experience.

> “Artificial intelligence enables a dialogue that can significantly improve the user experience.”\
> **— [Antony S.](https://medium.com/@antony54389/the-future-of-user-interface-ui-7e23dcb12a0b)**

According to Antony, Apple Vision Pro provides the first glimpse of how UI can become more spatial. However, experts believe AI will likely drive more significant innovations than VR, as it can enhance user experience in real-world scenarios. The interfaces of the future will be primarily about what enables users greater efficiency and ease in completing tasks.
