# Is artistic bento the new visual trend in web design?
> 

![Screenshot of "artistic bento" from Intercom's website](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/d10421d1-f6d8-4c43-816c-e90739918728_604x604.png "604x604")

Artistic Bento on Intercom.com

[Charlota Kolář Blunárová noticed](https://x.com/0xCharlota/status/1807708565458550813), that recently a new visual trend has been appearing on the web, which consists of components grouped in a box with a white background and rounded corners on a background photo of the page (often generated using artificial intelligence). You can check out the new trend, for example, on [Figma's](https://www.figma.com/) or [Intercom's](https://www.intercom.com/) website.
