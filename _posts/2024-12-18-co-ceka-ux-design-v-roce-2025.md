# What to Expect from UX Design in 2025
> 

The year 2025 brings [fundamental changes to UX design thanks to AI, automation, and pressure on business results](https://webdesignerdepot.com/the-state-of-ux-in-2025-what-designers-need-to-know/). The role of designers is shifting from the creative process to metric-driven efficiency. AI tools are taking over routine tasks, but at the same time, there is a risk of suppressing human intuition and empathy.

> “The risk of a metric-driven approach is the transformation of UX into a numbers game, where user satisfaction takes a back seat.”\
> **— [Alex Harper](https://webdesignerdepot.com/the-state-of-ux-in-2025-what-designers-need-to-know/)**

Design is increasingly subordinated to business KPIs, which may sacrifice user satisfaction. Designers therefore need to develop strategic skills, understand user psychology, and preserve the creativity that AI cannot replace. Although algorithms control processes, the true power of UX remains in designing for people, not just for numbers.

A deeper dive into expected trends can be found in [The State of UX 2025](https://trends.uxdesign.cc/2025).
