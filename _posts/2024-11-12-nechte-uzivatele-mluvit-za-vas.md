# Let Users Speak for You
> 

> „Social proof is a powerful persuasion tool that stems from the idea that when people are unsure, they follow the behavior of others.“

[Social proof in UX design increases credibility](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/how-to-let-your-users-do-the-talking-7db868c41feb) by inspiring users to act based on the behavior of others. This psychological phenomenon influences decision-making, for example through high numbers of followers, positive reviews, or labels like 'most popular'. Thanks to social proof, users feel more confident when choosing products and services, which improves the overall user experience and strengthens brand loyalty.
