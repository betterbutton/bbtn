# Should Lukačovič lose part of Seznam?
> 

[Respect](https://www.respekt.cz/kontext/rekonstrukce-jak-strnad-krouzil-kolem-seznamu-a-proc-musel-jakub-unger-odejit-ze-seznam-zprav) and [Page not found](https://pagenotfound.cz/clanek/spinava-hra-o-seznam) come with the same speculations that arms dealer Michal Strnad (Czechoslovak Group) wanted to acquire the media part of Seznam. Allegedly he cooperated with editor-in-chief Jakub Unger, who was supposed to manipulate owner Ivo Lukačovič with false reports about attacks on the ÚOHS and involvement of billionaire Pavel Tykač.

> „My Indian name is: The one with nerves of steel“\
> **— [Ivo Lukačovič](https://x.com/ilblog/status/1862211756141011452)**

Unger was also supposed to arrange a meeting with Prime Minister Fiala to add weight to his claims. But Lukačovič began to doubt and Unger was suddenly recalled. Both Strnad and Unger deny the accusations, but according to sources, they wanted to gain control through pressure over the key media house that fundamentally influences the Czech internet.
