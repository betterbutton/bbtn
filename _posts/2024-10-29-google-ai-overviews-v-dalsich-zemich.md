# Google's AI Overviews in Search Are Coming to 100+ Countries. No, Not the EU.
> 

Google is expanding [AI Overviews in Search to more than 100 countries](https://blog.google/products/search/ai-overviews-search-october-2024/) and making them available in more languages. AI-generated overviews help users more easily find relevant information and websites, while also creating new opportunities for creators, businesses, and publishers.

> AI Overviews will have more than 1 billion global users every month.

Since their launch in May and expansion beyond the US in August, AI Overviews have received positive feedback. With this new update, they will now be available in more languages, such as Spanish, Japanese, or Indonesian, making search easier for users worldwide.

Among [European countries](https://support.google.com/websearch/answer/14901683?sjid=3911880977568570131-NA#:~:text=AI%20Overviews%20availability), AI Overviews have so far only been launched in the United Kingdom. EU countries are still standing outside. Nonetheless, this change is important for webmasters with an international audience. Optimizing for AI Overviews is becoming important. They first appeared [in the US in May](https://blog.google/products/search/generative-ai-google-search-may-2024/) and [since August in other major markets](https://blog.google/products/search/new-ways-to-connect-to-the-web-with-ai-overviews/#:~:text=United%20Kingdom%2C%20India%2C%20Japan%2C%20Indonesia%2C%20Mexico%20and%20Brazil) in the UK, India, Japan, Indonesia, Mexico, and Brazil. It's only a matter of time before Google addresses European legislative requirements and launches AI overviews in the EU as well.
