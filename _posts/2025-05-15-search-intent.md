# Search intent
> Search intent represents the intention that brings users to search. It influences keyword selection and sets satisfaction criteria.

People don't search for words, but for answers. If you have a page on your site that precisely matches what the user is looking for, search engines will offer it preferentially in the results. And that is precisely the meaning of what is called [search intent](https://www.strafelda.cz/search-intent) – the intention with which the user types a query into the search engine.

Most commonly, search intent is divided into three to four categories:

**Informational queries** ([informational](https://ahrefs.com/seo/glossary/informational-query)): The user is looking for an answer, explanation, or guide. Examples: *how to cook lentils*, *what is a design system*, *Bosch dishwasher manual*.

**Navigational queries** ([navigational](https://www.wordstream.com/navigational-query)): The user is looking for a specific page or brand. Examples: *idnes*, *spotify login*, *equa bank account*.

**Transactional queries** ([transactional](https://en.ryte.com/wiki/Transactional_Search_Query/)): The user wants to do something – buy, download, book, log in. Examples: *buy iphone 14 pro*, *tax return online*, *hotel Brno reservation*.

**Comparative or commercial queries** ([commercial](https://rankmath.com/seo-glossary/commercial-query/)): The user is comparing options before making a decision. Examples: *best running shoes reviews*, *wordpress vs webflow*, *operator price list 2025*.

Sometimes a fifth type is distinguished – **local queries** ([local search](https://aioseo.com/seo-glossary/local-search-queries/)): The user is looking for something in their vicinity or in a specific geographic area. Examples: *cafés on Anděl, dentist nearby, nearest pharmacy open nonstop*.

When optimizing for search engines, we try to understand what intention the user has behind a specific keyword. It's not just about getting your page to appear in the results – it must be the right answer. If the search intent does not match the type of content on the page, the results will be poor despite high traffic.
