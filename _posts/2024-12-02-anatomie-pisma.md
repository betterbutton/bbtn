# Anatomy of Type
> 

Can you name the parts of type characters in English? The anatomy of type reveals the individual parts that make up each character, such as the ear, stem, or arch. These elements fundamentally influence readability, aesthetics, and the design mood of the type. Knowledge of terminology allows for better analysis of typefaces, effective combination, and precise communication with colleagues or clients.

Spidey Sense offers an [overview of English typographic terminology](https://medium.com/@spideysense693/inside-typography-the-building-blocks-of-letterforms-00d693d7435f) and you can [compare it with a similar list in Czech](https://typomil.com/anatomie/) by Martin T. Pecina. Key components include, for example, x-height, terminal, or serif. For both beginner and experienced designers, understanding anatomy is crucial for selecting suitable typefaces and refining their application in various projects, from logos to readable texts.
