# Linkodrome

**Form and function go hand in hand.** Design is not just a matter of aesthetics nor just functionality – both aspects are inseparable and mutually influence each other. Every element, whether purely decorative or highly functional, has its form and purpose. Designers differ only in the emphasis they place on one or the other component. Good design strives for a balance between efficiency and aesthetics, [writes Petr Staníček on his renewed blog](https://www.you-ex.cz/forma-nebo-funkcnost/), where aesthetics is not just a question of appeal but also includes emotions, intention, and context. Bad design often stems more from poor specifications or an unsuitable choice of designer than from an inherent flaw in the design itself.

**Do not separate the accessible version from the website.** Radek Pavlíček writes that creating separate versions of websites for users with specific needs, such as the blind, has several problems. These versions often restrict access to content, require costly maintenance, and users may perceive them as a form of segregation. The correct solution is [to ensure accessibility directly in the main version of the website](https://poslepu.cz/proc-separatni-verze-webu-selhavaji-v-zajisteni-jeho-pristupnosti/) – from compatibility with assistive technologies to clear design and understandable content. Accessibility should be part of the overall design, not a separate solution. An exception may be a language version for the deaf in the form of videos in Czech Sign Language.

**How to approach a website version in Czech Sign Language.** [Creating a website accessible to the deaf](https://www.deaffriendly.cz/blog/jak-ma-vypadat-webova-stranka-pristupna-neslysicim) means more than just adding video translations of texts into Czech Sign Language. The structure, content, and placement of videos must be carefully thought out. Deaf people for whom Czech is a foreign language prefer information in sign language. The videos should be available in a separate section of the website labeled "Information in Czech Sign Language" and ideally supplemented with the symbol for Czech Sign Language. The page layout should be linear, with emphasis on video visibility [above the fold](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/above-the-fold). Content must be regularly updated and adapted to the specific target group.
