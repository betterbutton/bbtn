# Professor Mařík: AI is not a being, but a tool
> 

Radio Universum invited Professor Vladimír Mařík, scientific director of the Czech Institute of Informatics, Robotics and Cybernetics at CTU, for an interview. The interview was released in two parts immediately. [In the first one](https://www.radiouniversum.cz/marik-vladimir-1d-vyzkumy-ohrozujici-podstatu-zivota-cloveka-tedy-zasahy-do-genetickeho-kodu-by-se-nemely-povolit/) Professor Mařík says that research threatening the essence of human life, i.e., interventions into the genetic code, should not be allowed and [in the second one](https://www.radiouniversum.cz/marik-vladimir-2d-divejme-se-na-umelou-inteligenci-jako-na-nastroj-nikoliv-jako-na-entitu-ktera-by-zacala-expandovat/): "Let's look at artificial intelligence as a tool, not as an entity that would start to expand." That is, that AI is not a being but a tool. You may not agree with everything with Professor Mařík, but his view on the current state of science and research is refreshing.
