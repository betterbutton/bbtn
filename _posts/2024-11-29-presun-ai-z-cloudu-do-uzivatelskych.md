# The Shift of AI from Cloud to User Devices Changes Our Lifestyle
> 

The future of AI is moving toward decentralization – from data centers to the devices we always have on hand. Qualcomm and other companies are promoting lightweight AI models that enable running directly on mobile devices. These solutions offer advantages such as speed, personalization, and greater privacy protection.

> "Every technological change – from 2G to 5G – brings major innovations, especially in the area of personal devices."\
> **— Piampoon Kay**

Historical examples show how the transition to technologies like 4G changed lifestyles. Now we face another shift – AI as a new user interface (UI) that enables direct communication with technology. This will bring a revolution in applications and daily use thanks to generative and multimodal AI.

The article [Transformative Experience with Generative AI](https://medium.com/odds-team/transformative-experience-with-generative-ai-d2b2dd1c2434) is written in Thai, but it's worth getting it translated.
