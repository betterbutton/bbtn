# Gmail adds new cards for quick email actions
> 

Gmail is introducing new *[summarizing cards](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/15195630?hl=cs)* that will make it easier for users to manage incoming mail. The cards organize information about purchases, travel, or payments and enable quick actions, such as package tracking or flight check-in.

For us product designers, this means we should review transactional email templates and test whether summarizing cards are displayed for them, as they will soon become an important part of the user's brand experience.

The improvements [according to TechCrunch respond to Apple's competition](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/02/gmail-enhanced-summary-cards-help-you-take-action-on-your-emails/), which added similar features to the Mail app in iOS 18. The new dynamic cards update information in real-time and add features like setting payment reminders or sharing events. The features will start rolling out gradually in October 2024.
