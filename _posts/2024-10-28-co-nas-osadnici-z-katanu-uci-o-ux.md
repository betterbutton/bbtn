# What Settlers of Catan Teaches Us About UX
> 

The board game [Settlers of Catan](https://www.kosmos.de/de/content/Markenwelten/CATAN/CATAN-Markenwelt) offers [useful insights for UX design](https://medium.com/@nalindeabrew/3-ux-insights-from-the-catan-board-game-06e94a616818). Through [signifiers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Signified_and_signifier) in the form of visual cues that guide players, Settlers of Catan facilitates orientation without complex explanations. It also provides players with reference cards, reducing memory load—much like UX design should provide necessary information at every step.

The only shortcoming is the lack of a simple display of the game's state, leading to ambiguity. Similarly, in UX, a system should always inform the user of the current state.
