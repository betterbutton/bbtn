The Upside Down podcast in this episode [focuses on inclusive design and accessibility](https://www.vzhurudolu.cz/podcast/252-tereza-kosnarova). The guest was Tereza Kosnarová Venerová, who explains the differences between inclusive design and accessibility.

> „Disadvantage is not a specific need, it is a normal need with a different path to solution.“\ > **— [Tereza Kosnarová Venerová](https://www.vzhurudolu.cz/podcast/252-tereza-kosnarova)**

According to her, web accessibility is a basic technical obligation, while inclusive design focuses on creating comparable experiences for everyone, even outside the digital environment. Specific examples such as delivery of purchases or public services are discussed. Tereza also shares practical tips on how to start with accessibility and inclusive design, and emphasizes the importance of empathy in design. The call to develop competencies and examples of good practice show what impact this topic has on companies and users.
