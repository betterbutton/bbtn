# Bring prototypes to life with sound and video
> 

Incorporating sound and video will significantly improve prototypes, [says Ethan Oh](https://medium.com/@ethan.oh227/enhancing-high-fidelity-prototypes-with-sound-and-video-6c7abd28bb32). Sound and video enhance navigation, user engagement, and the overall application flow. Sound provides immediate feedback and emotional connection, but requires careful control to avoid being distracting.

Video, in turn, enriches the experience with immersion and longer interaction, while ensuring accessibility through captions and controls is key. The key to success is user testing, which helps find the balance between interactivity and user comfort.
