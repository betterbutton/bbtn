# Dark mode will bring more concentration and productivity
> 

Dark mode not only improves the appearance but also the functionality of your digital devices, says Sudarshan Somanathan in the article [Discover the Benefits of Dark Mode: Enhance Your Screen Time Experience](https://clickup.com/blog/benefits-of-dark-mode/). It reduces eye strain, saves battery, and improves concentration, especially in low light. The dark interface also increases readability and offers higher contrast, which users with various visual needs will appreciate.

Thanks to the smaller amount of blue light emitted by dark mode, it can also have a positive effect on sleep quality. Whether you're working, playing games, or reading at night, dark mode provides a more pleasant and efficient screen experience.
