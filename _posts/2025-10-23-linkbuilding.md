# Linkbuilding
> Acquiring links from other websites to improve a site's visibility, credibility, and findability in search results, conversations with artificial intelligence, and natural web browsing.

[Linkbuilding](https://www.strafelda.cz/linkbuilding) ([link building](https://www.semrush.com/blog/link-building/)) is important not only for SEO but also to be visible in AI-powered search and, ideally, to drive organic traffic. Search engines, including those based on conversational AI, rely on backlinks when assessing the credibility and significance of content. Pages that appear in AI responses typically have quality links from respected domains.

When creating content or tools, do it in a way that makes others want to cite and link to you. Tools, interactive content, or original data naturally attract attention from media and professionals—and with that come the links that boost your credibility in the eyes of both AI and humans.

Old tricks no longer work. Link exchanges and cheap blog posts will likely harm you. Instead, focus on building quality relationships. Respond to journalists, publish in respected media, and tailor your content to local audiences—for Czech-language content, that means using Czech and Czech domains.
