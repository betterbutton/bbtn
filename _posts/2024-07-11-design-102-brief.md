# How to Write a Design Brief
> 

The British government design agency Design 102 summarizes the [basic requirements of the brief](https://design102.blog.gov.uk/2024/07/10/how-to-get-the-most-out-of-your-agency-briefing/) for designers: **Goal, Audience, Timeline, Budget.**

Additional points that are not mandatory: Insight, Message, Context, Specifics.
