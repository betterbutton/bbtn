# Transition from a large company to a small one: what is the difference?
> 

Ondřej Zajíc left the corporation Fortum for the startup Mia Health and [perceives it as a refreshing change](https://ondrejzajic.substack.com/p/from-corporate-to-startup). While at the corporation, Ondřej had access to large resources and worked on smaller projects for a limited group of users, at the startup he experiences greater freedom and speed in implementing ideas.

> „Although I was afraid that working on one product would bore me, it turned out to be dynamic and adventurous.“\
> — **[Ondřej Zajíc](https://ondrejzajic.substack.com/p/from-corporate-to-startup)**

Team collaboration and the atmosphere of a family environment at Mia provide him with a sense of belonging and engagement, while at Fortum he appreciates the experience with diverse projects and learning opportunities. He views both experiences positively, but the key for him remains team motivation and a sense of meaningful work.
