# OpenAI Introduces AI Course for Teachers
> 

OpenAI launched a course which [aims to show teachers how to use ChatGPT in teaching](https://techcrunch.com/2024/11/20/openai-releases-a-teachers-guide-to-chatgpt-but-some-educators-are-skeptical/). The course, created in collaboration with the organization Common Sense Media, emphasizes pedagogical applications of AI, but faces skeptical responses. Critics point out insufficient coverage of ethical issues and concerns about misuse of data created by teachers.

Some educators appreciate the transparency and potential of AI, others warn about the risk of OpenAI monopolizing the market. UNESCO calls for stricter regulation of AI in education, but implementation is lagging. Studies show mixed results, which further increases uncertainty about the real benefits of AI in the learning process.
