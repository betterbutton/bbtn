# Domain auctions have a new record: 152,077 CZK
> The buyer Lukáš Adamec [was reportedly prepared to bid up to 300,000 CZK](https://wladass.cz/rozhovor-s-majitelem-nejdrazsi-vyrazene-domeny-v-cr-na-aukci-od-nic-cz/), writes Vladimír Pilný, who conducted an interview on his blog with the new owner of the currently most expensive auctioned domain, [virtualnisidlo.cz](https://www.nic.cz/whois/domain/virtualnisidlo.cz/). The domain was auctioned for a record 152,077 CZK on the second attempt. [It was first auctioned for 95,001 CZK](https://blog.nic.cz/2024/06/19/aukce-domen-mesic-po-spusteni/), but the original buyer did not pay the amount, so the domain returned to auction.

The new record was brought to our attention by a [comment](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/24/comments#comment-61869814) under newsletter #24. Thank you!
