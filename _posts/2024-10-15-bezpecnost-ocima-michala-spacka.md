In the [Vzhůru dolů podcast](https://www.vzhurudolu.cz/podcast/251-podcast-michal-spacek), security expert [Michal Špaček](https://www.michalspacek.cz/), Robin Pokorný, and Martin Michálek discuss the importance of multi-factor authentication, proper password management using apps like 1Password, and generating temporary codes.

Michal also explains why he considers reporting security bugs crucial for developer growth and what approach he recommends for it.

He boasts about finding bugs in systems of large companies like Google or Atlassian. Listeners will also learn why it's important to stay in touch with developers via the [security.txt](https://securitytxt.org/) file.
