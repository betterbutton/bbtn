# Minimalism as a Revolution in Design
> 

Minimalist design, often considered a fleeting trend, is becoming a permanent part of the branding scene, according to Kwaku Panti. With an emphasis on cleanliness and functionality, minimalism removes unnecessary elements, which makes a refreshing impression in today's cluttered visual world [https://medium.com/@kwakupanti/minimalistic-designs-905beabe44c5].

Companies that have switched to a minimalist style gain strength and recognizability by focusing on the core of the message, thinks Kwaku Panti. Minimalism, which is both timeless and adaptable for digital environments, improves user experience and has the potential to further grow and change alongside new technologies.
