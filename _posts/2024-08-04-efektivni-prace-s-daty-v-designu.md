# Effective Data Work in Design

> 
Kai Wong writes the blog [Data & Design](https://dataanddesign.substack.com/about). According to Kai, designers often struggle with how to work effectively with data. Kai emphasizes that the key step is not to learn Python or Excel, but to acquire [the ability to think indirectly about data](https://dataanddesign.substack.com/p/the-most-critical-lesson-about-data). Instead of diving into huge tables, it is important to first define the questions and goals you want to discover from the data.

This approach helps avoid overwhelm and facilitates data analysis. Knowledge of working with queries and identifying signals in metrics are key for successful use of data in design.
