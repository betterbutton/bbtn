# Registration of YouTubers and Podcasts with RRTV
> 

[New registration obligation with the Council for Radio and Television Broadcasting](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/vysilaci-rada-chce-registrovat-vsechny) (RRTV) will not apply to all content creators. It targets YouTube channels and vloggers whose reach competes with television broadcasting and whose activities have a business character.

[Criteria include moving image, large audience, and commercial activity](https://www.irozhlas.cz/zpravy-domov/nikdo-nechce-cenzurovat-influencery-tvrdi-analytik-kritizuje-nestastnou_2411270600_cen), such as advertising or sponsorship. RRTV Chairman Václav Mencl confirmed that registration is not new, but problematic communication from the office has caused a wave of misunderstanding. Similar regulations already function in other European countries, and the Czech system will not yet include registration fees.
