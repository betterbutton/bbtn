# The Impact of UX on Search Engine Ratings
> 

Modern SEO is no longer just about keywords and backlinks – user experience also plays an important role. Search engines like Google now evaluate websites based on how effectively they fulfill user intentions. Friendly, aesthetic, and content-rich pages increase user engagement rates and reduce bounce rate, which [positively influences their position in search results](https://medium.com/verse-vision/the-role-of-user-experience-in-seo-why-it-matters-for-search-rankings-2dc83b2bcf10). Companies must therefore incorporate UX into their optimization strategies to remain competitive and offer a valuable experience that users will remember.
