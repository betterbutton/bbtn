# Why Stick to Lo-Fi in Wireframing
> 

Low-fidelity wireframes (lo-fi) are important for effective design. Thanks to their easy and quick creation, [they allow for quick testing of various variants and save time and costs](https://medium.com/@kcsji6370/article-on-why-do-you-need-to-design-wireframes-in-low-fidelity-than-high-fidelity-7e49d6cbf892). Lo-fi wireframes are accessible even for less experienced designers because they require fewer skills than advanced high-fidelity models (hi-fi).

Hi-fi wireframes, on the other hand, excel in precision, interactivity, and presentation to clients. Each approach has its place in the design process, and their choice depends on the needs of the project.
