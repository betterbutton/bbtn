# How to escape the pixel trap and increase your authority

> 

> „Pushing pixels can be important. Pixels matter. Making things beautiful and perfect can be critical.“\
> — **[Scott Berkun](https://whydesignishard.substack.com/p/how-to-escape-pixel-pushing)**

A designer who wants to avoid getting stuck just on pushing pixels, i.e., constant improvement of visual details, must focus their energy on solving project problems, [writes Scott Berkun](https://whydesignishard.substack.com/p/how-to-escape-pixel-pushing). Gaining influence in UX requires the ability to recognize when work on details brings real results and when it's necessary to take on greater responsibility.

The key is to show how better UX contributes to achieving project goals, gaining allies, and demonstrating credibility. It's also important to know when patience is the best strategy and when it's the right time to push your own designs.
