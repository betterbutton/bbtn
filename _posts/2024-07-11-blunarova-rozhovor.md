# Blunárová: With AI, I have more time for strategy
> 
> Elan Miller and Maria Tangarova [interviewed designer Charlota Blunárová](https://offmenu.substack.com/p/how-to-thrive-as-a-designer-in-the). Charlota talks about the transformation of the designer's role thanks to AI. She emphasizes the importance of taste and authenticity in the branding process. AI increases the amount of content, but the real challenge is maintaining brand consistency and communicating shared interests with the target audience.
>
> "With AI, I have more time for strategy, which allows for a deeper approach to the brand story and message."\
> **— Charlota Blunárová, [How to thrive as a designer in the age of AI](https://offmenu.substack.com/p/how-to-thrive-as-a-designer-in-the**
>
> AI technologies should be seen not only as a tool for replacing work processes but also as a means to expand designers' possibilities. Charlota also highlights the importance of a strategic approach and continuous skill development. Collaboration with AI facilitates routine tasks, thereby allowing for deeper focus on strategy and brand communication.
