# New book on service design
> 

Petra Jílková wrote [a book on designing digital services](https://www.grada.cz/service-design-management-14412/), with an emphasis on innovation and sustainability. It offers strategies on how to increase competitiveness through modern approaches and methods, such as design thinking and iterative development.

> "In the 21st century reality, when services are supported by digital products and online products behave like services, service design is the design of the future. Therefore, study this book, which will change your perspective on things – actually on service."\
> **— BoB Marvan**

Readers will become familiar with the design process, history of innovations, and case studies that illustrate key principles. The book also provides practical tools for team collaboration and suggests workshops that connect theoretical concepts with practice. It is intended for managers, marketing specialists, and academics.

The book [Service Design Management](https://www.grada.cz/service-design-management-14412/) will be released in September, but you can pre-order now.
