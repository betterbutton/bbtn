# Accessibility Symbol
> 

![Accessibility Symbol](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/f9ecedaf-d9b5-4732-9874-931bcdcdca47_488x512.png "276x289")

Symbol of universal design and accessibility

As the effective date of the European Accessibility Act approaches, the question arises of how to label accessibility settings for websites and applications or how to mark a more accessible version. [The search for the ideal accessibility symbol](https://www.designedtoinspire.com/blog/142365-searching-for-an-accessibility-symbol/) proved to be a complex process. The icon with the silhouette of a person with outstretched limbs, inspired by the [Vitruvian Man](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vitruvi%C3%A1nsk%C3%BD_mu%C5%BE), first appeared, apparently thanks to Apple, as a symbol of universal access.

The icon is used to denote accessibility because it symbolizes a person and their ability to interact with their surroundings, including various devices and environments. This way, universal design and availability for all users are emphasized.

The traditional blue wheelchair symbol does not meet all needs. Meanwhile, the symbol has found its way into operating system settings and is also appearing on websites. You can find it [among the Material Design icons](https://fonts.google.com/icons?selected=Material+Symbols+Outlined:accessibility_new:FILL@0;wght@400;GRAD@0;opsz@24&icon.query=accessibility&icon.size=24&icon.color=%23e8eaed), in the popular [Font Awesome](https://fontawesome.com/v5/icons/universal-access?s=solid), in [Flaticons](https://www.flaticon.com/free-icon/accessibility_7118029?term=accessibility&page=1&position=5&origin=search&related_id=7118029), and also in the collection [The Noun Project](https://thenounproject.com/icon/accessibility-4501929/).

The universal design symbol has not yet found its way into the Unicode standard, so we still have to work with it as an image. There are also situations where it is unsuitable. One of them is, for example, labeling [the version of the website in Czech Sign Language](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/i/151377948/zivot-s-technologiemi).
