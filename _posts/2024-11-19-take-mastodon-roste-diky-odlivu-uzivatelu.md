# Mastodon also grows thanks to the exodus of users from X
> 

The decentralized social network Mastodon has also seen an increase in users as a result of the exodus from the X platform, [writes TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/11/18/mastodon-sees-a-boost-from-the-x-exodus-too-founder-says/). According to founder Eugen Rochko, the number of app downloads increased by 47% on iOS and 17% on Android. Active users on all servers reached 894,000. Although Mastodon is experiencing growth, it is not reaching the peak from 2022, when it had over a million active users.
