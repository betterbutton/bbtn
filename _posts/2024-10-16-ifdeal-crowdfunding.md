# If…deal – new Czech crowdfunding
> 

![Screenshot of the if…deal website.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/0c32eec9-3441-4bc7-973e-a931b153bf0c_1271x723.png "1271x723")

[If…deal](https://ifdeal.com/)

A few days ago, [the if…deal project was launched](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7247526886434598912/), backed by [Jiří Janoušek](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jirij/) and [Marek Bouška](https://www.linkedin.com/in/marekbouska/). It is a crowdfunding platform, i.e., group project funding. It is similar to Kickstarter, IndieGoGo, or HitHit, but presents itself simply like, for example, Buy Me a Coffee or Go Fund Me. Moreover, it is Czech in origin, even though it has global ambitions.

[If…deal](https://ifdeal.com/) has already completed its first successfully funded campaign. For example, Patrick Zandl will now have to [finish his book Technoelity](https://ifdeal.com/ifdeals/25893d24-2316-460f-937d-d845d0602afe). The concept is that you set a condition (for example, how many people will contribute a specific amount), and if it is met, it's your turn to fulfill your promise. The platform takes seven percent of the funds raised, which is generous compared to similar projects.

Unlike similar websites, if…deal does not take responsibility for promotion. Your deal will remain accessible only via a rather complicated URL and is not listed in any public overview on their site. So, you have to bring your own contributors yourself, like with HeroHero.
