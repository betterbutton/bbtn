# Engaging Introverts in Workshops
> 

Introverts [have a lot to offer in Design Thinking workshops](https://medium.com/@elsacastro_38127/quiet-innovation-unleashing-the-power-of-introverts-in-design-thinking-fd32c348b4bd) thanks to their ability for deep listening and thoughtful analysis, but traditional formats can cause them difficulties. Elsa Castro offers advice on how to adapt workshops so that even the quietest can express their potential – for example, through written communication or quiet zones. Adapting the environment allows the diverse perspectives of all participants to be utilized, leading to richer and more creative outcomes.
