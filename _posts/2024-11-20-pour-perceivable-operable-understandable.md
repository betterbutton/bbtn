# POUR: Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, Robust. WCAG Principles Simply
> 

[Web Content Accessibility Guidelines](https://w3c.github.io/wcag/guidelines/22/) define standards for accessible digital content. Their core consists of four principles: [POUR: perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust](https://medium.com/@sgynaaa/design-sprint-vs-design-thinking-choosing-the-right-approach-for-innovation-8992105bdfde). These principles cover the use of alt text, keyboard navigation, simple language, and compatibility with various technologies. By following these rules, websites can be made accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. For more detailed information, the W3C documentation is available.
