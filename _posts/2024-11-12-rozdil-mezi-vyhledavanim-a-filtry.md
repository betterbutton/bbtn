# The difference between search and filters
> 

[Search and filters are tools with different functions](https://medium.com/@praneethraj55/search-and-filters-are-both-same-0a3a32a30a5b): search is used to quickly find specific information based on keywords, while filters help users explore options in a broader context based on criteria like genre or price.

Together they provide the user with control and clarity, allowing a smooth transition between precise search and structured exploration. It is important to visually and functionally separate these tools so that they do not overlap and facilitate user orientation.
