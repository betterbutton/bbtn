# Heureka blocks links to e-shops for robots
> 

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/09a7007b-8718-4751-9495-fdd99bf2903b_1895x1079.png "1456x829")

Example of robots.txt file content on Czech Heureka

[Heureka.cz/sk blocks](https://heureka.cz/robots.txt) links to e-shops that advertise on its pages via the [robots.txt](https://www.jakpsatweb.cz/robots-txt.html) file. This means that linking from Heureka to e-shops is almost useless for SEO. SEO expert [Pavel Ungr notes](https://www.pavelungr.cz/bacha-na-odkazy-na-heureka-cz-sk/) that link builders should consider whether to continue linking back to Heureka.

Linking to Heureka won't harm you, but you don't get a fair return value. So the question is whether it's not appropriate in such a case to redistribute the weight of your outgoing links in favor of other partners.
