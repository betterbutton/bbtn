# Figma is Alienating Designers
> 

Figma, originally a tool focused purely on designers, is losing its original direction, [expanding to LinkedIn designers](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7253128889965678592/), says Ilia Werner. Expanding features for developers and other roles is overcomplicating and obscuring Figma.

Ilia suggests splitting Figma into specialized tools: keep the original Figma for designers, add Figma Wireframer for concepts, Figma Dev for developers, and Figma Libraries for design system management. This modular approach should return Figma to its original simplicity and focus on design.
