# GPSR: Overview of new obligations for manufacturers, importers, and distributors
> 

The European Union is introducing from December 13, 2024, the new [GPSR regulation](https://commission.europa.eu/business-economy-euro/doing-business-eu/eu-product-safety-and-labelling/product-safety/general-product-safety-regulation_en), which brings stricter rules for product safety. The regulation applies to manufacturers, importers, distributors, and e-shops. [The new obligations are summarized by lawyer Petra Dolejšová.](https://www.petradolejsova.cz/blog/gpsr-evropa-posila-darek-pod-stromecek-a-ja-prakticky-tahak-pro-vyrobce-i-e-shopy)

Manufacturers must ensure the safety of their products, label them with identification data, and archive documentation for 10 years. Importers and distributors are responsible for checking compliance with these requirements and for safe storage and transportation. E-shops must add detailed product information to their pages. Sanctions are not yet defined, but compliance with obligations is important for continuing business activities.
