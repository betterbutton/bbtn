# Designing for Gen Z: Approach, Authenticity, UX
> 

[Vitaly Friedman ponders the right grasp of design](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/designing-gen-z-expectations-ux-guidelines-vitaly-friedman-v6fge/) for the youngest adult users of the Internet. Generation Z is technically proficient, inclusive, and demanding when it comes to user experiences. In design, they appreciate authenticity, clarity, and easy accessibility – from simple elements like high contrast and well-structured forms to mobile optimization.

> Typical advice for 'quick content' is not entirely appropriate: Gen Z desires depth and meaning.

Building trust is key to being authentic, and involving Gen Z in the design process can significantly contribute to the quality of the result. Emphasis on accessibility, sustainability, and respect is fundamental to quality UX for this generation.
