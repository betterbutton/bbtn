# Penpot Attracts Plugin Creators with Financial Rewards
> 

Penpot is an open-source clone of Figma. It lags behind its predecessor also due to a smaller ecosystem of community content and extensions. It doesn't want to leave it that way, so it [is holding a contest for the best plugins](https://penpot.app/plugins-contest). The winner will get a thousand dollars, and twenty other participants will get two hundred dollars each. The most original plugin can additionally get a special prize Outstanding Prize.

The deadline for applications is December 15, 2024, and the winners will be announced on January 17, 2025. You can apply by creating a plugin that will be innovative, functional, and well-coded.
