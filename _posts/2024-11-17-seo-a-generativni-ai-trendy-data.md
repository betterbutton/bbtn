# SEO and Generative AI: Trends, Data, and Strategies Through the Eyes of Pavel Ungr
> 

[AI insights](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/google-ai-overviews-v-dalsich-zemich) are displayed for only 12.4% of keywords – often in health and safety topics. Most commonly, they are associated with five-word queries and appear alongside [Featured Snippets](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/featured-snippets) in only 7.4% of cases. Google ads appear for 28.3% of these results, while shopping ads are more of an exception.

[Pavel emphasized the need for new marketing strategies](https://x.com/PavelUngr/status/1857018811951538468), optimizing websites for AI, and monitoring future trends in SEO. Generative AI brings new challenges and opportunities to the world of SEO, as [indicated by Pavel Ungr at the WebTop100 conference](https://speakerdeck.com/pavelungr/umre-seo-kvuli-generativni-ai).
