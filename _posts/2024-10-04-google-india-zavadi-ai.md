# Google dives into India with big AI
> 

Google Maps is introducing new AI features for India. One of them is automatic review summaries. And you know what, we don't blame them. There is a huge number of people in India, which is also reflected in the flood of reviews, where it is then difficult to navigate.

In addition, the maps will offer the ability to search for specific experiences and weather alerts. Users will be able to search for experiences regardless of pre-created categories. For example: unique picnic spots or thematic birthday cakes.

Furthermore, the app will offer warnings for fog or flooded roads. The new features will be available to users in India later in October. Some of these features have already been introduced in the USA.

At its tenth [Google for India](https://indianexpress.com/article/technology/tech-news-technology/google-for-india-2024-live-updates-10th-edition-9601112/) conference, Google also announced the launch of Gemini Live, an assistant with fluent voice interaction in Hindi, and eight other Indian languages. The company is expanding AI Overviews and visual search, which will allow [asking complex questions about videos](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/02/google-is-testing-gemini-powered-video-search-in-india/).

Google is also adding AI features for Indian businesses, such as automated animations and formatting for online menus. The company estimates that AI could bring up to four trillion dollars in economic value to India by 2030, and suggests investments in data and skills programs, [writes TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/03/google-flexes-its-edge-in-india-in-ai-showdown/).
