# Five Ways to Conduct UX Research Without a Budget
> 

For companies with limited resources, user research is often seen as out of reach. Lee Barguss offers [five practical methods to gain valuable user insights for free](https://medium.com/@leebarguss/maximising-user-research-on-a-zero-budget-ux-tactics-for-companies-d55a6250e310) or at minimal cost. Leverage data from customer support, social media reviews, internal team knowledge, analytics tools, and competitor analysis. Each of these approaches will help you understand user behavior, identify their pain points, and improve your product's UX. The key is creativity and a willingness to listen to customers where they are already sharing their opinions.
