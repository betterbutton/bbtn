# Notifications, iMessage, and calls from iPhone can now be received even on Windows 11
> 
[David Grudl announces on his Twitter](https://x.com/DavidGrudl/status/1795494076122632336), that now even Windows 11 users can read and write messages from iPhone on the computer and receive phone calls and monitor applications.

On a computer with Windows 11, you connect to the phone via Bluetooth using the app [Phone Link](https://support.microsoft.com/cs-cz/topic/propojen%C3%AD-s-telefonem-po%C5%BEadavky-a-nastaven%C3%AD-cd2a1ee7-75a7-66a6-9d4e-bf22e735f9e3#ID0EBD=iOS). On the phone, pairing is helped by the app [Link to Windows](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/link-to-windows/id6443686328), but it is not necessary.
