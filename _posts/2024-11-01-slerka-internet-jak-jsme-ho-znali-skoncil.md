4/8 The internet as we know it has ended. Why do we actually want to read texts from people?

## 

·Mosaic by Pavel Kasík

> *The internet as we knew it has ended.*\
> **— [Josef Šlerka](https://www.seznamzpravy.cz/clanek/audio-podcast-internet-jak-jej-zname-skoncil-proc-vlastne-chceme-cist-texty-od-lidi-263519)**

With the advent of generative AI, fundamental changes are taking place – most texts on the internet will no longer come from people, but from machines. Large language models, like ChatGPT, reduce the cost of text creation and thus challenge the traditional economy of content creators.

The future of the internet is heading towards generated content, which raises questions about the sustainability of creative motivations and credibility. How will people's approach to content change? Pavel Kasík discussed this topic with Josef [in the podcast Mosaic](https://www.seznamzpravy.cz/clanek/audio-podcast-internet-jak-jej-zname-skoncil-proc-vlastne-chceme-cist-texty-od-lidi-263519).
