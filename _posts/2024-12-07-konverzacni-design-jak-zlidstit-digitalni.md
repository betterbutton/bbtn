# Conversational Design: How to Humanize Digital Systems
> 

Conversational design aims to make digital systems feel more natural and human. It's not just about voice commands or text chats – true conversation requires a deeper understanding of human interaction, emphasis on personality, and careful integration of content, structure, and tone.

The author of the bestseller Just Enough Research, Erika Hall, also wrote the book Conversational Design. It is soon to be released in a second edition, and therefore Erika decided to offer the original 2018 book as a [free e-book](https://www.mulebooks.com/conversational-design).

The second edition of the book will be released in 2025 and brings updated advice on how to create technology based on the fundamental principles of human communication, despite the trends of large language models. Erika Hall teaches us how to improve user experience by combining design and communication.
