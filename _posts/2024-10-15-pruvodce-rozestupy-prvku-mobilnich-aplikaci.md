# Perfect Element Spacing in Mobile Apps
> 

Proper element placement in mobile apps is crucial for user-friendliness, readability, and aesthetics. [The guide introduces a 4-point grid system](https://medium.com/@dscode/comprehensive-guide-to-perfectly-spacing-elements-in-mobile-apps-a02fc7b1df18), which helps ensure consistent spacing and design optimization across different devices.

> „Element spacing in mobile app design might seem like a minor detail, but it can make or break the user experience.“

Emphasis is placed on the importance of whitespace and even spacing, which improve visual hierarchy and make the app easier to use. The guide also highlights common mistakes, such as grouping elements too closely, and recommends tools like Figma, which makes it easier to adhere to these principles.
