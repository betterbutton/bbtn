# Google Lens handles over 20 billion queries monthly
> 

Google Lens is becoming one of the fastest-growing search tools at Google. It handles more than 20 billion queries monthly. [According to the Think with Google website](https://www.thinkwithgoogle.com/marketing-strategies/search/google-search-innovations/), every fourth query has commercial intent. Features like [Circle to Search](https://support.google.com/websearch/answer/14508957?hl=cs), which Google introduced in the new Android, enable easy searching directly from the screen, a feature particularly popular with Generation Z.
