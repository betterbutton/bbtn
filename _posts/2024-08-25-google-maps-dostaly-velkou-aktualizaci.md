# Google Maps has received a major update
> 

Google Maps comes with several [updates](https://blog.google/products/maps/).

1.  [Live View feature](https://x.com/iam_chonchol/status/1816154336638218369) allows you to explore the surroundings using augmented reality (AR).

2.  [Dialog-based search.](https://x.com/iam_chonchol/status/1816154387095642501)

3.  [3D objects on the map.](https://x.com/iam_chonchol/status/1816154735289974854)

4.  [Bird's-eye view](https://x.com/iam_chonchol/status/1816154824330940696) (known from Mapy.cz).

5.  [AI recommendations.](https://x.com/iam_chonchol/status/1816154881151099083)

6.  [Image-based search.](https://x.com/iam_chonchol/status/1816154931403096205)

7.  [Google Lens in maps.](https://x.com/iam_chonchol/status/1816154981701148834)

The updates are also the subject of jokes. For example, Soren Iverson suggests that navigation could [send your location to a police patrol](https://www.threads.net/@soren.iverson/post/C_A_JKIuqZ6?xmt) if you exceed the speed limit, [redirect other traffic if you're in a hurry](https://www.threads.net/@soren.iverson/post/CxVfbdRL9Z6?xmt), or [send a message to the driver](https://www.threads.net/@soren.iverson/post/C-SvkYVgWpY?xmt) who is crawling in front of you. It's nice when users themselves come up with ideas for product improvements.
