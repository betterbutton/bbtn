# UX Design Patterns for AI
> 

The project [Shape of AI](https://www.shapeof.ai/) maps how artificial intelligence is changing user interfaces. With an emphasis on the enduring principles of good design, it offers a collection of UX patterns, such as navigation trails, templates, output tuning, or trustworthiness.

These patterns help users better understand and control AI, whether it's about process transparency, source citation, or setting a personal voice. Shape of AI emphasizes the importance of ethics, accuracy, and user control in the new paradigm of technology interaction.
