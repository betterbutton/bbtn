# Ministry for Local Development Launches Builder's Portal
> 

The Ministry for Local Development successfully launched the digitized agenda of construction management yesterday. [states on Twitter](https://x.com/mistnirozvoj/status/1807805999354417470) that the [builder's portal](https://portal.stavebnisprava.gov.cz/) was used by 4,000 people on the very first day.
