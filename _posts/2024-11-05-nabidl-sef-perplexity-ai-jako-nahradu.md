# Did Perplexity AI CEO offer to replace striking New York Times employees?
> 

The CEO of Perplexity, Aravind Srinivas, [offered assistance to the New York Times during a strike](https://techcrunch.com/2024/11/04/perplexity-ceo-offers-to-replace-striking-nyt-staff-with-ai/) by its technical workers, earning criticism for *"taking over the work of strikers."*

The strike, driven by demands for better pay and flexible office attendance, is taking place on the eve of the US presidential elections, which, according to NYT publisher A. G. Sulzberger, threatens the delivery of essential information.

Srinivas on social network X [offered technical support for high web traffic](https://x.com/AravSrinivas/status/1853501484006342801), but his call was seen as undermining the union action. The issue was compounded by already tense relations due to legal disputes between the NYT and Perplexity.
