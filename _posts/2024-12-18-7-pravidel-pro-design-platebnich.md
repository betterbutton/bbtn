# 7 Rules for Payment Button Design
> 

The payment button is a key element of e-commerce and SaaS websites. Its design fundamentally influences user experience and transaction success. Among the [best practices](https://uxplanet.org/payment-button-design-best-practices-57a9022645ca) are clear action labeling ("Pay" instead of "Submit"), visual distinction, displaying feedback during payment processing (e.g., animation), disabling the button during the process, and elegant error handling. Ensuring speed is also important – payment should not take longer than 5–10 seconds. Accessibility must be a priority, especially when working with keyboard or mobile controls.
