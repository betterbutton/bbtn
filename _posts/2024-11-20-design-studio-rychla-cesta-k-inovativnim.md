# Design Studio: Fast Path to Innovative Product Ideas
> 

Design Studio is a [fast and fun team collaboration method](https://jpattonassociates.com/design-studio-recipe/) for finding innovative solutions. It is based on the principle of ideation, which goes beyond mere iteration by emphasizing the generation of a quantity of ideas – from the first to the most creative. Everyone gets involved in the process – from designers through developers to users, ensuring diversity of perspectives and ideas.

[Recipe](https://kisk.phil.muni.cz/100metod/design-studio) includes steps from problem description, sharing inspirations, through group sketching to consolidation of the best proposals. The result is a rich foundation for the final design that takes into account various views and needs.
