# Case Study: Better UX by Rewriting Texts in the Mobills App
> 

The Mobills app redesign [improved user experience through text optimization](https://medium.com/@nathanroberto/ux-writing-projeto-de-ux-writing-para-o-app-mobills-c4327249b3c0). Nathan Roberto analyzed competing apps, defined user goals, and tailored texts for clear and motivating communication. The main goal was to create a clear onboarding and an intuitive presentation of features.

Using tools like FigJam and Dalton Lens, the author eliminated technical terms, added accessibility, and focused on user needs. The process involved brainstorming, desk research, testing, and feedback. The result highlights the role of UX Writing in creating a user-friendly environment.
