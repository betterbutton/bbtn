# Steigerwald: For the State, Portals and Applications Have Their Place, a Helpdesk Suffices
> 

Programmer Dan Steigerwald is known for his provocative views on the state. On Thursday, he [commented on the digital transformation of public administration](https://x.com/steidacz/status/1816375111764586943) and does not hide that he thinks the current government is going about it the wrong way by launching apps and portals like on a conveyor belt: *"No, don't do it. Don't write software. Use anything else that is possible."* He suggests that the state should have just one interface for citizens, like a helpdesk: *"The citizen opens a ticket or calls a universal number. Done. Let the state figure it out itself. All companies could do this with their left hand behind their back."*
