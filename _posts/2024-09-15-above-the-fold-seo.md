# Above the Fold SEO
> 

The term [above the fold](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Above_the_fold) is probably familiar – it refers to the content on the first screen of a webpage. Usually, the significance of content above the fold and loading speed is discussed in the context of [conversion optimization](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conversion_rate_optimization) and [bounce rate](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/1009409?hl=cs), but Cari Cruise introduces the term above the fold SEO – [search engine optimization above the fold](https://medium.com/@simplectg37/above-the-fold-seo-d4f1a3a36e20).

Cari says that search engines monitor user engagement metrics, and if people return to search from your pages (the page has a high bounce rate), the search engine will take that into account.

This brings us back to the claim that search engine optimization is nothing more than improving the user experience. Quality headings, appropriate images, and calls to action increase the chances of conversion, reduce bounce rate, and contribute to better positions in search results.

Cari also ponders how the first screen will be mixed up by personalization cards based on machine learning and artificial intelligence, which will add dynamic content to the pages.
