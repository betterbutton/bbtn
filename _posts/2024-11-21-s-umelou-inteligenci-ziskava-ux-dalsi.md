# UX gains another dimension with artificial intelligence
> 

> "UX design must be user-oriented because every design decision should revolve around user needs and habits."

UX design helps technologies become intuitive and user-friendly. The article [Beyond Buttons and Code: Principles of UX design and AI](https://medium.com/@quantamfork/beyond-buttons-and-code-principles-of-ux-design-and-ai-334ea64b867f) describes the basic principles of UX design, such as user-oriented approach, simplicity, consistency, and accessibility. These principles ensure smooth interaction with applications and websites. By adding artificial intelligence, UX gains another dimension — AI personalizes content, anticipates user needs, and facilitates communication through natural language processing. The combination of UX and AI creates experiences that are not only effective but also tailored to each user.
