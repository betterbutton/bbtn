# When and how often to send a newsletter
> 

Email cadence is crucial for success in email marketing because the right frequency and timing affect open rates, click-through rates, and subscriber satisfaction. Too many emails can lead to unsubscribes, while too few can result in low engagement.

Most commonly, companies send one to two newsletters per week and consider Tuesday the best day of the week for sending. Conversely, Sunday is the least suitable day for email campaigns.

Perfect cadence includes proper timing, number of emails, and sequence of sending, tailored to subscriber preferences and behavior. Companies should monitor and analyze their campaign performance, segment their audience, and test different approaches to achieve optimal results and build strong relationships with clients.
