# New Kindle will be fast and with a color display
> 

Amazon has refreshed the line of Kindle e-book readers. In addition to the [basic model](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B0CP32YGPY?ref_=as_li_ss_tl&language=en_US&btn_ref=srctok-f8ff73038d76da2e&ascsubtag=srctok-f8ff73038d76da2e&linkCode=gs4&linkId=f96bdb253e69b60696907ac9ee667c40&tag=underscoreduk-21), the [Kindle Paperwhite](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B0CFPXBJ9Y?ref_=as_li_ss_tl&language=en_US&btn_ref=srctok-8524b39646c8d0e8&ascsubtag=srctok-8524b39646c8d0e8&linkCode=gs4&linkId=791331b3404b509a629da069f95435b2&tag=underscoreduk-21) and [Kindle Scribe](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B0CZB5RHWX?ref_=as_li_ss_tl&language=en_US&btn_ref=srctok-e3d364405349d7a3&ascsubtag=srctok-e3d364405349d7a3&linkCode=gs4&linkId=c53f1056b38ed7885076e8cf73017a25&tag=underscoreduk-21) will also get an upgrade. Amazon is newly introducing the [Kindle Colorsoft](https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/devices/kindle-color-specs-price-announce),

[Kindle Colorsoft](https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/devices/kindle-color-specs-price-announce) is the first Kindle with a color display. The color e-reader will cost $280 and pre-sales started yesterday. While the [Kindle Paperwhite](https://www.amazon.com/Kindle-Paperwhite-16-GB-adjustable/dp/B09TMZKQR7) ($160) offers performance updates and new colors, the [Kindle Scribe](https://www.amazon.com/Kindle-Scribe-the-first-Kindle-for-reading-writing-journaling-and-sketching/dp/B09BS26B8B) ($400) retains the writing function with AI features that adjust handwritten notes. The basic [Kindle](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B0CP32YGPY?ref_=as_li_ss_tl&language=en_US&btn_ref=srctok-990f211cfca1168c&ascsubtag=srctok-990f211cfca1168c&linkCode=gs4&linkId=f96bdb253e69b60696907ac9ee667c40&tag=underscoreduk-21) in version 12 still provides a great experience for $110.
