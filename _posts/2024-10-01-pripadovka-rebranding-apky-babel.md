# Case Study: Rebranding of the Babbel App
> 

![Preview of the new look of Babbel's desktop and mobile web](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/fcb4d655-9974-4d58-9ff9-73d1bada4b4b_1250x721.png "1250x721")

Babbel after redesign

[Babbel](https://www.babbel.com/) is a language learning app. It has undergone an eighteen-month long [journey of complete rebranding](https://medium.com/babbeldesign/the-babbel-rebrand-translating-vision-into-design-2b2db76dc595). The team of designers together with the external agency Koto developed a new visual style. The result should be a personal approach similar to a language coach and a flexible, fun form of learning.

Thanks to the collaboration of visual designers with content creators, they managed to create an image library as well. The project ended with the implementation of the rebranding, but Babbel continues to develop and adapt it based on feedback and measurement.
