# Figma disabled the AI feature Make Design
> 
> "I asked our team to temporarily turn off the Make Design feature until we are sure we can stand behind its output."
> **— [Dylan Field](https://x.com/zoink/status/1808045664334668008), CEO of Figma on Twitter**

Figma responded to reports that the Make Design feature, built on artificial intelligence, in designing a weather forecast app [repeatedly copied the design](https://x.com/asallen/status/1807675146020454808) of Apple's Weather app for iOS. Figma's CEO [Dylan Field announced](https://x.com/zoink/status/1808045655082033483) that the AI is not trained on designs shared through the community or on existing apps. Nevertheless, Field in the same Twitter thread announces that [the Make Design feature will be temporarily deactivated](https://x.com/zoink/status/1808045655082033483) until developers fine-tune it, and apologizes for insufficient testing of the feature before launch.
