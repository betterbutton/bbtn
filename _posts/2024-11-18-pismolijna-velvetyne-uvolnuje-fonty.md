# Velvetyne Type Foundry Releases Fonts as Open Source
> 

The French type foundry Velvetyne is making its [font production](https://velvetyne.fr/) available under the SIL Open Font License, which allows its typefaces to be used freely and at no cost. Its fonts are very distinctive in shape and suitable for special visual effects. In our parts, we appreciate that the type definitions include characters with háčky, čárky, and kroužek.
