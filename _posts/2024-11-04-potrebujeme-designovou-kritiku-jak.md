# We need design critique. How to do it right?
> 

[Critique and review are two different practices in design](https://www.petermerholz.com/blog/critique-is-not-review-and-many-other-thoughts-on-an-overlooked-practice/), says Peter Merholz: while review serves to assess the readiness of work for the next phase, critique aims to improve the work. Effective critique requires psychological safety and structural support.

Critique must not serve as a tool of authority, but as a space for open feedback without fear of retaliation. The key is to establish regular "critical cells" and block time for shared feedback. Critique should be part of the workflow, not an additional "obligation".
