# Create personas and transform your projects
> 

User personas represent a tool in UX design that [helps understand users and create intuitive products](https://medium.com/@sanskarY/the-ultimate-guide-to-user-personas-in-ux-design-7890fdafbc94). They are fictional profiles based on real data that reflect the target group. Using user personas increases empathy, facilitates decision-making, and improves communication in the team.

To create personas, it is necessary to conduct user research, identify patterns, and create detailed profiles. Well-crafted personas lead to higher user satisfaction because products meet their needs. Don't forget to visualize personas and update them continuously.
