# User Murder Index: When UX in Medicine Decides on Life and Death
> 

Farhad Shokraneh sharply criticizes in the article the practices of scientific publication publishers that complicate access to vital information. He argues that every unnecessary step—a click, verification, or advertisement—can cost a patient their life.

The concept [User Murder Index](https://farhadinfo.medium.com/user-murder-index-umi-the-time-and-effort-it-takes-to-block-information-that-can-save-someones-a848ee020a) illustrates how complex information accessibility endangers both patients and doctors. Publishers, instead of improving access, often hinder users even after payment, thereby increasing the risk of death. Shokraneh calls for radical changes and simpler user experiences in the interest of saving lives.
