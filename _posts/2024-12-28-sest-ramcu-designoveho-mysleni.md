# Six Frameworks of Design Thinking
> 

Design thinking offers structured approaches to problem-solving and innovation. Here are [six key frameworks](https://medium.com/@designwithmeghna/exploring-design-thinking-frameworks-e9cd97b3b1ff):

1.  **[3I model by IDEO](https://designthinking.ideo.com/)** — Focused on inspiration, ideation, and implementation.

2.  **[Google Design Sprint](https://www.gv.com/sprint/)** — Five days of intensive collaboration in six steps.

3.  **[Double Diamond by British Design Council](http://Double%20Diamond%20od%20British%20Design%20Council)** — Combination of divergent and convergent thinking.

4.  **[Head, Heart, and Hand by AIGA](https://sanantonio.aiga.org/head-heart-and-hand-modern-design-practice/)** — Connection of logic, empathy, and practice.

5.  **[Collective Action Toolkit by Frog Design](https://www.frog.co/designmind/the-collective-action-toolkit-quick-start-guide)** — Framework for community collaboration.

6.  **[LUMA System of Innovation](https://www.luma-institute.com/about-luma/luma-system/)** — Methods of observation, analysis, and experimentation.\
    Each framework has its strengths and can be adapted to the specific needs of teams.
