# Features that streamlined work in Figma
> 

Designer Alex Borsuk describes [several solved issues](https://x.com/a1exborsuk/thread/1820906244330205640) when working in Figma thanks to the new interface:

1.  Independent property settings for each stroke layer.

2.  Grouping similar colors on the palette.

3.  Scroll-based interactivity.

4.  Pinning a layer to a chosen position.

5.  Versioning at the frame level.

6.  Choosing an object's rotation point.

7.  LAB and LCH color spaces.

8.  Assigning frames to team members.

9.  Archiving designs in their original state.

10. Collapsing items on the layers panel.
