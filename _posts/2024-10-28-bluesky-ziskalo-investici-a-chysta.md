# Bluesky secures investment and plans subscription rollout
> 

> “Twitter showed us how *not* to do subscriptions. The ‘pay to win’ feature ruins the network for everyone.” — **[Paul Frazee](https://bsky.app/profile/pfrazee.com/post/3l7biz5kpe22j)**

[The decentralized social network Bluesky](https://bsky.app) has secured a $15 million Series A investment and [plans to introduce a subscription](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/24/bluesky-raises-15m-series-a-plans-to-launch-subscriptions/) offering users features like higher-quality video uploads or profile customization. The service, originally incubated within Twitter and built on the open AT Protocol, is currently growing due to user dissatisfaction with platform X.

The company assures, however, that it is not pivoting to cryptocurrencies or NFTs and plans to focus on user experience and transparent development. The new investment round was led by Blockchain Capital, with Kinjal Shah from the firm joining Bluesky's board.
