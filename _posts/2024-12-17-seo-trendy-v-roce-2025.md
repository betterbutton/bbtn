# SEO trends in 2025
> 

[SEO marketing in 2025](https://medium.com/@n.ayotte85/emerging-trends-in-seo-marketing-for-2025-63bfe8b31f1b) will, according to N. Ayotte, shape several key trends. Artificial intelligence helps optimize content and personalize the user experience, while the growth of voice search emphasizes the importance of long, conversational keywords.

Focus on user experience ([Core Web Vitals](https://web.dev/articles/vitals)) and deep understanding of user search intent remain crucial. Video SEO, [local optimization](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/advent-2405) and visual search offer new opportunities, while Google places greater emphasis on expertise, authority, and trustworthiness of content ([E-E-A-T](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/ai-serp)). Companies that integrate ethical and sustainable practices will gain an advantage not only in SEO but also in the eyes of their customers.
