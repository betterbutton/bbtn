# Christmas templates for web
> 

In a month we have Christmas Eve, and soon websites will start to snow and display holly and comets in their headers. If you're now scared that you don't have anything prepared, don't worry. Eric Karkovack has prepared for us [an overview of over fifty resources for free Christmas graphics](https://speckyboy.com/free-christmas-templates-resources/) and templates.

It includes seasonal illustrations, icon sets, retro designs, hand-crafted templates, stylish fonts, as well as tools for Photoshop and Lightroom. These resources will help add a stylish and fresh atmosphere to your projects. It doesn't matter whether you need banners, labels, textures, or promotions—the collection covers everything from minimalist to richly decorative styles.
