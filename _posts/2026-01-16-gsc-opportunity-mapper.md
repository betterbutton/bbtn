# GSC Opportunity Mapper estimates keyword potential
> A tool that transforms Google Search Console exports into an SEO plan focused on prioritization, structure, and realistic opportunities.

![screenshot from the GSC Opportunity Mapper tool, visualizing the opportunity to gain search engine traffic for specific queries.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/2e02a130-aa49-4c64-8e0e-0c63c32ea16f_1918x1078.png "1456x818")

[GSC Opportunity Mapper](https://gsc-opportunity-mapper-ht9p92euufksxqjhthkhpa.streamlit.app/) reveals opportunities.

Most websites don't suffer from a lack of visitor potential. They suffer from a lack of priorities. This is exactly the problem that *[GSC Opportunity Mapper](https://gsc-opportunity-mapper-ht9p92euufksxqjhthkhpa.streamlit.app/)*, created by *[Rüdiger Dalchow](https://grumpy-old-seo.com/)*, solves. The tool takes two common exports from Google Search Console and transforms them into a clear, actionable SEO plan.

The principle is simple. You upload a CSV file with queries and a CSV file with pages from the *Performance → Search results* report. The export must be done in the English localization of the Google Search Console interface so that GSC Opportunity Mapper correctly matches the table columns. The application then reorganizes the data into views that make sense even outside SEO tools: pages with the highest growth potential, thematic clusters instead of individual keywords, segmentation by search intent, and topic-to-URL mapping. Special attention is also paid to alerts about situations where multiple pages compete for the same topic.

The tool doesn't ask what new content to write, but where visibility already exists today that can be quickly improved. Typically, this involves pages with high impressions and low CTR, missing internal links, or content that deserves consolidation instead of further expansion. This also aligns well with Google's recommendations: optimize what already works.

The real value of GSC Opportunity Mapper lies in uncovering structural problems and forcing you to set priorities. It often shows that strengthening existing pages has a greater impact than creating new ones.

The tool is free. It runs in the browser and is [also available as open source](https://github.com/ruedalchow/gsc-opportunity-mapper). If you want to move from SEO reports to real actions without another paid platform, it's worth trying.
