# What will happen to artists when AI is so good?
> 

The development of generative AI brings a new situation for artists. Technologies can serve as tools to expand their creativity, similar to how synthesizers or cameras were used before, [writes TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/11/05/as-generative-ai-gets-better-what-will-happen-to-artists/). CEO of [Suno](https://suno.com/), Mikey Shulman, showed music students that AI can function more as a helper than a competitor.

Nevertheless, concerns persist – some artists fear that technology trained on their work will take away their livelihood. While Hollywood fights to protect jobs from AI, companies like Splice hope that new technology will enable a broader public to create and share art.
