# Automation makes marketing easier even for small entrepreneurs
> 

> Marketing automation is not just for large companies; small businesses can also use it for more efficient operations and better customer experience – it [shows how to do it](https://www.samsobemarketerem.cz/p/co-lze-v-marketingu-snadno-automatizovat). Email campaigns, social media posts, automatic form responses, or review collection – all of this can be easily automated. It's important to start with activities that take up the most time.

> "We should try to automate or delegate (even to AI) all routine tasks that are repeated. Why? Because then we have time for other important things."\
> **— Filip Dřímalka, [Future of Non-Work](https://drimalka.com/neprace)**

For example, Ecomail helps with targeted customer segmentation and email planning, while Meta Business Suite simplifies social media management. Tools like IFTTT or Airtable allow you to track key metrics and processes. Properly set up automation saves time and increases efficiency.
