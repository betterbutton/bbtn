# Kvasnička: Most Common Mistakes of Czechoslovak E-Shops
> 

[Honza Kvasnička](http://www.kvasnickajan.cz/) spoke at [Shopcamp](https://shopcamp.cz/) with an overview of [the most common mistakes of Czech and Slovak e-shops](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/nejvetsi-chyby-na-cz-sk-e-shopech-checklist-pro-vas-shopcamp-2024/271778663#3). As always, Honza's presentation is packed with screenshots and examples from well-known e-shops, which we would expect to be fine-tuned and optimized. Honza translated his findings into the form of a [checklist](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfRzoNTHGDtYo4py1DSSeCFIzcmfHbdM-4pzwLAtqPTLC2LLg/viewform?fbzx=1044377673257377827), which he offers for download in exchange for contact information.
