# Automated Systems Skew Email Campaign Statistics
> 

During the key period for email marketing – Black Friday and Christmas – Jakub Olexa from Mailkit warns of a rise in fake clicks. Since the beginning of 2024, their number has doubled and is still growing, [MediaGuru writes](https://www.mediaguru.cz/clanky/2024/10/vanocni-e-maling-ohrozuje-narust-falesnych-kliknuti/).

Fake clicks are caused by automated security checks from email service and tool operators. Their activity distorts campaign results. Evaluating recipient interest in emails without accounting for automated traffic can lead to flawed marketing decisions.
