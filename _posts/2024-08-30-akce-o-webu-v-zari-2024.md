# Web Event in September 2024
> 

After the holiday lull, September is once again packed with events for designers, frontend developers, and people around the web. Right on Monday, September 2nd, we have [UX Monday on imposter syndrome](https://www.meetup.com/asociace-ux/events/303040650/). In the second half of the week, there will be a [live interview with Martin Michálek](https://www.meetup.com/front-cookies-open/events/302013982/) on Front & Cookie. [SEOloger](https://form.simpleshop.cz/jDqyn/) will focus on user research. The legendary [WARP Camp](https://www.warpcamp.cz/) is happening again!

For weekend events like [MeasureCamp](https://czechia.measurecamp.org/) or [SEO bootcamp](https://seobootcamp.cz/), you probably won't make it, but for [ShopCamp](https://www.shopcamp.cz/), [UX Healthcare](https://uxhealthcare.co/), or [Design Systems in Practice](https://friends.figma.com/events/details/figma-czech-republic-presents-design-systemy-v-praxi-vienna-insurance-group/), you can still get tickets.

In addition, several online events are taking place, and we also bring you some tips for events in Krakow, Vienna, Berlin, Freiburg, and London.

You can find the [complete overview of events](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/konference-2024-09) as usual on our website. If yours is missing, [let us know](https://tally.so/r/mV5zOE)!
