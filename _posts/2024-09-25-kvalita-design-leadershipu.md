# How to Recognize Quality Leadership in Design?
> 

Design leaders often measure their success by their team's results. This leads to the assumption that quality results mean quality leadership. [But Jesse James Garrett questions if that's enough.](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7241506974926872576/) He calls for reflection on how leaders could consciously develop leadership quality just as they develop design quality. He suggests they focus not only on outcomes but also on their own growth, and explores how they might measure their leadership quality level—whether through metrics or more subtle qualitative indicators.
