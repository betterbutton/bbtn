# Seven Fundamental Design Principles
> 

Design principles are the fundamental rules that UX designers follow when creating products to ensure they are easy and efficient to use. The [Seven Fundamental UX](https://medium.com/@yogaproduct/design-principles-in-ux-design-550d2c001673) design principles, as written about by Yoga Pratama, are:

- simplicity,

- consistency,

- hierarchy,

- contrast,

- readability,

- functionality, and

- perceived affordance.

Each of these principles plays a key role in creating a user-friendly and intuitive interface, where information is clearly structured and easily accessible. Proper application of these principles leads to a better user experience and helps designers achieve their ideal.
