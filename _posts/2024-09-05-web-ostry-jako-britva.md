# Sharp Decade of Jan Řezáč
> 

Ten years ago, the book [Web Sharp as a Razor](https://www.houseofrezac.com/kniha) was published, in which Jan Řezáč summarized his approach to web design. The book is largely based on research of sources with references, so it works well as a springboard for further self-education. Not much has been published in Czech about the web, so an entire generation of web creators and their clients grew up on this book.

[Jan on Linkedin Promises a Sharp September](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7236279097809678337/) – a month-long revival of the book's ideas and their update with a decade's perspective.

[We're looking forward to it, Philosopher!](https://web.archive.org/web/20140313044603/http://www.filosof.biz/)
