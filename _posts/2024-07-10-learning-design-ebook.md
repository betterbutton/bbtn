# New ebook on learning design in Czech
> 

Klára Pirklová from the National Pedagogical Institute wrote a [book on learning design](https://www.klarapirklova.cz/learning-design-pro-ucitele-a-lektory-e-book/) and published it as a free ebook. The book will guide you through a familiar design process, but this time you are not creating a website or an application, but an educational project.
