# Forget SEO – optimize for ChatGPT and other AI models
> 

With the growing popularity of large language models (LLMs) like ChatGPT or Gemini, optimizing content for these tools is crucial for increasing brand visibility. LLMs work differently from traditional search engines – they often focus on mentions rather than links. As Crystal Carter, Head of SEO Communications at WiXu, explains in the article [SEO for brand visibility in LLMs](https://www.wix.com/seo/learn/resource/llm-brand-visibility).

To maximize effectiveness, it's important to track the differences between static models and those that supplement information from search engines. The key to success is adapting content, monitoring performance, managing entities, and engaging on platforms like Perplexity or creating custom GPT tools. SEO for LLMs combines proven methods with new technological possibilities.
