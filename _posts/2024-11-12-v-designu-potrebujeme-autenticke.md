# We need authentic leaders in design

> 

[Experienced design leaders often face pressure to conform rather than lead authentically.](https://medium.com/@dsmithdesignleadership/strong-opinions-strongly-held-why-seasoned-leaders-should-be-unleashed-not-restrained-81bdca59b977) However, this approach suppresses their potential to drive change and innovation that could fundamentally advance the organization.

> "Strong leaders recognize which compromises are worth making and which would compromise the quality and integrity of the product."\
> **— Darren Smith**

Design culture driven by experience, as promoted by companies like Airbnb or Canva, benefits from principles of strong craftsmanship and bold opinions, enabling the creation of products that resonate with users. When designers are forced to adapt rather than lead, it reduces their role to mere executors. Organizations should create an environment where their voices are heard and their experience is recognized as key to innovation.
