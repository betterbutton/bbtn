# Temu's business newly falls into the category of very large online platforms according to DSA
> 

Temu serves 45 million customers in the EU monthly. This is above the threshold set by the European Commission for very large online platforms (VLOP) under the Digital Services Act. At the latest by the end of September, [Temu must comply with strict rules for large service providers](https://ec.europa.eu/commission/presscorner/detail/en/ip_24_3047), such as the obligation to properly assess and mitigate all systemic risks arising from its services, including the listing and sale of counterfeit goods, dangerous or illegal products, and items infringing intellectual property rights.

[What Temu will now have to comply with in the EU](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7203274965159137280/), is clearly summarized by Karel Navrátil.
