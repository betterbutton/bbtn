# UX Errors That Kill Conversions
> 

A poor user experience can be fatal for an e-shop's conversion rate. Kalpana Bhuvaraghavan cites as a case study an unsuccessful purchase on a photo frames page, where [slow loading and a poorly placed payment button led to cart abandonment](https://medium.com/@KalpanaBhuvaraghavan/ouch-that-hurts-ux-conversion-c346bd52ea37).

Kalpana highlights UX issues such as slow loading, confusing navigation, weak calls-to-action, a complicated purchase process, lack of trustworthiness, and lack of mobile optimization. Shortcomings can be addressed with simple adjustments – from improving speed and clear CTAs to better mobile design. A UX audit is key to higher conversions.
