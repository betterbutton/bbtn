# How to Write Notes That Actually Work
> 

Cassidy Williams refers to herself as an [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) girl. On her blog, she reflects on her note-taking habits. Obsidian helps her create a mythical [second brain](https://www.buildingasecondbrain.com/). She criticizes the tendency to accumulate data without actual use. She suggests an approach of dividing notes into those meant for future use (with tags and links) and those that help organize thoughts during thinking. Cassidy recommends focusing on thinking about what to do next, rather than constantly collecting information, and [adding links to resources](https://cassidoo.co/post/note-takers-are-data-hoarders/) that can improve the writing process.
