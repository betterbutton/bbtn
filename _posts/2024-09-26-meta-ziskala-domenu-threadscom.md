# The threads.com domain now belongs to Meta
> 

![WHOIS records for the threads.com domain show that since Monday 15:39 GMT, the domain has been under the management of Facebook's name servers.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/6c1d2b5b-5d68-481f-8a56-c3141e57bb71_1268x696.png "1268x696")

The threads.com domain changed hands for the second time this year. After it was acquired by Shopify in June under undisclosed terms, a record appeared on Tuesday at 15:39 GMT indicating the transfer of the domain to Meta. According to TechCrunch, [Meta confirmed the transaction without further comment](https://techcrunch.com/2024/09/25/meta-acquires-the-threads-com-domain-name/).

Meta operates the Threads social network on the threads.net domain. The threads.com domain thus receives a lot of unintended traffic due to confusion with the top-level domain. The transfer of the domain to Meta's management will therefore also be appreciated by users.
