# Can empathy transform your daily life?
> 

Empathy is a crucial skill in User Experience, but it can also be challenging. In the UX profession, the key ability is to understand and connect with people, which can reflect in everyday life as well.

This practice involves attention, listening, and the ability to see things from different perspectives—skills that can significantly improve not only our products but also ourselves. UX approaches thus help [design not only digital experiences but also a higher quality personal life](https://medium.com/@papettifrancesco646/user-experience-into-daily-life-cd4a5ef73b0d). The author encourages applying these principles beyond work, as life is our biggest project.
