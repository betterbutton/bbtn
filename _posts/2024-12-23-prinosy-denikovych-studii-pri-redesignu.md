# Benefits of Diary Studies in Redesign
> 

[Diary studies helped Stéphanie Walter redesign](https://stephaniewalter.design/blog/improving-user-experience-with-diary-studies/) an 18-year-old loan management tool at an investment bank. The studies enabled the collection of data on long-term usage, which could not be captured during hour-long user tests.

The process involved preparing a plan, defining goals, setting up diary templates in Excel, and conducting a thorough analysis of the collected data. The result was an improved user interface, resolved errors, and the creation of training materials.

Despite their demanding nature and dependence on the quality of recorded data, diary studies provided key insights that led to significant improvements before the tool's final launch.
