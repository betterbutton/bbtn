# In-depth Interviews: Cheat Sheet for Follow-up Questions
> 

Stéphanie Walter has an amazing [overview of questions for user interviews](https://stephaniewalter.design/blog/a-cheatsheet-for-user-interview-and-follow-ups-questions/) and follow-up questions on her website. She uses them when preparing semi-structured interviews. She has categorized the questions by the type of information she wants to gather – from open-ended questions to specific queries about tasks or problems.

> - *How does the described problem affect you?*
>
> - *If you had a magic wand, what would you change?*
>
> - *How should it ideally work?*
>
>   *…*

Stéphanie emphasizes the importance of tailoring questions to the specific person and situation, especially when working with neurodivergent users or participants from different cultural backgrounds. Finally, she includes tips for further resources to gain a deeper understanding of user research issues.
