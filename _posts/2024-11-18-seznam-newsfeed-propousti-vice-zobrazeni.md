# Seznam news feed is driving more views to smaller websites
> 

Ivo Lukačovič, owner of Seznam, wrote on his Twitter that the recommendation algorithm on Seznam's homepage, in a year-on-year comparison, is driving [11 percentage points more views](https://x.com/ilblog/status/1857133202495992136) in favor of smaller websites. This is happening as a result of more sophisticated personalization.

![The share of views of the top 50 websites on Seznam's homepage shows 11 percentage points more views year-on-year for smaller websites.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/e3fe9e7c-ec39-4bf1-9249-baa8bf96e9ea_1920x1080.png "1456x819")
