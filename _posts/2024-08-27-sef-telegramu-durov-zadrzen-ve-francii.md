# Telegram CEO Durov detained in France, investigation continues
> 

The CEO and co-founder of the messenger [Telegram](https://telegram.org/) [Pavel Durov](https://cs.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pavel_Durov) was detained by French authorities, allegedly due to accusations that the platform inadequately moderates content and facilitates criminals. Durov was detained after arriving from Azerbaijan and [his investigative detention was extended for 96 hours](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2024/8/25/telegram-messaging-app-ceo-pavel-durov-arrested-in-france), during which a court must decide on further proceedings.

> Yesterday, state prosecutor Laure Beccau issued a [press release](https://www.tribunal-de-paris.justice.fr/sites/default/files/2024-08/2024-08-26%20-%20CP%20TELEGRAM%20.pdf) outlining the suspicions for which Pavel Durov will likely remain in custody.

Telegram stated in its response that it complies with European laws and that it is absurd to shift responsibility for the misuse of the platform onto its owner. Durov often travels throughout Europe and has, by his own account, nothing to hide.

Some sources report that official [Russian officials were instructed](https://www.novinky.cz/clanek/valka-na-ukrajine-vymazte-sve-konverzace-z-telegramu-naridili-ruskym-cinitelum-40485393) to remove their conversations from Telegram. The Russian regime is apparently concerned that conversations of its officials could be handed over to French authorities.

> [The arrest and controversies surrounding Pavel Durov](https://podcasts.apple.com/cz/podcast/francouzi-zatkli-rusk%C3%A9ho-zuckerberga-co-jim-vad%C3%AD-na/id1462047800?i=1000666663339) are also the subject of a podcast by Ondřej Kundra on Respekt.

The responsibility for platform misuse is now a topic of discussion among people on social networks. In March, Durov told the Financial Times that Telegram plans to introduce AI-based content moderation.

> The weblog20 profile on Twitter summarizes [what we know and don't know about Telegram](https://x.com/weblog20/status/1828307326148743246). According to that summary, it cannot be said with certainty that Telegram is as free a network as it presents itself.

The Russian [Ministry of Foreign Affairs requested](https://x.com/ajit4g/status/1827713967911039098) the possibility for a member of the Russian consular corps to visit Durov in custody.
