# APEK: June boom in online service purchases
> 

In June, the [number of Czechs purchasing online services](https://www.apek.cz/clanky/on-line-sluzby-objednalo-v-cervnu-nejvice-cechu-v) reached record values for this year, exceeding 28% of internet users, which is 3-4 percentage points more than in previous months.

> "In the second quarter, both the number of shoppers and average spending are higher than in the first quarter. This confirms that Czech e-commerce is thriving again and sales are growing."\
> **—[Jan Vetyška](https://www.apek.cz/clanky/on-line-sluzby-objednalo-v-cervnu-nejvice-cechu-v), Association for Electronic Commerce**

The main motivation is securing holidays, which is also confirmed by APEK's executive director Jan Vetyška. The average spending on services was 5,764 CZK. The APEK research also revealed a stabilized economic situation of households, with 85.4% of internet users purchasing goods online in June with an average spending over 4,300 CZK. The results show a growing trend in Czech e-commerce.
