# Nearly 40% of us avoid internet ads
> 

> "Men block ads more often than women, at 34% compared to 19%."

According to a survey by [ResSolution Group](https://www.ressolution.cz/novinky), nearly 40% of Czech internet users avoid ads, most commonly using an AdBlock tool. Younger people under 34 are the largest group who pay to avoid seeing ads. Aggressive formats, such as pop-ups and unskippable video ads, are considered the most annoying. Despite efforts to block ads, however, most respondents admit that by blocking, websites lose revenue. [MediaGuru writes more about the research.](https://www.mediaguru.cz/clanky/2024/10/internetove-reklame-se-vyhyba-temer-40-ceskych-uzivatelu/)
