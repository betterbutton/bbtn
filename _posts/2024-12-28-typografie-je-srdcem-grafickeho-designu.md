# Typography is the heart of graphic design
> 

[Typography is the foundation of graphic design.](https://medium.com/@1way.todigital/importance-of-typography-in-graphic-design-cfff97438426) It influences not only aesthetics but also readability and perception of the message. Its history dates back to Gutenberg's invention of the printing press, while today it adapts to modern media and technologies. Typography determines the tone of communication – for example, sans-serif expresses modernity, while serif expresses tradition. Key elements such as kerning, tracking, and leading play a role in the visual harmony of text. The correct choice and application of typography ensure a user-friendly experience and effective message delivery.
