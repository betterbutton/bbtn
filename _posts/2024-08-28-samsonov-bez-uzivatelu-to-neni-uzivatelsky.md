# Samsonov: Without Users, It's Not User Research and AI Can't Replace It
> 

Pavel Samsonov [criticizes designers' growing reliance on AI for user research](https://uxmag.com/articles/no-ai-user-research-is-not-better-than-nothing-its-much-worse). It supposedly leads to the degradation of products into commodities. Pavel warns that AI-generated outputs are not real research, but rather a quick path to destroying product uniqueness. He argues that AI research does not reflect real user needs and will ultimately lead to loss of competitive advantage.

> "If we normalize the use of LLMs to understand user needs, we will abandon our role as 'champions of users' and open a race to the bottom."\
> **— Pavel Samsonov, [No, AI User Research is Not “Better Than Nothing”—It’s Much Worse](https://uxmag.com/articles/no-ai-user-research-is-not-better-than-nothing-its-much-worse)**

The fundamental problem is that AI has no own experiences, and therefore cannot provide deep, user-oriented, and experience-formed insights that are key to quality design.
