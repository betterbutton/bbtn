# Every fourth mobile user enlarges text
> 

*“We only tested with four respondents, but one of them had to go get their glasses,”* our students said during the Design sprint course, *“we need to enlarge the text.”* We regularly advertise small text to students, but worse, we often advertise it to professional designers too. Statistics prove us right.

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/a9dfc946-7c47-4c51-8cfe-f7c3e0dd337a_1473x795.png "1456x786")

According to findings from the Dutch initiative Appt, independently of the operating system used, [a quarter of users enlarge the default font on mobile](https://appt.org/en/about).
