# Miro: AI as Another Team Member
> 

*„Every AI application looks like a chat. Until now,“* [says Mitchell Clements about the novelty](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7219339860648607744/) on the interactive Miro board. Miro has introduced [AI Sidekick](https://miro.com/ai/), an AI assistant that moves around the canvas like another user. This tool analyzes ideas, shares feedback, identifies gaps, and generates new ideas.

> „Instead of replacing a team member, Sidekick becomes another team member.“\
> **— [Mitchell Clements](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7219339860648607744/), Linkedin**

Unlike traditional chatbots that require specific instructions, AI Sidekick integrates AI directly into the creative process on the canvas. With this approach, Miro does not intend to replace team members but to provide an additional perspective and support, allowing AI to build on human ideas.
