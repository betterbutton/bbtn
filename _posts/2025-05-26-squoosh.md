# Squoosh will reduce the data volume of images directly in your browser
> A web tool for compressing images without uploading – locally, with quality, and ideally for high-performance websites.

![Screenshot from the Squoosh tool](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/1632daf7-30a8-4939-9996-82d5bf047d33_1896x1071.png "586x330")

[Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/) is a tool for converting formats and compressing images

[Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/) is a simple application that runs directly in your browser and allows you to reduce the size of images without losing quality. You don't need to install anything or upload anywhere – everything happens on your device, so your images remain safe. The application supports modern formats like WebP, AVIF, or MozJPEG, and often can reduce the file size to less than a third of the original value.

Using it is very easy. You upload an image, immediately see the difference, and then save the modified file. You can also adjust quality or color settings if you want to achieve the smallest possible size. Thanks to the split view, you can easily compare the original and modified versions and immediately know what you'll get.

Smaller images mean faster page loading and a better user experience, especially on slower connections. The application is not intended for automated deployment, but it is ideal for manual preparation of images before publishing. It will be appreciated by both developers, as well as designers and content managers.

The application runs on technology that enables fast processing directly in the browser. This is particularly advantageous when you need to maintain privacy or are working with sensitive data.

Squoosh is a smart helper for anyone who wants to keep their images under control and make their website as fast as possible.
