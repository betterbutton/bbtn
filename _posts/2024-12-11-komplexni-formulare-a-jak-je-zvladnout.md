# Complex Forms and How to Master Them
> 

[Caroline Jarrett](https://www.effortmark.co.uk/) at the Content Club meeting in London presented, [how form design influences results](https://www.effortmark.co.uk/talk-content-club-forms/). She focused on [the differences between forms and surveys](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/getting-forms-right-how-better-words-lead-to-better-results-e317/272428971), which are determined by the purpose of their questions: forms serve individuals, surveys aggregate data. There was also discussion about the complexity of forms, where the key is the protocol of questions.

A beneficial part was the debate on the complexity matrix, which compares the number of users with the frequency of use. Inspiration was the inclusion of forms for managing medical services. The meeting showed how crucial precision is in both words and design. [The full recording is available on YouTube.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qjgs2MqxVGU)
