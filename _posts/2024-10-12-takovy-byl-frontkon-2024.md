# Jan Polzer: Such Was FrontKon 2024
> 

[FrontKon 2024](https://www.frontkon.cz/) saw a record attendance of 700 visitors and confirmed that the originally community-driven event has grown into a professional conference, writes Jan Polzer in his [look back at the annual meeting of Frontendists](https://www.maxiorel.cz/frontkon-2024-letosnim-rocnikem-puvodne-komunitni-akce-dospela-ve-vyzralou-konferenci). This year, topics of web accessibility, which will become mandatory in 2025, and server-side rendering dominated.

The organization was precise, including the modern O2 Universum venue and the user-friendly Eventee app. Miloš Čermák humorously introduced AI as a changing force, while Martin Michálek emphasized the importance of website speed. FrontKon once again proved it's an event worth visiting in the coming years.
