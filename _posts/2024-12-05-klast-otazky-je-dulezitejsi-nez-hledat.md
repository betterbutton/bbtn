# Asking questions is more important than finding solutions
> 

> “The quality of your solutions depends on the kind of questions you ask.”\
> **— [Urvi Thakare](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/why-you-should-come-up-with-more-questions-than-solutions-059505b1e2f2**

[Einstein's method](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/why-you-should-come-up-with-more-questions-than-solutions-059505b1e2f2) recommends spending 55 minutes analyzing the problem and only 5 minutes finding a solution. An approach based on asking questions instead of striving for immediate answers helps identify the real causes of troubles.

For example, instead of trying to make the registration button more visible, ask why users are not completing the registration process. Good questions lead to more accurate and innovative solutions.
