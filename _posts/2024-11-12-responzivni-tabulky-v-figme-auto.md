# Responsive tables in Figma with auto-layout
> 

Tables are a common element in UI design, especially in enterprise applications. For creating responsive, scalable tables [in Figma, the column-based approach](https://medium.com/@revanth.krishna.444/columnar-tables-the-best-way-to-build-tables-on-figma-3514562a9abe) based on auto-layout is most effective. This approach simplifies working with column widths, facilitates reorganization and adding new columns or rows.

Each column is an auto layout containing cells, which ensures the table's responsiveness without complex manual cell width settings. By adding "gutters" between columns, the free space is distributed evenly, increasing the flexibility of the entire table.
