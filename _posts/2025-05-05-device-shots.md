# With Device Shots, you can create professional website or app mockups on a range of devices
> An online tool for easily creating realistic visualizations of websites and mobile apps on devices – ideal for social media and presentations.

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/dabf0bfa-a9d5-4c50-86bd-7033b35394e7_1896x1078.png "1456x828")

[Device Shots](https://deviceshots.com/) will create a website or app mockup

[Device Shots](https://deviceshots.com/) is a free web tool that helps you showcase your app or website in a professional way. Just upload a screenshot and place it into the frame of your chosen device. You can then download the result as an image or vector file.

You can choose from many different devices – phones, tablets, laptops, monitors, TVs, and even smartwatches. You can add custom text to the design, adjust the font, color, and size, and simply move it to the desired location.

If you are creating content for social media, you will appreciate the ready-made templates for the most popular platforms. This way, you can create graphics quickly and without worrying about dimensions. Need a different format? You can set the canvas precisely to your needs.

You can choose the background color from a preset palette, mix it manually, or enter an exact value. This ensures the final design matches your visual identity. Using layer tools, you can easily arrange individual elements as needed.

Device Shots is an ideal choice for developers, marketers, and website administrators. If you need to create high-quality graphics quickly and don't want to get bogged down with graphic software, this is the solution. Just a few clicks and you're done.
