# UX Maturity in the Company
> 

UXinsight is collecting questionnaires in its [UX Maturity Benchmark 2024](https://web.norstatsurveys.com/survey/selfserve/53c/2408572?list=100#?) survey. It asks UX professionals from companies (not agencies or freelancers) about the level of maturity of UX within their organization. The result will be the opportunity to compare yourself with others and find out how you are doing right now.

Similarly, the [self-assessment of UX maturity](https://forms.nngroup.com/s3/Maturity-Quiz) from the Nielsen Norman Group works – just without the option to compare with others.
