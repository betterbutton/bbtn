# Hall: Designers as Heroes, or Shaping the Future with Design
> 

[](https://player.vimeo.com/video/351167991)

Play

Erika Hall in her talk [Designing for the Triple Storyline](https://vimeo.com/351167991) at UX London 2019 highlighted the crucial role of designers in shaping the future. She warns against narrowing the focus too much on good user experience as a goal, which can mask deeper societal and environmental consequences.

Erika proposes expanding the design approach to include three dimensions: benefit for the user, profit for the business, and sustainability for the planet. She advocates for thoughtful planning of systems that create real value without exploiting users or natural resources.
