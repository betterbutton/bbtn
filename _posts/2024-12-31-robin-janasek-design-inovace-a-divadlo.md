# Robin Janásek: Design, Innovation, and Theater
> 

[Robin Janásek](https://www.linkedin.com/in/robinjanasek?originalSubdomain=cz), Chief Innovation Officer at [Proof & Reason](https://www.proofreason.com/), shares his perspective on design, innovation, and the role of mistakes as opportunities for growth. In a conversation with [Jana Kopecká](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jana-kopecka/?originalSubdomain=cz), he describes his [journey from graphics and theater to leading research and design teams](https://medium.com/design-kisk/robin-jan%C3%A1sek-po%C5%99%C3%A1d-je%C5%A1t%C4%9B-nen%C3%AD-pravidlem-aby-firmy-v-chyb%C3%A1ch-vid%C4%9Bly-p%C5%99%C3%ADle%C5%BEitost-k-posunu-6a96d9f48b1c).

“A mistake is an essential part of progress, and the company does not punish it.”\
**— Robin Janásek**

Robin explains that mistakes and feedback are key to progress, a principle the company actively supports. He sees importance in the way of thinking about problems, open communication, and a deeper understanding of the business. Besides professional ambitions, he also emphasizes educating new designers and long-term collaboration with clients.
