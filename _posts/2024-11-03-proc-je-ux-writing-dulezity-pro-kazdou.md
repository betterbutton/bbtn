# Why is UX writing important for every application?
> 

UX writing is often an underestimated but important part of creating user experience. Instead of design, it focuses on clear and [friendly text in digital products](https://medium.com/@ashrafulislamuiux/ux-writing-the-overlooked-element-of-user-experience-cf1b4b5e7f8a), which makes it easier for users to navigate. Thanks to well-thought-out phrases in buttons and menus, users feel more confident and less lost.

> „Good UX writing also means that everyone can understand and use the product, including people with disabilities.“\
> **— [Ashraful Islam](https://medium.com/@ashrafulislamuiux/ux-writing-the-overlooked-element-of-user-experience-cf1b4b5e7f8a)**

Quality UX writing is especially important for building trust, ensuring accessibility, and guiding users in the right direction, which positively reflects on overall user satisfaction. Writing texts is a dynamic process – success must be tested and gradually improved based on user reactions.
