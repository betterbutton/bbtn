# 10 UX Myths You Shouldn't Believe
> 

[A number of myths are spreading in UX design](https://www.icreatives.com/iblog/10-common-ui-ux-myths-debunked-and-the-truth-behind-them/), which can negatively impact the work and development of beginner designers. From underestimating the importance of white space to the mistaken idea that UX is only about aesthetics or that it necessarily requires coding knowledge – these incorrect assumptions can hinder effective design and user satisfaction.

> Remember, you are not the user, so you must constantly consider the expectations of your target group.

Every designer must focus on the real needs of users, not just their own expectations. On the path to success, constant skill updates and seeking feedback from the professional community are key.
