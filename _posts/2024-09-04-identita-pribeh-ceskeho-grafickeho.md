# Identity: The Story of Czech Graphic Design in a New Monograph
> 

![Identity Book](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/6c994b43-790b-48e2-a333-b6c82e8d40a7_2000x1500.jpeg "1456x1092")

Monograph [Identity – the story of Czech graphic design](https://projektidentita.cz/#content_plan3_ID) by Linda Kudrnovská and others brings a comprehensive view on more than a century-long history of Czech graphics and typography. We wrote about Identity at the beginning of the year when a documentary series aired on television [documentary series](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/02?utm_source=publication-search#:~:text=Martin%20mezi%20nimi.-,Identita,-.%20Tento%20po%C5%99ad). Now it returns [in book form](https://shop.bontonfilm.cz/identita/3723-identita-pribeh-ceskeho-grafickeho-designu.html).

The publication focuses on the development of the field from the perspective of significant creators, theorists, and current trends that shape our daily visual experience. Part of the book are interviews with leading Czech designers, such as Aleš Najbrt or Radim Peško. The individual chapters were written by renowned authors, for example Jiří Přibáň or Steven Heller. The graphic design is the work of Adéla Svobodová.
