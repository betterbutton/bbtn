# Will AI avatars replace PowerPoint?
> 

[Synthesia](https://www.synthesia.io/), London-based leader in AI video, is changing the way companies communicate. Its platform offers realistic avatar video presentations that can replace traditional PowerPoint. With over a million users and a valuation of over a billion dollars, the company builds on a fast, replicable, and cost-effective solution.

> „We don't know what is real and what is not, but the solution is to add metadata to videos that clearly indicates their origin.“\
> **— Alexandru Voica**

[Wired interviewed Alexandru Voica from Synthesia.](https://www.wired.cz/clanky/avatari-nahradi-power-point-rika-alexandru-voica-z-nejvetsi-ai-videoplatformy-synthesia) The novelty is the ability of avatars to naturally express emotions and soon they will master gestures and interaction. Synthesia aims for mainstream adoption of AI videos within a few years, with an emphasis on ethics and content safety, including protection against unauthorized deepfake videos.
