# Is the Microsoft UX Design Certificate Worth It?
> 

The [Microsoft UX Design Professional](https://www.coursera.org/programs/upskilling-with-coursera-e4f38/professional-certificates/ux-design) certification exceeded the expectations of an experienced designer, [writes Rithy Bun in his review](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/microsoft-ux-design-professional-certificate-review-b9521a3ce7bf). The course covered a wide range of topics from user research to design system creation, with an emphasis on practical skills that can be immediately applied to projects.

> “Earning the certificate felt rewarding. Given Microsoft’s brand reputation, this certification is a valuable addition to my portfolio.”\
> **— [Rithy Bun](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/microsoft-ux-design-professional-certificate-review-b9521a3ce7bf)**

Excellent quality of materials, support, and the opportunity for feedback from the community significantly facilitated progress. The greatest benefit were the practical projects, which not only deepened knowledge but also enriched the graduate's portfolio. Thanks to the Microsoft brand, the certificate strengthens professional credibility and can be a valuable asset for a future career.
