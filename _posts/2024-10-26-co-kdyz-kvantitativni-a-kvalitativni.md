# What if quantitative and qualitative data disagree
> 

When qualitative and quantitative data do not align, it's not about determining a winner, but creating a comprehensive story that includes both perspectives. Archana Shah recommends collaboration between teams that allows [thoroughly examining data and generating new hypotheses](https://medium.com/lexisnexis-design/what-to-do-when-qual-and-quant-disagree-18a535164ca6).

> „Present the findings to the stakeholders together. Layout the entire story together. New perspectives, data points and hypotheses that would have otherwise been left unexplored will emerge.“\
> — **[Archana Shah](https://medium.com/lexisnexis-design/what-to-do-when-qual-and-quant-disagree-18a535164ca6)**

For example, if customers believe they frequently use a certain feature, but the data doesn't show it, it's possible they are looking for it elsewhere. The key is to examine data together, verify hypotheses, and find a story that explains both perspectives. This supports a culture of learning and openness to change.
