# The 5 Why Method Will Make You Better Designers
> 

[The 5 Why Method](https://kisk.phil.muni.cz/100metod/5-proc) is a simple but powerful tool for uncovering the root causes of problems in design. By asking 'why' repeatedly, you uncover layer by layer the core of the problem, like peeling an onion. Using this method allows product designers to understand the real needs of users and make informed design decisions. Check out an example of using the method in the article [The 5 Why Framework for Product Designers (And Why It’s a Game Changer)](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/the-5-why-framework-for-product-designers-and-why-its-a-game-changer-ecb57cc42321).
