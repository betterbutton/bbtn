# Practical Tips for Ethical UX Research
> 

Michele Valongo presents [ethical principles in user research](https://medium.com/@michelevalongo/princ%C3%ADpios-%C3%A9ticos-em-ux-research-07050e1b36b1). They include protecting privacy, ensuring anonymization, respecting diversity, and transparent communication with users.

> “We must remain transparent about the goals and methods of the research. Avoiding manipulation or deception is fundamental to maintaining participant trust and data validity.”\
> **— [Michele Valongo](https://medium.com/@michelevalongo/princ%C3%ADpios-%C3%A9ticos-em-ux-research-07050e1b36b1)**

She emphasizes obtaining informed consent, maintaining confidentiality and integrity during data collection and interpretation. Another principle is social responsibility – UX researchers should consider the potential impact of their products on broader society. Proper adherence to these principles ensures research credibility and fosters a healthy relationship between researcher and user.
