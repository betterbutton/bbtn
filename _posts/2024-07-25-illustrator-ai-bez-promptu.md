# Illustrator changes the game: AI generation without prompts
> 
> "You draw a shape and let the artificial intelligence figure it out by itself. No prompt engineering is needed. Everything in vectors."\
> **— [Filip Greš](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7221778770494464000/)**

Iconographer [Filip Greš writes](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7221778770494464000/), that Adobe Illustrator has introduced an exciting update. Now you can draw a shape and [let AI work without the need to formulate prompts](https://www.adobe.com/products/illustrator/ai.html). Everything in vectors, which allows for easy manipulation of the result. This new feature brings control and simplicity, where prompts are only used for fine-tuning details.

Illustrator still holds its position among design tools even after 15 years of use, which speaks to its quality and innovativeness.
