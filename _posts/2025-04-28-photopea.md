# Photopea will replace graphic editors
> Photopea – a web-based graphic editor with Photoshop-level features, local file management, AI tools, and flexible cloud storage for digital professionals.

![Screenshot of the Photopea web application](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/50797304-85b8-4ead-9d15-aaef5eacedcc_1527x1079.png "1456x1029")

The graphic editor [Photopea](https://www.photopea.com/) runs in the browser.

[Photopea](https://www.photopea.com/) is a powerful graphic editor that works directly in an internet browser. It offers a user experience similar to Photoshop and allows image editing and illustration creation without needing to install anything. Users can work with formats such as PSD, JPEG, PNG, GIF, SVG, and PDF. Files are saved locally by default, so personal data remains secure.

The editor offers a wide range of professional tools. For example, it includes spot healing, clone stamp, work with layers, masks, and smart objects. The interface is very similar to Photoshop, which will be appreciated by those who have already worked with Adobe tools.

Photopea includes ads for free. However, you can get a paid subscription. With it, you get an ad-free environment, more cloud storage, a longer editing history, and three thousand credits for using artificial intelligence features. Photopea also offers the option to run on your own servers for a monthly fee.

Projects can be saved either to your device, to PeaDrive, or directly to services like Dropbox and Google Drive. The free version provides 500 MB of space, the paid version 5 GB.

The author of the Photopea editor is [Ivan Kutskir](https://blog.ivank.net/about). He is originally from Ukraine, but since 2009 he has been studying in Prague at Matfyz [and has stayed in the Czech Republic](https://www.matfyz.cz/clanky/matfyzak-vyviji-cesky-photoshop). Photopea has become a popular choice for digital creators looking for a powerful, accessible, and easy-to-use editor.

For creators, managers, and operators of web and mobile applications, Photopea is the ideal tool for everyday graphic work. You won't need any other bitmap graphic editor.
