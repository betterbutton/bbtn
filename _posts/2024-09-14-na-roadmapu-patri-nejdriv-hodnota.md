# Value before features on the roadmap
> 

Features are overemphasized on the roadmap. The real meaning of the roadmap, according to Pavel Samsonov, lies in [determining the fastest path to achieving valuable results](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7239688335537975296/), not just in planning the fastest release of features.

> „Feature roadmaps are not *not* important - stakeholders like to know what they can expect when - but there's a much more valuable purpose where a roadmap is invaluable *to the team*.”\
> **— [Pavel Samsonov](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7239688335537975296/)**

It's important to start from the target value and plan activities that lead to it. This approach helps avoid shortcuts that ultimately lead to failure. Although planning with the end value in mind can be challenging, skipping this step often brings even more work.
