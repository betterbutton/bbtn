# Site Check by Frontend Dogma - Test your website in over 90 tools
> Instant access to performance, SEO, accessibility, and UX tools. All in one place.

![Screenshot of the Site Check tool.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/8e4cf966-2762-40e0-af5d-bc739b19fa4f_1021x637.png "1021x637")

[Site Check by Frontend Dogma](https://frontenddogma.com/tools/check/) makes 90 tools available from one place

[Site Check by Frontend Dogma](https://frontenddogma.com/tools/check/) is a web tool that gives you access to over ninety testing services with a single click. You enter your website's address and within a few seconds launch a whole series of tests – from loading speed and accessibility to SEO and responsive design.

The tools are carefully selected. You'll find well-known services like [WAVE](https://wave.webaim.org/) or [GTmetrix](https://gtmetrix.com/) among them, but also lesser-known utilities – such as mobile device emulators, HTTP header analyzers, or favicon testers. This saves you time and gives you a thorough overview of your website's condition without unnecessary tab switching.

The tool is free, requires no installation or registration. Just enter the website address and start testing immediately. Frontend Dogma constantly develops and expands the tool. The developer community can get involved by suggesting new tools or improvements.

Whether you're a developer, tester, or team lead, Site Check will help you quickly find out how your website stands in terms of performance, security, usability, and technical execution. It's a smart helper that should be in every browser.
