# Czech freelancers are thriving
> 

The results of the Na volné noze portal survey show that [Czech freelancers are thriving well](https://navolnenoze.cz/novinky/jaksedari/), with 97% of them feeling the same or better than before while working independently. The biggest advantages are flexibility (92%) and freedom, but they also face challenges such as irregular income and the blurring of boundaries between work and private life.

The profile of a typical freelancer includes higher education (67%), computer-based work (91%), and a strong focus on personal growth and education (88%).
