# The Art of Asking Questions in Design
> 

The question of how much a designer should ask is crucial for the quality of their work. [Tomáš Svěrák recalls](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7219291541285019648/) his experience at Proof & Reason, where he was nicknamed *Bold designer* precisely because of his curiosity. Colleague designer Lucie Sikorová introduced him as someone who asks twice as much as she does.

> "I'm just a designer and I don't understand this topic at all, can you please explain it to me?"\
> **— [Tomáš Svěrák](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7219291541285019648/)**

From Tereza Kosnarová, he learned to use the phrase *"I'm just a designer and I don't understand this topic at all, can you please explain it to me?"*. According to Tomáš, designers should overcome the pressure for quick results and devote themselves to a thorough understanding of the problem in order to collaborate effectively and create quality products.
