# Microinteractions shape user experiences
> 

Microinteractions – small design elements such as button animations or color changes – are subtle but crucial components of UX. Details create an emotional connection between the user and the product, facilitate navigation, and build trust.

Bismark Gyebi Duah emphasizes that these invisible threads often determine how pleasant the user experience is, even though they remain outside the main focus. While larger design choices dominate discussions, precisely microinteractions have a profound impact on user behavior and their overall satisfaction.
