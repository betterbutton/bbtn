# Alza began selling over-the-counter drugs
> 

Alza is newly expanding its assortment with [over-the-counter drugs](https://www.alza.cz/volne-prodejne-leky/18910726.htm) and homeopathic remedies, [writes the magazine Živě](https://www.zive.cz/clanky/v-alze-uz-koupite-ibalgin-i-homeopatika-koupila-kamennou-lekarnu-aby-mohla-prodavat-online/sc-3-a-230536/default.aspx). After acquiring a brick-and-mortar [pharmacy in Zdicích](https://www.alza.cz/article/44622.htm), it met legislative conditions and can offer these products online.

Customers will order drugs with delivery to AlzaBox, which is a competitive step against established sellers like [Dr. Max](https://www.drmax.cz/) or [Pilulka.cz](https://www.pilulka.cz/). However, the company will not expand to foreign markets for now.

In case of success on the Czech market, it is likely that Alza will lobby for a change in legislation to allow selling prescription drugs over the internet as well.
