# The Legendary MetaFilter Has Been With Us for 25 Years
> 

Celebrating an incredible 25 years of existence today is [MetaFilter.com](https://www.metafilter.com/)! Don't know it? MetaFilter is a pioneering website in many respects. It launched two years before Wikipedia and brought community-moderated content to the internet. Today, our news feeds are primarily managed by social networks, but in 1999, the internet was ruled by discussions, email conferences, IRC (and ICQ). When something appeared on MetaFilter, web servers usually went to their knees.

Where Quora would shine a few years later, MetaFilter already launched AskMetaFilter in 2003. Its influence on internet culture is undeniable.
