# Linkodrome

**What does Googlebot see on your website?** [Zdeněk Dvořák reminds us](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7284466213773021184/) that we should occasionally open the preview of how our website is displayed in [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/). This will show us how the search engine crawler renders the page.

**Search engines, index now!** Would you like to know the incantation that makes a search engine robot come to index a newly created webpage immediately? That's exactly how the IndexNow API works. [You register your API key](https://www.bing.com/indexnow/getstarted), and then you can ping search engines with a new URL for indexing. The good news is that you only need to notify one of the search engines that support IndexNow, as they share information among themselves. Google hasn't joined the party yet, but the protocol [is supported by the Czech Seznam and also by Bing](https://www.indexnow.org/faq), so your addresses will quickly reach users of Copilot and ChatGPT as well.

**Myths about UX.** Aleš Krejčí translated into Czech [an overview of common myths](https://ales.net/ux-myty/). Since we've been teaching, we've been trying to find sources for students of popular sayings and claims about correct design. We often find that the claim has some basis in reality, but its validity is strongly conditioned by a specific context – no, they are simply nonsense, and now you [even have a link](https://uxmyths.com/) you can send people when they come to you with something again.

**How to test page appearance.** Visual design influences users' first impressions and brand perception. To test appearance, we can use attitudinal methods (e.g., [the five-second test](https://kisk.phil.muni.cz/100metod/petisekundovy-test), [the first-click test](https://maze.co/resources/glossary/first-click-testing/), or [preference testing](https://userpilot.com/blog/preference-testing/)), which explore first impressions, or behavioral methods ([A/B testing](https://www.ilincev.com/a-b-testovani), [eyetracking](https://kisk.phil.muni.cz/100metod/eye-tracking)), which analyze the impact on behavior. The choice of method depends on the goals – from aesthetics to usability. Megan Chan will help you choose the right method in the article [Testing Visual Design: A Comprehensive Guide](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/testing-visual-design/).
