# The CyberSchool project offers schools packages for teaching cybersecurity.
> 
[CyberSchool](https://kyberskola.framer.website/) is a project by students of the Department of Multimedia at the University of Economics and was created during a project seminar. [Students want to improve cybersecurity education](https://kme.vse.cz/aktuality/kyberskola-inovativni-projekt-pro-vyuku-kyberbezpecnosti-na-zakladnich-skolach/) in primary schools and bring teachers easy-to-use and effective teaching packages.
