**Animation effects longer than 500 milliseconds** worsen the Web Vitals metric, [writes on Twitter Machal](https://x.com/machal/status/1891759207659053150). An animated effect in code influences the [Cumulative Layout Shift](https://web.dev/articles/cls) metric and can affect user experience and, consequently, search result placement.

**A template for clear and concise design documentation.** When documenting design systems, designers struggle with the clarity of instructions for using their components. Michael Haggerty-Villa shares a proven method: [using a structured table](https://www.buttonconf.com/blog/how-to-bring-structure-and-clarity-to-design-system-components), which helps systematize content and speed up documentation creation.

**Hyper-legible font and immediately in seven variants.** Atkinson Hyperlegible is a font designed for readers with impaired vision. The new variant, Atkinson Hyperlegible Next, offers seven weights for variable font and support for over one hundred fifty languages. Atkinson Hyperlegible Mono is a monospaced version intended for tables or developer editors. All variants are available [free for personal and commercial use](https://www.brailleinstitute.org/freefont/).
