# The Future of E-commerce in the Czech Republic According to Honza Kvasnička

> UX expert [Honza Kvasnička analyzes current errors of Czech e-shops](https://www.3it.cz/kam-kraci-ceska-e-commerce-podle-honzy-kvasnicky/) in an interview with Michalis Katapodis. The biggest problems he sees in repeating basic UX errors, even among large players, and underestimating simple improvements, such as working with discount codes or clear product recommendations.

> Czech e-commerce is among the best because we have huge competition and e-shops must do many things really well."\
> **— Jan Kvasnička**

The future he sees in professionalization, personalization, and automation, especially with the help of AI. He recommends thorough market analysis and local customs when expanding abroad. According to him, Czech e-commerce is among the best and has room for growth even for smaller specialized players.
