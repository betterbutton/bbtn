# Lectures from the INSPO ′24 conference are online
> 

A few days ago, the INSPO conference on assistive technologies for people with specific needs was held at the Congress Palace in Prague. In the morning session, you could watch a lecture on [using alternative aids in everyday life](https://www.inspo.cz/prednasky/vyuzivani-alternativnich-pomucek-v-beznem-zivote/), [on mental health care with the help of online therapy](https://www.inspo.cz/prednasky/pece-o-dusevni-zdravi-s-pomoci-online-terapie/), or [on alternative control of Apple products](https://www.inspo.cz/prednasky/alternativni-ovladani-produktu-apple/). The afternoon session then featured a highlight [on the law on accessibility requirements for certain products and services](https://www.inspo.cz/prednasky/zakon-o-pozadavcich-na-pristupnost-nekterych-vyrobku-a-sluzeb/) and [on its weaknesses](https://www.inspo.cz/prednasky/slaba-mista-zakona-o-pozadavcich-na-pristupnost-nekterych-vyrobku-a-sluzeb/).

Recordings from the [morning joint session](https://www.facebook.com/TeiresiasCenter/videos/1519636785233425) and from the [afternoon session dedicated to web, app, and document accessibility](https://www.facebook.com/TeiresiasCenter/videos/542495812028518) are available online on the Facebook profile of the organizer, which is the Teiresias center. The recordings are also interesting in their format. In addition to footage of the speakers and presentations, they also show interpretation into sign language and live transcription.
