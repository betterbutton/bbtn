# Consent or Pay Now Also at Economia
> 

Economia is joining Seznam, Mafra, and CNC in applying the "German approach" to consent for personal data processing for visitors to their websites. [Economia is introducing so-called](https://www.mediaguru.cz/clanky/2024/11/economia-zpoplatnuje-nesouhlasy-se-zpracovanim-dat-rozsireni-zvazi/) *[consent wall](https://www.mediaguru.cz/clanky/2024/11/economia-zpoplatnuje-nesouhlasy-se-zpracovanim-dat-rozsireni-zvazi/)*, which requires payment for access to ad-free content if users do not consent to personal data processing.

> "The drop in programmatic advertising revenue can be 20 to 40%. In the case of Czech media houses, this amounts to tens to hundreds of millions of crowns,"
> **— David Voráček**

The fee is 79 CZK per month and so far applies to the website Žena.cz, with the possibility of expansion to other websites such as Aktuálně.cz. Media are introducing this model to compensate for the loss of revenue from targeted advertising, for which user data is lacking. Economia expects that media company revenues will in the future be more dependent on subscriptions than on advertising.
