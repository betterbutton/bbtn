# From July, eDocuments will be accepted by authorities, courts, and police.
> 

From July 1, it will be possible to use eDocuments in more places. They will now be accepted at employment offices, tax offices, regional authorities, municipalities with extended competence, courts, with the remaining part of the police, and at other locations.
