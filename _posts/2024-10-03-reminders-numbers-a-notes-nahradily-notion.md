# How Reminders, Numbers and Notes Replaced Notion
> 

Joan Westenberg explains why [she abandoned Notion and replaced it with Apple's native apps](https://medium.com/the-realist/how-i-replaced-notion-with-reminders-numbers-and-notes-38282543b29b): Reminders, Numbers and Notes. She appreciates their deep integration into the Apple ecosystem, speed, and privacy without needing to pay for a subscription.

> "The best productivity system is the one you’ll actually use."

Reminders serves as the central hub with a Kanban board feature, while Notes enables effective writing and Numbers provides data visualization. This trio offers a simpler but highly effective alternative to complex tools like Notion, which the author sees as a path to increased productivity and minimalism in tech setup.

Moreover, Joan saves 114 euros per year, so with a few more such savings, she'll soon be able to buy another iPad.
