# The Art of Empathy in UX Design
> 

Joanne Thomas shares how empathy shaped her journey from school compromises to professional design. As a [UX designer, she emphasizes empathy and inclusivity](https://medium.com/@joannetkm15/designing-for-humans-the-art-of-empathy-in-ui-ux-dbcb2d40dc22), so users feel supported and a sense of belonging. Thomas describes her experiences, such as working with an autistic boy, where she abandoned the script in favor of his enthusiasm for dinosaurs.

[Empathy maps](https://xplane.com/the-empathy-map-a-human-centered-tool-for-understanding-how-your-audience-thinks/) and [journey maps](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/journey-mapping-101/) are essential tools for her, bringing deeper understanding of the user experience. Thomas believes empathy is not just theory—it is the heart of every meaningful design that combines aesthetics with humanity.
