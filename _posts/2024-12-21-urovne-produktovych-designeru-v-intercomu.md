# Product Designer Levels at Intercom
> 

Intercom defines career growth for designers [from Associate to Senior Principal](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1YloFi80QoXPk5-U9ga1Ivxojamy7dU4MsaUNnQs8Rig/edit?tab=t.0) based on specific competencies, such as product leadership, craft quality, business acumen, and area expertise. Progression from Associate to Mid-Level is expected within 12 months, while moving to Senior takes about two years. Higher levels, like Staff and Principal, require developing leadership and broader impact on both the team and the company. Emphasis is placed on company values and regular feedback. Progression is not mandatory; the goal is excellence in the current role and contribution to the organization.
