# French media take dispute with X over payments for news previews to court
> 

Major French newspapers, such as Le Monde, Le Figaro and Le Parisien, have jointly [sued the social network X over rights to share content](https://techcrunch.com/2024/11/12/french-newspapers-want-social-media-platform-x-to-pay-for-news-reuse/). Under European legislation, digital platforms are obliged to negotiate compensation with publishers for the use of their content.

Meta and Google have already concluded agreements with French publishers, but X refuses to negotiate. The Paris court will therefore decide on the value of article previews shared on the X platform.
