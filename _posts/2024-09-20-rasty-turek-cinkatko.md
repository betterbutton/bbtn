# 

Rasty Turek on the peculiarities of founders and the future of entertainment

## 

·Cinkátko

For the [Cinkátko podcast](https://www.miton.cz/blog/rasty-turek-o-zvlastnostech-founderu-a-budoucnosti-zabavy/), Michala Gregorová invited [Rasty Turek](https://x.com/synopsi) – a serial founder of startups, known for founding the company Pex. They talk about the difference between the role of a founder and a classic manager. They discuss Paul Graham's essay [Founders Mode](https://paulgraham.com/foundermode.html) and the problems that founders face when they are pushed into management.

Rasty emphasizes that founders often bring deep knowledge of the company and the project, which is their advantage. At the same time, they risk being perceived as micromanagers. Founders also face pressure from investors and employees, which can lead to internal conflict.
