# From Chaos to Clarity: On Data Visualization and UX
> 

Data visualization is an integral part of UX design, enabling users to better understand complex information. The foundation is understanding the target audience, simplifying the presentation, and choosing the right type of visualization. [Effective visualizations facilitate decision-making](https://medium.com/glow-team/understanding-the-process-of-insurance-product-design-e9844b2d644e), reduce cognitive load, and increase user engagement.

“Data is only valuable when it is understood.”\
**— [Stanislav Kovalsky](https://medium.com/glow-team/understanding-the-process-of-insurance-product-design-e9844b2d644e)**

Best practices include the strategic use of color, interactive elements, contextual explanations, and adaptation to various devices. Avoid common mistakes such as information overload or insufficient user testing. The future of data visualization includes AI, real-time insights, and the use of virtual reality.
