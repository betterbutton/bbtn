# Definitive UX Guide for Business Owners
> 

UX directly impacts business. An example is the Al Yasmin restaurant in Riyadh, which increased online orders by 30% after improving its UX design. The key to success is providing a quality website experience, which helps companies retain customers and increase their satisfaction.

The growing market requires businesses to have not just a website, but also quality UX design. This means that business owners should also get involved in UX. However, we don't mean a role in the development team, but real entrepreneurs and business owners. At the MuseMind agency, they said they are preparing [an UX guide specifically for business owners](https://medium.com/@musemind.agency/the-ultimate-guide-to-ux-design-for-business-owners-in-riyadh-boost-conversions-and-grow-your-993556c5e31a). It's similar to [our book](https://reknisioweb.cz/p/kniha), which is aimed at clients.

Competition is high and customers expect fast, mobile-friendly, and easy-to-use pages. Good UX design ensures simple navigation, fast loading, and visible calls to action, which supports conversions.
