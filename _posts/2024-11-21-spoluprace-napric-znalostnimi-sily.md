# Collaboration across knowledge domains
> 

Effective [collaboration between UX designers and developers](https://medium.com/uxcentury/breaking-down-silos-how-ux-and-development-teams-can-collaborate-better-2b68f1577367) is key to user-friendly products. Unified goals from the start, joint meetings, and involving developers in design processes minimize misunderstandings. Communication enhanced by tools like Slack and Figma ensures smooth dialogue and quick problem resolution. Iterative approaches like Agile allow for flexibility and consideration of technical constraints.

Empathy between teams strengthens mutual understanding, for example through training or shadowing. Support from leadership and rewarding collaboration reinforce a culture of partnership, which leads to better outcomes and higher-quality products.
