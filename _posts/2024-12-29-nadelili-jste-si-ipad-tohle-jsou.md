# Did you get an iPad? These are the best iPad apps for developing your creativity
> 

iPad has become a powerful tool for creative work, whether it's digital art, video editing, or graphic design. Among the [best apps for creativity on iPad](https://techcrunch.com/2024/12/28/best-ipad-apps-for-unleashing-and-exploring-your-creativity/) according to TechCrunch magazine are:

- [Procreate](https://procreate.com/) for drawing and illustrations,

- [LumaFusion](https://filmora.wondershare.net/filmora-video-editor.html?gad_source=1&gclid=Cj0KCQiA4L67BhDUARIsADWrl7HDGThsrzPRIgJ1P_nTtHSJDO4VyF_BFM1vI8OV9O8hoUOCmNGsBm8aAmW6EALw_wcB) for video editing,

- [Canva](https://www.canva.com/download/ios/) for easy visual content design,

- [Affinity Designer 2](https://affinity.serif.com/en-us/designer/ipad/?srsltid=AfmBOoqPFIapL4N69-hxZVHFjlJt5i0FV77bkFEeYOUMDceVuw3oLrFr) for professional graphics and

- [Concepts](https://concepts.app/en/) for sketching and planning.

These apps offer an intuitive interface and a range of features that facilitate the creative process, either with one-time fees or as part of a subscription. Thanks to them, you can turn your iPad into a real creative studio.
