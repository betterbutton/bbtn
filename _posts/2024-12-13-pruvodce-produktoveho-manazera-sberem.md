# Product Manager's Guide to Picking Low-Hanging Fruit
> 
[Focus on Low-Hanging Fruit](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/a-product-managers-guide-to-harvesting-the-low-hanging-fruit-db7d41d84c18) is an effective product management strategy that brings quick results, builds team motivation, and improves stakeholder confidence. The key is to identify small but impactful changes using feedback, pattern observation, or data analysis.

Examples include simplifying user processes, such as shortening forms or improving app navigation. Successful implementations require prioritizing impact, team collaboration, and quick execution. Small wins, however, should not distract from bigger problems, and it's important not to overestimate their impact.
