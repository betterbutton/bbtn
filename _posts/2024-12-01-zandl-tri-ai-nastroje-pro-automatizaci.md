# Zandl: Three AI Tools for Automating Podcast Creation
> 
Patrick Zandl explored the possibilities offered by [current tools for automated podcast creation](https://www.marigold.cz/item/podcasty-z-textu-automaticky/). [Google NotebookML](https://notebooklm.google/) excels with the Audio Overview feature, generating realistic dialogues based on documents, but so far only in English. [Elevenlabs GenFM](https://elevenlabs.io/genfm) allows podcasts in Czech, albeit with limited customization and no easy export option. [Superpodcast](https://www.superpodcast.ai/) offers flexibility but suffers from overload.

NotebookML is ideal for analyzing source materials and academic work, while GenFM is suitable for quick personal use. The development of all mentioned services promises early expansion of functionality and support for more languages.

You may have noticed that [our podcast created from voiceovers for the newsletter](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/podcast) is also created with a synthesized voice. We use [Elevenlabs](https://elevenlabs.io/), where we have sampled our own voices to make the show sound authentic.
