# Leah Buley and Joe Natoli: New UX Team of One
> 

Leah Buley and Joe Natoli, authors of the second edition of the seminal book [UX Team of One](https://rosenfeldmedia.com/books/the-user-experience-team-of-one-second-edition/), were guests on the podcast of their publisher, Louise Rosenfeld. The principles of the first edition remain, but the authors have adapted the choice of methods to the fast pace of modern business.

Joe emphasizes that the new methods are faster, simpler, and more direct, helping to avoid unnecessary overthinking and extra work. The second edition offers practical and adaptable tools that assist UX professionals in various organizational contexts.
