# CZC.cz is now Allegro
> 

![E-mail announcing that the CZC.cz e-shop will be redirected to Allegro.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/51c18782-0188-4e73-bec3-8b1fb339be71_1354x944.jpeg "1354x944")

The domain czc.cz was redirected to the Allegro platform this evening and the original website is no longer available. Allegro announced its move a month ago. When can we expect a similar step from another Polish acquisition in the Czech Republic, Mall.cz?

![New look of CZC.cz with the Allegro logo in the header and the original name only in the banner.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/b82983ca-3fe6-4d54-8d05-b32459e372b2_1890x923.png "1456x711")
