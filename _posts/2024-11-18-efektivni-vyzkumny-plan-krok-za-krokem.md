# Effective Research Plan Step by Step
> 

A research plan is a key document for any UX study. It structures the research goals, methods, and logistics, ensuring its effectiveness and clarity for the entire team and stakeholders. It contains information about participants, the methodology used, relevant documents, and logistical details.

[How to organize, document, and format a research plan](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/pm-research-plan/) writes in an article on the Nielsen Norman Group website by Maria Rosala, and she also [shares a template in Word](https://media.nngroup.com/media/articles/attachments/Template_Research_Plan.docx).

> “A research plan ensures that no important information is missed during planning, creates transparency, builds confidence in the research, and is key for future reference and study replication.”
> **— Maria Rosala**

The plan helps maintain consistency and serves as reference material for future replications. It is recommended to share the plan with stakeholders and update it according to changes during the research. Creating a template for research plans will facilitate their use and ensure standardization across projects.
