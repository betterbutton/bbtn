# Tomáš Svěrák: Am I Still a UX Designer?
> 

Tomáš Svěrák reflects on [the differences between UX and CX in the context of digital companies](https://tomassverak.substack.com/p/jsem-jeste-ux-designer). He explains that User Experience (UX) is often narrowed down to [designing websites and applications in Figma](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jan2rezac_figmatizace-martin-kopta-m%C4%9B-nau%C4%8Dil-nov%C3%A9-activity-7275429667023536128-DJIK), while the actual user experience includes all interactions with the brand – from customer support to advertising.

In a B2C environment, where users are also customers, UX and CX intertwine. The key is to focus on aligning customer expectations with reality and improving their overall experience. Tomáš asks whether the term market orientation should have similarly strong representation as CX.
