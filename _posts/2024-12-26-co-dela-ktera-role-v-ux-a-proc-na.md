# What each role does in UX and why it matters
> 

UX design encompasses a wide spectrum of specializations that correspond to the five phases of the design thinking process: empathy, definition, ideation, prototyping, and testing. Roles such as UX designer, UI designer, UX manager, user researcher, or information architect contribute to creating user-friendly and visually appealing products.

Collaboration between these roles is a prerequisite for effective development. [In large companies, roles are specialized, while in smaller teams they often overlap.](https://insideuxdesign.medium.com/who-does-what-in-ux-a-detailed-look-at-the-roles-and-their-contributions-617b336d0001) Each role, from research to strategic management, plays a crucial role in creating digital products that are functional and inspiring.
