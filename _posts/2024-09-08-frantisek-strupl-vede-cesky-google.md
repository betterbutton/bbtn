# František Štrupl Leads Czech Google
> 

[František Štrupl](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fstrupl/) became the new [director of the Czech branch of Google](https://blog.google/intl/cs-cz/firemni-novinky/aktivity-googlu/frantisek-strupl-novym-sefem/), where he replaced Taťána le Moigne, [with whom we said goodbye](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/09?utm_source=publication-search#:~:text=Ta%C5%A5%C3%A1na%20le%20Moigne%20po%2018%20letech%20kon%C4%8D%C3%AD%20v%C2%A0roli%20%C5%99editelky%20%C4%8Desk%C3%A9%20pobo%C4%8Dky%20Googlu.) in the newsletter Better Button #09 this March. Taťána left after 18 years. František has been working at Google for over 12 years and many of us still remember him from his beginnings in the legendary H1.cz team. The new boss's task will be to lead local teams and further develop digital business in the Czech Republic, including expansion in the field of artificial intelligence.

František, we're rooting for you!
