# Five tips for clear and functional categories
> 

Category names on a website significantly influence user experience. To help people easily find what they're looking for, it's crucial to choose clear and descriptive names. Creative or internal terms might be tempting, but they often confuse.

> Users prefer clear over clever category names.

Therefore, it's important to use commonly understandable language, avoid category overlap, and structure the website based on users' mental models, not internal organization. User research methods, such as card sorting, can help evaluate name clarity and proper division.

Hoa Loranger and Taylor Dykes offer five tips, [how to name categories understandably](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/category-names-suck/):

1.  Be descriptive and relevant

2.  Avoid made-up terms

3.  Check that categories don't overlap

4.  Categorization based on users' mental models

5.  Don't rely on instincts
