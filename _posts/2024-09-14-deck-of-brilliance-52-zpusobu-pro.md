# Deck of Brilliance: 52 Ways to Generate Ideas
> 

![Deck of Brilliance (logo)](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/759a21ad-c7a9-4ee4-b568-c5c5223b86d6_251x120.svg "172x82")

> “Professionals don’t wait for ideas to come to them; they hunt them where they live.”\
> **— [Deck of Brilliance](https://deckofbrilliance.com/)**

Generating ideas is getting harder—deadlines are shorter and competition is growing. [Deck of Brilliance](https://deckofbrilliance.com/) offers 52 cards for creatives to rapidly develop ideas. Authors Juggi Ramakrishnan and Todd McCracken argue that a systematic approach is a necessity, as professionals in all fields use tools. Without them, the creative process can become chaotic and random. With these proven methods, developed over more than 50 years of practice, you’ll no longer need to wait for sudden flashes of inspiration. The tools are available for free, supplemented with examples from talented creatives around the world.
