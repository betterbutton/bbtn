# Linkodrome

**Seznam is testing AI to summarize articles linked from its homepage.** A screenshot posted on Twitter by [Petra Brzka](https://twitter.com/PetrBrzek/status/1771291869781102878) shows a "summary" label next to an article headline on Seznam's homepage. Clicking it opens an overlay with a summary of the linked article. According to [a statement by Marek Váček](https://x.com/VacekMarek/status/1770795056909164690), product manager for Seznam's homepage, this is currently just an A/B test. If you don't see the summary in your browser, try an incognito window or a different browser.

![Screenshot of the article summary from Seznam's homepage.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/6447f911-aaf1-441c-8a7f-524759b426b9.avif "600x338")

**NÚKIB won't make it to prepare the Czech law for the NIS2 cybersecurity directive.** We [wrote about the European NIS2 directive back in February](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/05). The directive categorizes companies and institutions by importance and imposes an obligation to protect their IT systems. [NÚKIB](https://osveta.nukib.cz/course/view.php?id=145), which is [responsible for preparing the NIS2 law](https://www.odok.cz/portal/veklep/material/ALBSCSSFKU7S/), estimates that it [will come into effect here next year at the earliest](https://www.lupa.cz/clanky/regulace-podle-nis2-nukib-termin-transpozice-smernice-nestihne-sankci-se-ale-neboji/).

# Life with Technologies

Jakub and Martin spoke at the [SEO Restart](https://www.seorestart.cz/ conference about current UX trends and their impact on search engine optimization. UX designers and SEO specialists sometimes struggle to find common ground. Current developments in search engines are bringing both digital specializations onto the same boat. For example, [Bing introduced Deep Search](https://searchengineland.com/microsoft-bing-deep-search-435446 and Google launched [Search Generative Experience](https://www.howtogeek.com/what-is-google-search-generative-experience/), both AI-powered search services that transform the search engine from a list of links into an answerer.

![Martin Kopta (left) and Jakub Goldmann on stage at the SEO Restart conference, with a slide behind them showing an AI chatbot on the Liverpool city council website.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/9d3ea39c-f224-4e3e-afff-b4534c467e26_600x400.webp "600x400")

Photo: © [Lukáš Zlatkovský](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lukaszlatkovsky/), SEO Restart 2024

User experience is becoming a signal for search engines. Metrics like [Core Web Vitals](https://web.dev/articles/vitals), [bounce rate](https://www.strafelda.cz/mira-opusteni), and [mobile-first](https://www.vzhurudolu.cz/prirucka/mobile-first) indexing attempt to quantify UX quality so that search engines can use it as one of the factors for ranking pages in search results.

> "Search engines want to keep users long-term, so they prioritize high-quality user experience on linked pages."
> **— Jakub Goldmann**

We focused on some of the trends:

- **AI Chatbots** – Integrating AI chatbots into websites shouldn't mean that knowledge bases, help sections, or FAQ lists disappear. Search engines need content to index. Without it, they can't determine how relevant a website is to a topic.

- **AI-generated and translated text** – AI-generated and machine-translated texts have low quality and added value. They're the [new lorem ipsum](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/pavel-samsonov-44ba2833_as-if-ai-generated-user-research-wasnt-activity-7174049062189436928-pj14/). The [ongoing core update at Google](https://blog.google/products/search/google-search-update-march-2024/) is targeting generated text, and for example, [Medium has made it clear](https://blog.medium.com/were-ramping-up-efforts-to-protect-the-medium-community-from-spam-and-fraud-7efaadd76247 that they don't value generated content. To avoid being ignored by search engines, texts should always be at least edited by a living person who adds their own touch of originality.

- **Mini-websites** – Single-page websites are easy to maintain but place high demands on link routing setup (and possibly server-side rendering) and proper metadata injection to allow search engines to correctly identify the beginning and end of individual thematic blocks. A specific case of single-page websites are [Single Page Apps](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Glossary/SPA) or profiles on various social networks and platforms.

- **Personalization** – Tailoring content to the user is primarily the domain of e-commerce and media, but thanks to tools like [Adobe Experience](https://business.adobe.com/cz/products/journey-optimizer/in-app-personalization.html), it's also reaching corporate websites. This means that the same URL can have slightly different content each time. Essentially, it's [a shady cloaking practice](https://rockcontent.com/blog/cloaking-in-seo/).

- **No-code** – The use of no-code tools is logically associated with limited influence over source code quality, which can impact [on-page SEO factors](https://yoast.com/what-is-onpage-seo/). However, the main benefit and simultaneously threat is the democratization of website creation, which is often accompanied by generally poorer information architecture. With tools like [Webnode](https://www.webnode.com/cs/blog/jak-vyuzit-umelou-inteligenci-pri-tvorbe-webu/ or [Wix](https://www.wix.com/ai-website-builder), generated content also comes into play, but we'd be repeating ourselves.

# What We're Currently Working On

Jakub and his colleagues from the [Švejda Goldmann](https://svejda-goldmann.cz/ agency are working on a more understandable and effective form of their analyses: *"We create really good detailed heuristics and quick scans. Thanks to them, we identify barriers in the user experience that complicate online shopping. We're really proud of them. We thought our reports were easy to understand, but it turns out that sometimes our clients interpret them differently than we intended. We're trying to change that."* He realizes that the key to success is not only that the analysis reveals problems, but also that it presents them understandably with practical steps for resolution. *"Our goal is to provide clear guidance and support the creation of intuitive and user-friendly digital products."*
