# How to design for little explorers aged 5–7 years
> 

The second part on designing for children follows up on [article on designing for children from two to four years](https://www.reknisioweb.cz/p/jak-navrhovat-pro-deti-ktere-se-naucily). [Designing UX for children aged 5–7 years](https://medium.com/x-periment-asteroid/little-users-big-needs-designing-ux-for-kids-chapter-2-7ff3f8ba7935) requires a combination of intuitive design, fun elements, and educational opportunities. It's important to adjust the size of touch areas for small fingers, use simple and consistent element layouts, and incorporate clear colors, readable texts, and interactive elements like drag-and-drop.

> „Learning through play is the best approach — they’re hungry for knowledge and eager to explore.“

Children will also enjoy engaging stories, audio support will help them with reading, and parental controls will ensure safety. Main goal? To teach through play and support their natural curiosity in an environment that is both inclusive and safe.
