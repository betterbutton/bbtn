# Language models are changing the rules of the game in e-commerce
> 

Jiří Rostecký invited the heavy weight in SEO, Pavel Ungr, to the podcast and they talked about [what impact SearchGPT will have on search](https://rostecky.cz/searchgpt-muze-zmenit-jak-lide-hledaji-na-internetu-co-to-znamena-pro-vas-marketing-pavel-ungr-t52636) and what impacts it will have on e-commerce as a whole.

The conversation focuses on comparing language models, such as ChatGPT, with traditional search engines like Google. Language models excel at answering specific queries, but so far lag behind in areas like e-commerce, where search engines offer broader context.

Jiří and Pavel also discuss the impact of AI on SEO, the benefits of personalization, and the future of voice search. According to Pavel, the path to success is creating high-quality content and building brand credibility, because cheap and average texts will be replaced by AI. Concerns about risks associated with AI were also raised, including misinformation or misuse of technology.
