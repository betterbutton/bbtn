# Designers from the UK Government's design community advise on content accessibility
> 

The first part of the Unlocking Accessibility series brings experiences from government content designers who share their first encounters with accessibility and [recommendations for creating accessible content](https://accessibility.blog.gov.uk/2024/11/05/unlocking-accessibility-content-designers-share-their-experience-and-advice/). From emphasizing plain language and the importance of alt text to structuring texts – the authors agree that accessibility is key to better understanding and user experience.

The advice focuses on practical tips for integrating accessibility into every step of content creation, from writing to visual elements, and helping to make digital services usable for everyone.
