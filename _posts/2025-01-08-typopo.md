# Typopo fixes common typographical errors in manuscripts
> Typopo automatically handles incorrect quotes, apostrophes, dashes, ellipses, and adds non-breaking spaces in the correct places in texts. It supports Czech, Slovak, English, and other languages.

![Example of the Typopo tool interface, with the original text on the left and the corrected text on the right.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/2f94f5a8-bfb8-477d-9997-221d99e62a69_1174x424.png "1174x424")

[Typopo](https://typopo.org) handles basic typographical corrections for text

When preparing print, you have an add-on in InDesign that solves most typographical shortcomings using regular expressions, but what about on the web or when preparing a newsletter? Exactly ten years ago, [Braňo Šandala](https://brano.me/) was struggling with this. From his struggle emerged a simple tool [Typopo](https://typopo.org/), which for you will insert correct quotes and apostrophes in the text, fix dashes and spaces, replace three dots with an ellipsis, add non-breaking spaces where they belong, and a few other common manuscript ailments. And what's great about it, it handles Czech, Slovak, English, German, and even Rusyn.
