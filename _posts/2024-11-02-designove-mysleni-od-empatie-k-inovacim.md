# Design Thinking from Empathy to Innovation
> 

Tabish Khan led a special design thinking workshop aimed at introducing third-year students to the basic [principles of creating intuitive and functional products](https://medium.com/@khantabish2448/from-empathy-to-impact-essentials-of-design-thinking-665d9aadee4d).

“Empathy is the foundation of user-centered design.”\
**— [Tabish Khan](https://medium.com/@khantabish2448/from-empathy-to-impact-essentials-of-design-thinking-665d9aadee4d)**

Students went through the five key phases of design thinking—empathy, definition, ideation, prototyping, and testing—with an emphasis on empathy as the foundation of user-centered design. The workshop inspired them to think about the future of design and how the products they create can truly enrich both users and society.
