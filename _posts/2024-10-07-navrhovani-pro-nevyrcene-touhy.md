# Designing for Unspoken Desires
> 

The article [Designing for Unconscious Desires](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/designing-for-unconscious-desires-a-journey-into-user-centered-ux-7c36a2dc9f9d) focuses on uncovering users' unspoken desires in UX design. Instead of directly asking about needs, the author AB recommends deep exploration of user behavior and habits to understand what truly motivates them.

> "It's like the iceberg theory in design — most of what matters is hidden beneath the surface."

The chaos of daily life influences user decisions, so it's important to consider not only functionality but also the emotional aspect of the experience. The article also emphasizes the importance of the user journey and connecting design with storytelling, which improves communication with stakeholders.
