# OgilvyApp.com will help you write better texts faster
> OgilvyApp.com is a smart AI editor. It helps you write like David Ogilvy – without taking control of your text.

![Screenshot of the OgilvyApp.com editor](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/a64bf21e-03c5-4614-a44a-08ba6201948a_1919x1079.png "570x320")

Do you want to learn to write like David Ogilvy?

[OgilvyApp.com](https://ogilvyapp.com/) is a tool that will help you write better texts – quickly, simply, and with the help of artificial intelligence. It was created based on the principles of [David Ogilvy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Ogilvy_(businessman), a legend of advertising writing.

Just insert your text and highlight the part you want to edit. You can shorten it, add humor, or change the tone. The artificial intelligence will offer various options. You just choose the one that suits you best.

The editor also includes a text analysis tool. It will immediately show you what sounds weak, what could be improved, or where the message's strength is getting lost.

However, OgilvyApp.com does not want to write for you. It believes that the best text is created through rewriting. You have control – the tool just guides you and saves time. If you don't like jumping between different applications, this is the solution. You have everything in one place. You write, edit, and finalize directly in the editor.

Great text doesn't happen on the first try. Great text is rewritten. And that is exactly what OgilvyApp.com will help you with.
