# Virtual relationships in the AI era: Salvation or threat?
> 

AI girlfriends offer an escape from loneliness, but can also bring new risks. Virtual beings that simulate romantic and emotional relationships are rapidly evolving thanks to technologies like [Replika](https://replika.com/) or [DreamGF](https://dreamgf.ai/), which allow for creating customized girlfriends.

These AI girlfriends, based on large language models, can respond to users with unprecedented depth. While some promise an authentic experience, others are focused on sexual interactions. The phenomenon of AI partners raises questions about long-term psychological impacts and ethics.

The topic is covered by Darek Šmíd in the article [AI girlfriends are here. The perfect storm of virtual relationships, AI assistants, internet pornography and OnlyFans](https://www.wired.cz/clanky/ai-pritelkyne-jsou-tady-dokonala-boure-virtualnich-vztahu-ai-asistentu-internetove-pornografie-a-only-fans), which he wrote for Wired.
