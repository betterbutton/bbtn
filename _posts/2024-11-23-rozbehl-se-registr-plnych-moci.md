# The Register of Powers of Attorney Has Been Launched
> 

[Register of Representation](https://www.dia.gov.cz/zakladni-registry/registr-zastupovani/) (REZA), managed by the state, now contains 15 million implicit authorizations, such as parent-child or company-manager relationships, which do not need to be confirmed by a power of attorney, [writes Lupa](https://www.lupa.cz/aktuality/registr-zastupovani-nacetl-15-milionu-opravneni-z-jinych-rejstriku/). In addition, it offers templates for explicit authorizations, issued by the will of the represented person.

Existing paper-based powers of attorney will remain unchanged; digitization will not affect them. The administration and deployment of the templates are handled by authorities [with the support of the Digital and Information Agency](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7264993675963686912/) (DIA). Citizens can choose for themselves whether to use the paper or digital solution.
