The Identita project, dedicated to Czech graphic design, approaches its history and present through a series, an exhibition, a [film](https://www.bontonfilm.cz/identitafilm), and a comprehensive book. On October 10, the [film premiere](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qv2zpPWNNqA&t=2s) took place and [on ČSFD it received 82%](https://www.csfd.cz/film/1566614-identita-film-o-ceskem-grafickem-designu/prehled/). The film follows New Yorker Lowry on his path through Czech design traces. The authors want to show the public that Czech graphic design is world-class.

> “Graphic design has power that we often fail to realize. It intervenes in all spheres of life.”\
> — **[Linda Kudrnovská](https://www.wired.cz/clanky/od-bati-po-logo-ostrava-milniky-ceskeho-grafickeho-designu-odhaluje-rozsahly-projekt-identita)**
