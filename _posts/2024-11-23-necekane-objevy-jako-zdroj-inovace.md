# Unexpected discoveries as a source of innovation
> 

Serendipity, meaning the ability to utilize unexpected discoveries, is key to innovations in UX research. [Random user behavior, incidentally spoken comments, or re-examination of old data can reveal hidden needs](https://medium.com/@khannasunny86/the-science-of-serendipity-how-accidental-findings-drive-ux-innovation-16cec8b60fd5) and inspire breakthrough solutions.

Examples like the creation of Post-it blocks or the transformation of Instagram show how "failures" or details outside planned research lead to significant improvements. To support serendipity, experts recommend openness, exploration beyond rigid frameworks, and documentation of every observation. Serendipity is not a substitute for structured research, but its essential complement, which brings creativity and flexibility.
