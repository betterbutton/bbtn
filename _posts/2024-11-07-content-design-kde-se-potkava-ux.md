# Content design: Where UX and Content Meet
> 

[Integrating content into the UX process](https://carolinaanamarcal.medium.com/como-integrar-o-conte%C3%BAdo-no-processo-de-ux-ce652345db67) means content designers don't just write copy—they can truly influence the entire user experience. Through design thinking and the double diamond, user needs can be more effectively considered while also demonstrating the value of well-designed content.

The phases of empathy, definition, ideation, prototyping, and testing are crucial. In these phases, the designer not only develops content based on data but also actively tests and optimizes its form based on user feedback, ensuring the content meets their real needs.
