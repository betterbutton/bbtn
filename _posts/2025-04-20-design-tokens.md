# Design tokens
> Design tokens store fundamental values such as colors, font sizes, or spacing.

[Design tokens](https://m3.material.io/foundations/design-tokens/overview) store fundamental values such as colors, font sizes, or spacing. They help maintain a consistent look across different platforms. They enable developers and designers to communicate clearly and simply.

Unlike regular variables in code, they are not tied to a specific technology. They can be easily used on the web, in mobile apps, and elsewhere. Tools serve to automatically convert a single file of values into various formats. This allows, for example, easy switching between light and dark modes or adapting the design system for a new brand.

Instead of working with a specific color, we use a generic label, such as *primary color*. This allows us to change the look of the entire system very quickly and without errors.

For the token system to function well, clear rules need to be set. Having an overview of naming. Version tracking and alignment between design and code are key.

Initially, this may mean more work, but in the long run, the effort pays off. Everything is clearer, faster, and less prone to errors. Design tokens are not just data. They are decisions that help turn a visual idea into a finished and consistent product.
