# What do people on the internet actually want?
> 

Successful will be brands that [offer more than just content – create experiences](https://scanclub.substack.com/p/what-digital-audiences-want-in-2024). Digital audiences are looking for new ways of connection, authenticity, and an escape from stress. Important trends are holistic brand worlds (metaverse, sonic branding), nostalgia and comfort, closed communities, and personal recommendations.

[Consumers additionally require value for their money](https://scanclub.substack.com/p/what-digital-audiences-want-in-2024-674), transparency, and ethics. Future marketing will focus on connecting emotions, communities, and real stories, with technologies like AI and AR/VR playing a key role. This approach transforms ordinary promotion into an engaging world of experiences that enchants and attracts consumers.
