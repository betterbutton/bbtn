# Web accessibility clearly as a metro map
> 

![WCAG criteria as a metro map](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/d11d4700-84f1-4276-bef5-d1e722f3a6d3_3000x2100.png "1456x1019")

[WCAG 2.2 as a metro map](https://www.andrewhick.com/accessibility/wcag-map/), which makes it easier to navigate web content accessibility topics, is the work of accessibility specialist Andrew Hick from the British government agency GDS. The visualization displays 55 criteria divided into seven thematic routes, such as Keyboard, Zoom and Readability, or Gestures. The criteria are organized by A and AA levels and are interconnected according to their practical relationships, which helps to better understand their application.
