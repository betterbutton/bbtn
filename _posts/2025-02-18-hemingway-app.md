# Hemingway will teach you to write clearly and concisely
> Hemingway is a free online text editor that highlights complex sentences, stylistic errors, and redundant words to make your English-written content more readable and understandable.

![Screenshot of the Hemingway app with errors highlighted.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/74902102-20d8-46ee-ac92-21d063290b67_2388x1472.png "1456x898")

[Hemingway App](https://hemingwayapp.com/) will show you how to write better.

[Hemingway App](https://hemingwayapp.com/) improves the readability and understandability of English text. It highlights complex sentences, common grammatical errors, and redundant words, so you can easily edit your content. Just paste your text into the editor and you'll immediately see suggestions for edits. If you want to write without distraction, use the Write button, which hides the highlighting and allows you to focus on creating content.

Hemingway's tricks involve analyzing sentence complexity, choosing the right words, and improving style. Yellow highlighting indicates long or difficult-to-read sentences – split them or simplify. Red highlighting warns of overly complex sentences that need to be significantly rewritten. Purple color indicates words that can be replaced with simpler alternatives for better readability. Blue highlighting points out adverbs, passive voice, and weak phrases. The app recommends removing them or replacing with stronger expressions.

In the paid version, Hemingway Editor Plus works similarly to [Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com/) or [DeepL Write](https://www.deepl.com/en/write). Artificial intelligence will automatically fix the highlighted issues. It also includes advanced grammar checking that will uncover less obvious errors. You can also adjust the tone and style of your text using AI tools.

We have always wished for a similar app to be created for Czech as well. In the United States, they have the [Plain Writing Act](https://www.plainlanguage.gov/law/), a law that since 2010 requires federal agencies to write in plain language that is understandable by eighth-grade graduates (in some cases, sixth-grade). They are helped by formulas like the [Gunning fog index](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gunning_fog_index) and the [Flesch-Kincaid readability test](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flesch%E2%80%93Kincaid_readability_tests). Short sentences composed of short words are considered more readable.

With Hemingway App, your text will be clear, engaging, and easy to understand – ideal for the digital environment where effective communication is important.
