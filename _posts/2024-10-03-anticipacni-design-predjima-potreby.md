# Anticipatory Design Anticipates User Needs
> 

Anticipatory design brings a revolutionary approach to user experiences by predicting user needs before they even become aware of them – [writes AB on Medium](<<<URL_0_b7c7b>>>). Key elements include contextual sensitivity, empathy, and decision simplification. This design approach improves user experience, reduces friction, increases efficiency, and builds loyalty.

> “When users feel understood, they form a connection. And that connection transforms into long-term loyalty.”

Brands like Spotify or Amazon use anticipatory design to deliver personalized experiences to users. The *Discover Weekly* playlist isn't just a gimmick; it's an experience that keeps users engaged with Spotify. The future belongs to those who can anticipate needs and deliver proactive solutions.
