# US Department of Justice forces Google to sell Chrome
> 

The US Department of Justice, according to information from TechCrunch, plans to [force Google to divest its Chrome browser](https://techcrunch.com/2024/11/18/justice-department-reportedly-pushing-google-to-spin-off-chrome/). This move is part of a historic antitrust case against Google's monopoly position, which the agency won this August.

Judge Amit Mehta ruled that Google violated antitrust laws in both search and advertising. Chrome controls approximately 61% of the US browser market, making it a key gateway to Google Search. Moreover, Google is expected to face further restrictions in the areas of artificial intelligence and the Android operating system.
