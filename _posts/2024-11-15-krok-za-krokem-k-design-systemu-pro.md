# Step by Step to a Multi-Brand Design System
> 

Pavel Kiselev focused on [creating a multi-brand design system](https://uxdesign.cc/flexible-styles-for-multi-brand-design-systems-638f9c25c227), which allows for easy adaptation of the design for various projects. The key is a main file with components and styles that can be copied and safely modified as needed for individual projects. This helps maintain consistency while also allowing for easy application of updates. The main challenge was propagating changes across multiple projects without manual edits, leading to the search for automated solutions for greater design flexibility.
