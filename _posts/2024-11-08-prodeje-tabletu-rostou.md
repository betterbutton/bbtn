# Tablet Sales Are Growing
> 

The tablet market is seeing an upswing: [in the third quarter of 2024, 37.4 million tablets were sold](https://www.chip.cz/prodeje-tabletu-se-zlepsuji-jednookym-kralem-mezi-slepymi-je-apple), which is a year-on-year increase of 10.9%. Apple remains the market leader with a 36.3% share and 13.5 million units sold, but its share fell from last year's 40.1%. Samsung holds second place with 18.4%, followed by Lenovo. Xiaomi and Huawei show the fastest growth; Xiaomi in particular recorded a 58.4% increase. Forecasts suggest that annual sales could reach up to 140 million units.
