## Linkodrome

**What is humanity in design?** [Alexandru Ticalo](https://www.alexandruticalo.com/) shared at UXcamp Europe 25 his four principles for [designing humanly in the AI era](https://www.figma.com/deck/TS3kS7L9XlzgGWDzgAkgxW/Human-Design-in-the-Age-of-AI-by-Alexandru-Ticalo?node-id=7-101&viewport=-158%2C-141%2C0.73&t=WYe01RZmn8DiI3Xh-1&scaling=min-zoom&content-scaling=fixed&page-id=0%3A1): humanity (e.g., the story of a handcrafted flute), accessibility (European Accessibility Act 2025), honesty (critique of dark UX practices), and usefulness (as in Shazam apps or London bike maps). Despite the rapid development of AI, the role of designers remains the same – to protect humanity in technology.

**How to stay a relevant designer,** even when [everything is on fire](https://www.canva.com/design/DAGpF6TLchg/I4OqTZB77RcBUgWx8U1-KA/edit), was the question posed in Berlin over the weekend by [Thiago Esser](https://www.thiagoesser.com.br/). He deals with topics like remote work, mental well-being, lifelong learning, and why leadership skills are not losing importance. It was no coincidence that his room was packed to the rafters.

> “Screwing up is part of the process!”\
> **— [Lucie Krulichová](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7335584501663744000/)**

**An introduction to conceptual diagrams** was given at the UXcamp by [Peter Boersma](https://about.me/pboersma). It might just be [rectangles with arrows](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/4gbrk2panb82fieynadiv/Concept_Diagrams_UXCE2025_share.pdf?rlkey=x74md354ql5dewnr7oolgukeb&e=1&st=5yypy7mz&dl=0), but their benefit is enormous, both for mapping the current state and for designing a new concept.

**With over a hundred interesting talks** in ten halls over two days, the UXcamp participants delivered. Among our favorites were also [Guerilla Org Design](https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVIrI2dnI=/) by [Sascha Brossmann](https://brsma.de/), Building on the [Other People Research](https://uxcampeuropecommunity.slack.com/files/UJRHV431C/F08VDMNAB2T/building_on_research-session17-uxcampeurope25.pdf) by [Nymphaey Notschaele](https://nnotschaele.nl/), and [RIP Lorem Ipsum](https://noti.st/gunnarbittersmann/LvPNza/lorem-ipsum-rest-in-peace) by [Gunnar Bittersmann](https://noti.st/gunnarbittersmann).
