# Create a flexible plan for UX research
> 

Creating a flexible roadmap for UX research is a prerequisite for effective priority management and handling changing organizational requirements. [Lawton Pybus presents the approach](https://depth.drillbitlabs.com/p/ux-research-roadmaps) from creating a backlog of ideas through estimating costs and resources to logical scheduling of research over time.

He emphasizes the importance of prioritization using the Impact-Effort Matrix and regular updates to the roadmap to keep it relevant. The goal is to create a living document that supports strategic planning and makes the UX team a trusted partner. The right roadmap eliminates frustration from constant changes and contributes to achieving key goals.
