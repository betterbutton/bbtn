# Myths and Facts about Google Discover
> 

Google Discover is a personalized content feed in the Google app and Chrome, whose influence on website traffic is constantly growing. It is something similar to the feed on Seznam's homepage, with the difference that page creators do not need to enter into a contract with Google and do not have to display its advertisements.

For media outlets, it is a key source today, even though its performance is highly variable. Discover recommends content based on user data, preferences, and interactions, with the crucial combination being high-quality, current content and well-targeted topics. Unlike traditional search, it involves passive content consumption.

Although Google provides recommendations on what works on Discover, its performance is often unpredictable and relies on algorithms similar to social networks. [Tereza Schambergerová will tell you everything else in her overview.](https://www.seo-terka.cz/google-discover/)
