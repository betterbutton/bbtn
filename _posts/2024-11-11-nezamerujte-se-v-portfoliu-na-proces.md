# Don't Focus on Process in Your Portfolio, But on Results
> 

Focusing on [the design process in a portfolio can be one of the worst approaches for designers](https://dataanddesign.substack.com/p/focusing-on-your-design-process-is). Companies and recruiters aren't looking for details about the design methodology, but for the specific benefit and value the designer can deliver.

One of the most effective approaches is a single-page project summary built on a simple Why–What–How model, which clearly demonstrates the business impacts of the design. For job applicants, this means emphasizing the project's outcome and impact on the company instead of a long explanation of process steps.
