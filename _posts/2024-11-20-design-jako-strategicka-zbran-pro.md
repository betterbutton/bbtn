# Design as a strategic weapon for business success
> 

Design is no longer just an aesthetic element, but a strategic pillar of businesses, which [improves products, services, and user experience](https://medium.com/@efeso.granieri_57437/o-impacto-do-design-nos-neg%C3%B3cios-83dcd2e30faa). Companies like Nike, Apple, or Natura show that focus on design brings competitive advantage and customer loyalty. The design thinking methodology enables innovation and problem-solving through empathy, prototyping, and testing.

Properly utilized design increases brand value, differentiates it in the market, improves user experience, and supports sustainability. Key is also the measurability of results, which large companies like IBM or Airbnb use to optimize processes and demonstrate the return on investment in design.
