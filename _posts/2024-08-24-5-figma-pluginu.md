# 5 Figma Plugins That Save Time and Effort
> 

Using top-tier plugins in Figma significantly improves the design process by automating tasks and providing essential tools right at your fingertips. Zakir Hussain selected [five of his favorite Figma plugins](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7229590798965604353/) and shared them with us:

- [Styler](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/820660579767995949/styler) – more efficient style and property management

- [Wireframe](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/742764242781786818/wireframe) – pre-built block library for rough drafts

- [Redlines](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/781354942292031141/redlines) – annotation that makes you an even bigger pro in front of developers

- [Heroicons](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/876119978690687541/heroicons) – over 350 open-source SVG icons from Tailwind for every occasion

- [Ghost](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1017135840453013129/ghost) – turns your final designs into a set of placeholders for empty states

And which Figma plugins boost your productivity?
