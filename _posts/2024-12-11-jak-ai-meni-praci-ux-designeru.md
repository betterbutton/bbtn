# How AI is changing the work of UX designers
> 

The rise of AI is changing the face of UX design: from automating wireframes to personalizing designs. AI tools, like Figma AI, enable faster prototyping and optimization through data analysis. Although AI offers efficiency, it carries risks such as replacing routine tasks, loss of creativity, or ethical problems with bias.

[The future of designers lies in their ability to use AI for creative work](https://medium.com/@muhammaddhimas000/ui-ux-designers-in-the-ai-era-collaboration-or-competition-198a610197d1), strategic leadership and maintaining the human dimension in technology. UI/UX designers will be key bridges between technology and users if they embrace AI as a tool for collaboration and adaptation.
