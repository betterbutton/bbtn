# Good news from America for UX researchers
> 

Data shows that [UX research is the second fastest growing job](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/new-data-on-the-product-job-market) in the entire tech industry, not just in design. The field is gaining importance thanks to its ability to analyze various data signals for better product outcomes.

> “Looks like the UX job drought is ending.”\
> **— [Matthew MacLaurin](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7222318359541956608/)**

The importance of UX researchers has long been undervalued, which is now changing. AI is bringing a wave of new work in UI, as UI becomes a key part of marketing. While AI quickly becomes a commodity, experience and design remain the main factors of differentiation in the market.
