# AI Brings Revolution to UX
> 

ChatGPT brings simplification of work and higher quality results to the world of UX design. It helps generate ideas, provides feedback, designs innovative prototypes, solves accessibility issues, and optimizes workflows. Subroto Ram Bayda on Linkedin wrote down his [ten ways to use AI for design](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/10-ways-chatgpt-can-revolutionize-your-uiux-design-subroto-ram-bayda-eafuc/).

Designers can use AI for brainstorming, information synthesis, or even generating code for prototypes, which saves time and supports creativity. Nevertheless, it is important to pay attention to ethical aspects and critically evaluate AI outputs, [warns Yuliia Fedyk](https://quarte.design/blog/using-chat-gpt-in-ux-design). Thus, ChatGPT allows designers to work faster and gain deeper insights into user needs.
