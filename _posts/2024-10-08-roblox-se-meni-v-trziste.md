[Roblox is transforming into a giant virtual marketplace](https://www.shopify.com/news/shopify-roblox), which connects its gaming metaverse with real-world business. [Wired writes](https://www.wired.cz/clanky/herni-platforma-roblox-se-stava-obrim-trzistem-mozna-az-pro-300-milionu-lidi-denne) that thanks to a collaboration with Shopify, developers and brands can sell physical products directly within games.

> "You can order a product in the game without having to leave it, and a delivery service will bring it right to your real front door."\
> — **Ernest Cline**, [Ready Player One](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/9969571-ready-player-one)

This move confirms the growing Roblox economy, which aims to reach up to 300 million daily users. The platform already enables purchases in the virtual world, where users can receive real products without needing to leave the game. In 2023, the Roblox creator economy generated $741 million.
