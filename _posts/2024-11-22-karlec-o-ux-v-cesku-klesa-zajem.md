# Karlec: Interest in UX in the Czech Republic is declining
> 

Jakub Karlec looked at the [Czech job market for UX specialists](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/zanev%C5%99ely-%C4%8Desk%C3%A9-firmy-na-ux-m%C3%A1me-data-jakub-karlec-jg95c/). According to data from StartupJobs, the job market in the UX field is going through a crisis. The number of job offers for design positions has been declining since mid-2022. The main reasons are economic uncertainty, layoffs in large tech companies, and a drop in investments in digitalization.

> „If you don't have a job, it will be harder to find one. If you have one, it will be harder to change it.“
> **— Jakub Karlec**

The situation is also complicated by the oversupply of junior designers who joined companies during the pandemic. This devalued the quality and trust in UX as a key discipline. Nevertheless, there are signs of recovery, as shown by the unexpected growth in demand in the last quarter of 2024. For designers, it is now important to focus on the business value of their work and be prepared for more challenging market conditions.
