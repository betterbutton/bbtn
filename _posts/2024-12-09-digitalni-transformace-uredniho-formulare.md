# Digital transformation of the official form
> 

GOV.UK Forms evolved from paper documents and PDF files [to a modern platform for creating online forms](https://gds.blog.gov.uk/2024/12/03/gov-uk-forms-through-the-ages/). Its origins date back to the GOV.UK Submit project (2017), which inspired the form-building community. In 2021, a new phase began focused on making HTML form creation accessible to officials without digital knowledge.

Since the private beta in 2022, the system has delivered significant time and resource savings thanks to its ease of use and adaptability. Since November 2024, it has been in public beta mode, aiming to expand accessibility and add new features such as question branching or complex responses.
