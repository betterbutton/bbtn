# Master respondent recruitment even over Christmas
> 

Recruiting participants for user research during the Christmas season is not easy, but with good planning it is manageable. Avoid key holidays, especially between December 22nd and New Year's.

If you must conduct research close to the holidays, it is recommended to extend recruitment by ten percent to account for dropouts. After the holidays, it is ideal to wait until the second week of January. To reduce participant dropouts, consider higher compensation, flexible times, and remote sessions. With a well-thought-out plan and attractive incentives, [you can successfully manage recruitment even during the hectic period](https://people4research.medium.com/our-top-tips-to-recruit-participants-over-the-holidays-31ac645d9493).
