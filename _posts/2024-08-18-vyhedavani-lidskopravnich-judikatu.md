# Searching Human Rights Judgments
> 

The government agent for representing the Czech Republic before international human rights bodies, Petr Konůpka, on his office's website [mezisoudy.cz](https://mezisoudy.cz/) has made available a [search engine for browsing decisions](https://mezisoudy.cz/databaze-judikatury) of the European Court of Human Rights, the Constitutional Court of the Czech Republic, the UN Human Rights Committees, and the European Committee of Social Rights. The search engine searches in both Czech and English, and the autocomplete feature of the search field facilitates query input for successful search results.

Justice was one of the first ministries to have its registries online (for example, the business register perhaps since the 1990s). We are glad to see that the digitization of public administration has not stopped here. We look forward to when the websites of Mezi soudy and Justice move under the [gov.cz](http://gov.cz) domain and it will be possible to search all judgments of various types of courts in one place.
