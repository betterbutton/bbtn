# Design Thinking changes the world
> 

Design thinking is an approach to innovation that proves key in modern companies for solving complex problems. The method is based on human-centered design and includes five phases: empathy, definition, ideation, prototyping, and testing. It focuses on user needs, supports creativity and collaboration among diverse teams, thereby enabling the creation of original products and processes. Thanks to its iterative approach, design thinking streamlines problem-solving, minimizes risks, and enhances competitiveness. Companies thus gain a tool that connects technological advances with a human approach.
