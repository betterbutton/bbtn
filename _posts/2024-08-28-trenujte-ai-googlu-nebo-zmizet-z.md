# Train Google's AI, or disappear from search?

> 

Google according to the website Pivot to AI [fundamentally changes the rules of the game for publishers](https://pivot-to-ai.com/2024/08/19/google-is-no-longer-asking-feed-the-ai-or-youre-not-in-search-results/). According to the sourced article, if content providers refuse to make their data available for AI training, they may disappear from search results. Although Google claims that blocking Googlebot does not lead to removal from results, contradictory information remains unclear.

Publishers reportedly face a difficult decision: cooperate and gradually lose relevance, or face an immediate drop in traffic. Critics see this as an abuse of monopoly position, with Google additionally planning to include ads in AI summaries. This move raises concerns about the accuracy of information and the future of the internet ecosystem.
