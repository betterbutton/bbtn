# Diary studies as a valuable tool for user research
> 

Stéphanie Walter writes that [diary studies are an effective research method](https://condens.io/blog/user-diary-study/), which allows data collection on user behavior and experiences over a longer period. This method is particularly suitable for discovering habits or situations that cannot be directly observed.

„The main advantage of diary studies is the ability to collect data over time. This allows understanding of how user behavior changes and evolves.“\
**— [Stéphanie Walter](https://condens.io/blog/user-diary-study/)**

Studies can provide both qualitative and quantitative data and are suitable for researching behavior that changes over time. Advantages include natural data collection, but the method requires high participant engagement. Diary studies can be used in the initial phase of research or when validating already designed solutions.
