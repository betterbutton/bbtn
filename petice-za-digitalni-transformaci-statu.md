# Petition: Experts call on Prime Minister Fiala to ensure the continuation of the state's digital transformation with a focus on users
> 

Experts in software development and service design have drafted a petition in which they [call on politicians to create and adhere to a long-term strategy for the digitization of public administration](https://gov.cz/e-petice/882-vyzva-za-strategicky-pristup-k-digitalizaci-kvalita-flexibilita-a-udrzitelnost-ve-statnich-projektech). They appeal for ensuring the continuity, efficiency, and flexibility of digitization projects, which is crucial for the quality of life of citizens and the competitiveness of the Czech Republic. Key requirements include strategic planning, a focus on user needs, and keeping know-how in state hands.

> "Digitalization will only be successful if it also takes into account the needs of real users."

The petition is backed by former director of Česko.Digital Eva Pavlíková together with Lukáš Marvan (member of the board of the UX Association), Jakub Nešetřil (founder of Česko.Digital), Jakub Onderka (security analyst), Jiří Sekera (UX designer), and Ladislava Zbiecjzuk Suchá (service designer and academic). At the time of publication of this one-liner, 66 people had signed the petition.
