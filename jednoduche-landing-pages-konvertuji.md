# Simple landing pages convert better
> 

[Simple landing pages have a better chance of converting](https://medium.com/@devbajra/why-simple-landing-pages-convert-better-38d07c8e1b83), because they eliminate unnecessary visual elements and reduce cognitive load. Users decide quickly, so pages must be clear and immediately understandable.

Studies show that simple websites with fast loading times and clear calls-to-action perform better than aesthetically complex ones. The key is a balance between design and functionality, using micro-animations and social proof, such as reviews or counts of satisfied customers, which increases user trust.
