# 49 American startups became unicorns. Which ones do you know?
> 

Despite the challenging environment for venture capital, 49 new unicorns, i.e., startups valued at over a billion dollars, have already emerged in the United States in 2024. Among them is, for example, [xAI](https://x.ai/) by Elon Musk, valued at 24 billion dollars, and a number of other companies focused on artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, health tech, and fintech.

[The list, which was revealed by TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/24/38-startups-have-become-unicorns-so-far-in-2024-heres-the-full-list/), also includes newer players such as [Rentberry](https://rentberry.com/), [Safe Superintelligence](https://ssi.inc/), and [Twelve](https://www.twelve.co/). This overview of startups shows which sectors are leading investors, and it will be continuously updated throughout the year.
