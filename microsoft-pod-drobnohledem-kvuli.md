# Microsoft under scrutiny due to dominance in cloud and AI
> 

Microsoft has come [under stricter control of the German antitrust office](https://www.bundeskartellamt.de/SharedDocs/Meldung/EN/Pressemitteilungen/2024/30_09_2024_Microsoft_19a.html), which now has the ability to intervene in its activities if it considers its behavior to be threatening competition. This decision, valid for five years, allows the antitrust office to monitor in particular the use of generative artificial intelligence, such as Copilot.

> „Microsoft's products are ubiquitous in companies, offices, and private households and have become indispensable.“\
> — **Andreas Mundt**, president of [Bundeskartellamt](https://www.bundeskartellamt.de/SharedDocs/Meldung/EN/Pressemitteilungen/2024/30_09_2024_Microsoft_19a.html)

The antitrust office also highlighted Microsoft's connection with OpenAI, which increases its market power. No specific measures have been taken yet, but the antitrust office now has extended powers to regulate Microsoft's activities, including areas that do not fall under the European DMA law.

[Microsoft will also face a complaint from Google in the EU](https://www.theregister.com/2024/09/25/google_ms_ec_complaint/) regarding its dominance in the market.
