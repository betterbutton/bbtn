# Bluesky is Ten Million
> 

The social network Bluesky announced that it now has over ten million users. We joined Bluesky half a year ago when the network had five million users. It took the network two and a half years to get those first five million profiles. It gained another five million in half a year.

Bluesky is a decentralized microblogging platform created by Bluesky Social as a concept for the AT Protocol – the goal is to enable interaction between different social networks through an open standard. Critics often point out that the protocol is not compatible with the fediverse, hence with the ActivityPub standard, which leads to fragmentation of the online community.

Bluesky was long available only by invitation and saw a sharp growth in users, especially after opening registrations to the public in February 2024 and after issues with Twitter in Brazil in August of this year. One of the main initiators of Bluesky is former Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey. The network is a common refuge for Twitter users who are leaving the platform after its takeover by Elon Musk.
