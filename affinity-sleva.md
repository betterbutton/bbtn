# Affinity gives 50% summer discount
> 

If you work in the creative industry and are considering leaving Adobe or Figma, maybe now is the right time [give Affinity a chance](https://affinity.serif.com/en-gb/). Their tools [Designer 2](https://affinity.serif.com/en-gb/designer/) (vectors), [Photo 2](https://affinity.serif.com/en-gb/photo/) (bitmaps) and [Publisher 2](https://affinity.serif.com/en-gb/publisher/) (layouts) you can now get for half price, and one-time. Either any of the desktop apps for 899 CZK (299 for iPad), or all for 2150 CZK. And if you're not sure, the discount is valid until August 15 and the trial period is valid for half a year.
