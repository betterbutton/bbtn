# Improved User Experience with the Law of Proximity

> The law of proximity ([law of proximity](https://lawsofux.com/law-of-proximity/)), one of the principles of Gestalt psychology, plays a key role in creating visual hierarchy and improving user experience. Similar elements are grouped close together in design to facilitate orientation and minimize confusion.

> "When related elements are organized together, users can easily find the necessary information without confusion."\
> — **Geetha**

Proper use of whitespace between these elements will ensure clarity, but it's important to maintain sufficient distance between unrelated elements and ensure the design remains consistent with other principles such as alignment, color, or size. Specific tips with examples can be found in the article [Using the Law of Proximity to Enhance User Experience](https://medium.com/design-bootcamp/using-the-law-of-proximity-to-enhance-user-experience-4d3b041082e9).

Geetha writes a series of articles on [other UX laws and how to apply them in design](https://medium.com/@geethap0212).
