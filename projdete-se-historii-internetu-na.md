# Explore the history of the internet at Web Design Museum
> 

[Web Design Museum](https://www.webdesignmuseum.org/all-websites) is a fascinating archive focused on web design past from the 90s to 2010. You can immerse yourself in memories for hours and hours, but why not, when it's Friday.

> Web Design Museum is a perfect retro time capsule full of forgotten web pages – from Google from the 90s to Y2K Club Penguin.“\
> — **[Natalie Fear](https://www.creativebloq.com/web-design/i-just-discovered-web-design-museum-and-got-lost-for-two-hours)**

This digital time machine offers nostalgic views on long-forgotten pages, from Google in the 90s to old versions of Facebook and YouTube. Pages can be sorted by years, categories, and graphic styles. For lovers of retro webs, it's an irresistible experience that reminds of a time when design was more playful and less controlled by commercial rules.

> „If anything has shown the past, it is that in recent years we have lost playfulness with web design.“\
> — **[Natalie Fear](https://www.creativebloq.com/web-design/i-just-discovered-web-design-museum-and-got-lost-for-two-hours)**
