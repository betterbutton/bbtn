# Tumblr Will Run on WordPress
> 

[Tumblr](https://tumblr.com) is a social platform on the border between Twitter and blogs. It is known for its diverse and creative user base. Automattic, after acquiring Tumblr, announced a plan to migrate the backend to WordPress.

Automattic emphasizes that [it is not about changing Tumblr's identity, but a technical change](https://automattic.com/2024/08/27/shipping-tumblr-and-wordpress/). The move, which will be one of the largest migrations on the internet, is said to bring benefits not only to Tumblr but also to WordPress.

Tumblr was founded in 2007. It has about half a billion users and [Martin runs a blog on it](https://blog.garcon.cz/). ;)
