# Apple Design Awards has 14 awarded apps and games in seven categories

> 
Apple announced the winners of the [Apple Design Awards 2024](https://developer.apple.com/design/awards/). Awards for outstanding design of apps and games are given in seven categories. In each category, Apple awards one app and one game:

- Joy and Fun: [Bears Gratitude](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/bears-gratitude/id6443609622), [NYT Games](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/nyt-games-word-games-sudoku/id307569751)
- Inclusivity: [oko](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/oko-accessible-navigation/id1583614988), [Crayola Adventures](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/crayola-adventures/id6444109249)
- Innovation: [Procreate Dreams](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/procreate-dreams/id1595520602), [Lost in Play](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/lost-in-play/id6449434958)
- Interaction: [Crouton](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/crouton-recipe-manager/id1461650987), [Rytmos](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/rytmos/id1673697954)
- Social Impact: [Gentler Streak Fitness Tracker](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/gentler-streak-workout-tracker/id1576857102), [The Wreck](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/the-wreck/id6450220805)
- Visuals and Graphics: [Rooms](https://apps.apple.com/fr/app/rooms/id6443548715?l=en-GB), [Lies of P](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/lies-of-p/id6459738231)
- Spatial computing: [djay](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/djay-dj-app-ai-mixer/id450527929), [Blackbox](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/blackbox-for-vision/id6458588937)

The awards are presented as part of the annual [Worldwide Developers Conference](https://developer.apple.com/wwdc24/). The awards have been given since 1997 and their goal is to highlight the creative use of Apple products. Winners receive a trophy in the shape of an aluminum cube that lights up with the Apple logo when touched.
