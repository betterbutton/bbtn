# Crystal Magnifying Glass 2024 is Collecting Nominations
> 

The [Crystal Magnifying Glass 2024](https://kristalova.lupa.cz/2024/) – Czech Internet Award has launched its nineteenth edition. **Until August 16, you can nominate** favorite apps, online services, videos, or personalities in eight categories. The expert jury nominates in the remaining nine categories.

Newly added categories are Influencer and Star. Nominations are taking place online. Final voting will be from September 25 to November 1, results will be announced on November 28 at Divadlo pod Palmovkou. The award is organized by Lupa.cz.
