# Why is ChatGPT bad at math? 
> 

ChatGPT and other AI models struggle with solving mathematical tasks because their foundation is statistical analysis and text processing, not precise mathematical logic.

Tokenization, the process of breaking text into pieces, often [distorts relationships between numbers](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/02/why-is-chatgpt-so-bad-at-math/). Moreover, AI relies on patterns, which can lead to incorrect results. Although new models, such as OpenAI's o1, show significant improvement in solving complex calculations, experts like Yuntian Deng suggest that some mathematical problems may be solved over time, but tools like calculators remain indispensable for now.

Perhaps LLMs would need something like a math coprocessor.
