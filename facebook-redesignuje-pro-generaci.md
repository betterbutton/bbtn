# Facebook redesigns for Generation Z
> 

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/7cb24b86-b8e6-49f1-902e-93a76bc9a4b9_312x679.jpeg "312x679")

Local photos section on Facebook

> „The changes are meant to make Facebook more than just a platform for friends, with an emphasis on local content and personalized recommendations.“ — **[TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/04/facebook-launches-a-gen-z-focused-redesign/)**

Facebook is focusing with its new redesign on [attracting a younger generation](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/04/facebook-launches-a-gen-z-focused-redesign/) of users, especially those who spend time on platforms like TikTok and Instagram. New features, such as the local tab 'Local' and improvements to groups or Marketplace, are meant to support interaction within local communities.

The changes also include a greater focus on videos, especially the Reels format, so that the platform can better compete with visual apps. Meta thus hopes that these adjustments will particularly appeal to twenty-somethings who still use some practical features, such as thrifting.
